A Profinite Presentation of the Absolute Galois Group of ℚ2

David Roe and David Corneliu Turturean

An explicit presentation of the absolute Galois group of the field of 2-adic numbers: four generators, two relations — the dyadic counterpart to the classical presentations at odd primes — developed alongside two independent Lean 4 formalizations.
