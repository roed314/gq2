    Assembly of the §9 block enrichment's non-κ⁰ fields for the concrete frame RF = blockFrame T Blk hE2: q/qbar (from prop_7_4 + mForm_of_qbar), the coupling hqbar, the radical/vanishing clauses hrad/hTzero, invariance hinv, quadraticity/ nonsingularity hquad/hns (P-17d2c packaging), and the frame-local cover square hq.
