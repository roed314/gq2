    The Hermitian-line count (paper Prop 6.18, unramified computation) #

    The final computation of the paper's Prop 6.18 (unramified case): on D = 𝔽_{2^{2m}} the Hermitian trace form x ↦ Tr(c·x^{2^m+1}) (c outside the fixed field D₀ = 𝔽_{2^m}, so the D₀-level form Tr_{D₀/𝔽₂}(a·N(x)) in absolute-trace clothing) has exactly 1 + (2^m+1)(2^{m−1}−1) = 2^{2m−1} − 2^{m−1} zeros — the minus-type count. Everything is finite-field counting: norm fibres are ker-cosets of size 2^m+1 (cyclic gcd count), and the nonzero trace-kernel of the fixed field contributes 2^{m−1}−1.

    This file is part of the GQ2.DeepPart split (the deep-part proof); see GQ2/DeepPart.lean for the overview.
