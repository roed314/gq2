    • The carrier splice: evensAux/bS/evensNormFun are Quotient.out-free (membership tests and s-translates only), so the ↥U₀- and ↥k.fixingSubgroup-side Evens cochains agree pointwise under the underlying-identity ι₀; the B²-witness of the field-side vanishing pulls back along ι₀ (the DeepDualityK.kerToFixing pattern), and H2ofFun ↥U₀ … = 0 follows.
