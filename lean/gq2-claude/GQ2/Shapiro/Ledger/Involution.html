    The involution-orbit Shapiro ledger #

    The compatible transversal, position identity, and final involution coboundary chain.

    See GQ2.Shapiro.Ledger for the paper-facing overview, source citations, and deviations.

    Lemma 6.15, involution orbits (105) — foundations #

    The involution case compares graphPullback(invOrbitDatum_{N,ḡ}) with cor_{U₀→G} of the two-point Evens cocycle evensNormFun_{N≤U₀} (paper (107)–(109)), where U₀ = ⟨N, ĝ⟩ is the index-2-over-N subgroup (fixed field K₀ = K^{⟨ḡ⟩}) and ḡ = mk ĝ is an involution of G/N. These are the setup lemmas: ḡ is an involution, G/U₀ is finite, and the two index sets G/U₀ and (G/N)/⟨ḡ⟩ correspond (U₀ maps onto ⟨ḡ⟩ under G ↠ G/N).
