    The candidate boundary package boundaryPackage_GammaA (Stage C, Prop 3.14) #

    This file assembles the candidate-side Stage-C data of Γ_A into a single BoundaryPackage GammaA, realising the manuscript's Proposition 3.14 (reference/q2_manuscript.tex, prop:compatiblemarking, l.1085–1110) and plan Turn 4 §6: the common tame quotient Γ_A ↠ T_A and the maximal pro-2 quotient Γ_A ↠ Π may be chosen with a common unramified character ν : Γ_A ↠ ℤ₂, compatible after passage to every finite quotient C_{2^m}.

    The three ingredients are already built:

    • tameMap := gammaTameMap (Boundary/CandidateTame.lean), surjective by gammaTameMap_surjective;
    • pro2Map := p (Boundary/CandidatePro2.lean), the canonical pro-2 quotient Γ_A ⟶ Π (its generator-image lemmas p_gammaGen_* make the compatibility immediate), surjective by p_surjective;
    • nu := gammaTameMap ≫ nu_t, so the tame compatibility tame_nu is rfl.
