    The profinite Nielsen comparison D₀ ≅ Π (Stage C, work order C4) #

    This file assembles the genuine profinite Nielsen isomorphism D₀ ≅ Π (manuscript reference/q2_manuscript.tex, Corollary 3.12 cor:relativeDemushkin, building on Proposition 3.11 prop:abstractDemushkin, l.~998–1035) from the finite-quotient category equivalence D0_Pi_index_equiv : D0MarkedFinite ≌ PiMarkedFinite (Boundary/Nielsen.lean) together with limit-invariance under an equivalence of the (cofiltered) index categories.

    Method #

    The Nielsen functor D0_to_Pi : D0MarkedFinite ⥤ PiMarkedFinite remarks the same underlying finite group: (D0_to_Pi.obj X).n = X.n definitionally (the obj-map copies n, groupFin, … and only permutes the marking), and the morphism map is the same underlying homomorphism Fin X.n →* Fin Y.n. Since both d0Diagram and piDiagram send their index object to ProfiniteGrp.ofFiniteGrp (FiniteGrp.of (Fin ·.n)) and a morphism to ProfiniteGrp.ofFiniteGrpHom (FiniteGrp.ofHom ·.toMonoidHom), the two inverse systems agree on the nose along the Nielsen functor: D0_to_Pi ⋙ piDiagram ≅ d0Diagram (the natural iso d0CompIso, with identity components).

    Limit-invariance under the index equivalence (HasLimit.isoOfEquivalence applied to D0_Pi_index_equiv) then gives the profinite isomorphism D₀ = limit d0Diagram ≅ limit piDiagram = Π.

        The Nielsen comparison preserves the unramified markings #

        The genuine profinite Nielsen isomorphism D₀ ≅ Π intertwines the two unramified characters ν_ur : D₀ ↠ ℤ₂ (Local/NuUr.lean, marks (a,s,y) ↦ (-2,1,0)) and ν₂ : Π ↠ ℤ₂ (Boundary/PiGroup.lean, marks (σ,x₀,x₁) ↦ (1,0,0)): ν_ur = D0_iso_PiGrp.hom ≫ ν₂. This is the marking half of manuscript Cor. 3.12 / Prop compatiblemarking (l.1085–1110): the Nielsen change of generators σ = s, x₁ = y, x₀ = s⁻²a⁻¹ (equivalently a = x₀⁻¹σ⁻²) carries the marks compatibly — ν₂(x₀⁻¹σ⁻²) = -2·1 = ν_ur(a), ν₂(σ) = 1 = ν_ur(s), ν₂(x₁) = 0 = ν_ur(y).

        The proof works through the inverse limits: HasLimit.isoOfEquivalence_inv_π identifies the D₀-coordinate of D0_iso_PiGrp.inv p at a Demushkin quotient j with the Π-coordinate of p at the Nielsen-relabelled quotient D0_to_Pi.obj j (the comparison natural iso d0CompIso has identity components). Evaluating the unramified layers at the auxiliary cyclic quotient cyclicD0 n then reduces the character identity to the three generator marks, computed by cyclicD0Equiv_*.
