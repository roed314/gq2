    The Q₂ presentation words, as data (Stage A) #

    The manuscript's marked generators σ, τ, x₀, x₁, the auxiliary words σ₂, u₀, u₁, d₀, z₀, c₀, g₀, d_g, h_c, h₀, and the two displayed relators (tameRelator, wildRelator) are recorded here as elements of the abstract syntax GExpr. They are deliberately boring data: every theorem about them is obtained by evaluating and applying GExpr.eval_map / GExpr.eval_pi, so there is no hand-simplification here (plan §2).

    Conventions (manuscript): right conjugation x^g = g⁻¹ x g (rconjExpr) and the commutator (x, y) = x⁻¹ y⁻¹ x y (commExpr).
