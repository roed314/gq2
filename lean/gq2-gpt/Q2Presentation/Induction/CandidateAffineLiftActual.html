    Coherent candidate affine-lift packet #

    This sidecar replaces the three current arbitrary-tower candidate Section 8 semantic residuals by one coherent chosen packet.

    The important dependency is that the middle obstruction evaluation and the top affine-fibre equivalence are chosen together. This avoids proving a top affine-fibre equivalence for an arbitrary obstruction function, which is stronger than the manuscript's Section 8 construction.
