    Candidate affine-lift component proofs #

    This sidecar gives the final Section 8 candidate route a narrower input than the three arbitrary elementary-tower component residuals used by CandidateAffineLiftActualProofs.lean.

    The packet consumed here is the canonical candidate concrete packet attached to a supplied canonical target quotient tower D : CentralCoverTargetCanonicalQuotientTowerData B. Thus the quotient-frame map, middle R-lift obstruction, and Frattini affine-fibre equivalence all belong to the same manuscript tower Y -> Y/R -> Y/K; there is no arbitrary bare T detached from the actual block.
