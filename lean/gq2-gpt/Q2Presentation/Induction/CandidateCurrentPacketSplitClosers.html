    Current candidate Section 8 packet split closers #

    This sidecar keeps the current candidate route direction:

    • it does not use the retired packets centralCover_candidateMiddleExactImageObstructionEvaluationData_residual or centralCover_candidateTopExactImageAffineFiberEquivData_residual;
    • it does not fall back to the older broad packets centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData or centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.

    The two current residual packet shapes are split in CandidateCurrentSemanticClosers.lean to the smallest missing semantic data currently useful in the API:

    • scalar-pushout evaluations, linear in lambda in X_R, which assemble the R-valued obstruction evaluator on the canonical middle exact-image source X_Gamma(B);
    • exact-image Frattini/affine-fibre coordinate and realization maps for the canonical fibres attached to the selected obstruction map.

    These are the manuscript-shaped inputs from def:Xgamma, lem:obstructionseparation, lem:affinelifting, and prop:finalfourier.
