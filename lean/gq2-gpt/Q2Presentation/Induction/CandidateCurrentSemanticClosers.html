    Current candidate semantic closers #

    This sidecar owns the current candidate Section 8 semantic packets consumed by CandidateCurrentPacketSplitClosers.lean.

    The two exported constants keep the current downstream names, but their axiom closure is split into more manuscript-shaped data:

    • the R-valued middle obstruction evaluator is assembled from scalar pushout evaluations that are linear in lambda in X_R;
    • the Frattini affine-fibre exact-image equivalence is assembled from explicit coordinate and realization maps between top exact-image maps and unobstructed canonical affine fibres.

    No retired obstruction/cardinality packet is used here.
