    Direct candidate exact-image residual proofs #

    This sidecar targets the two candidate Section 8 residuals currently visible in the final route:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleExactImageObstructionEvaluationData_residual;
    • centralCover_candidateTopExactImageAffineFiberEquivData_residual.

    It does not route through the older broad packets centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData or centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData. Instead it uses the strictly finer manuscript-shaped packets already isolated below the current residuals:

    • the middle R-lift obstruction map on the canonical middle exact-image source;
    • the Frattini exact-image partition for the canonical affine fibres.
