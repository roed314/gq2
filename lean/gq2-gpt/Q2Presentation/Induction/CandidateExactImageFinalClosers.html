    Final candidate exact-image closers #

    This sidecar targets the two broad candidate Section 8 packet axioms still visible in the current final closure:

    • centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.

    The obstruction-map packet is harmless once the actual middle exact-image obstruction evaluation is available: any supplied finite obstruction index maps to the middle exact-image source, so the obstruction is pulled back along that map.

    The Frattini cardinality packet is too strong for an arbitrary index. The manuscript statement is canonical: it uses the middle exact-image index X_Gamma(C) and the affine fibres attached to that same obstruction. We therefore close the canonical instance from the exact-image affine-fibre equivalence and route the candidate lift-partition theorem through that canonical instance.
