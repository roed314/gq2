    Candidate exact-image packet closers #

    This sidecar splits the live candidate exact-image aggregate packet from CandidateExactImageResiduals.lean into the smaller manuscript-facing pieces:

    • boundary-compatible postcomposition (lem:covertransform);
    • a finite obstruction index and base map (def:Xgamma);
    • the R-valued obstruction map (lem:obstructionseparation);
    • the Frattini exact-image cardinality statement (prop:finalfourier).

    The affine fibre torsor remains theorem-level through centralCover_candidateCanonicalAffineFiberTorsorData.
