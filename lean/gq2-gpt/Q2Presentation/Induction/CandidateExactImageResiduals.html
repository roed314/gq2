    Candidate exact-image residual refinements #

    This sidecar refines the three candidate exact-image packets isolated in CandidateLiftPartitionCardinality.lean.

    The current tower API exposes the finite quotient maps in the target tower, but not enough boundary-frame compatibility to construct the candidate exact-image maps outright. The packets below therefore keep the missing data concrete:

    • a surjective profinite postcomposition map from the top target to the middle quotient, together with the boundary-frame compatibility needed to send top boundary-framed maps to middle boundary-framed maps;
    • a finite obstruction index, its map to the middle exact-image source, and the R-valued obstruction map on that same index;
    • the finite cardinality statement whose choice-free content is the Frattini exact-image lift partition.
