    Candidate Frattini route50 proofs #

    This sidecar is candidate Section 8 only: it does not edit the routed IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs* files. It keeps the Route49 target, kernel, and pushout providers, but replaces the Route48 candidate provider

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleFrattiniResidualProofData

    by the two lower manuscript-shaped exact-image packets:

    • centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.

    The two packets are the Section 8 obstruction-separation map and the final Frattini/Fourier exact-image cardinality statement. The quotient-frame and canonical middle exact-image index are supplied theorem-level by the existing candidate sidecars.
