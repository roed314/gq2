    Candidate Frattini Route52 proofs #

    This sidecar is candidate Section 8 only. It does not edit the routed IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs* files.

    Route51 exposes the two broad candidate leaves

    • centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.

    Route52 avoids both names in the routed candidate provider. The lift partition is assembled instead from the current split packets:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarObstructionEvaluatorData, the scalar-pushout evaluator, linear in lambda : X_R;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniAffineFiberCoordinateData, the coordinate and realization maps for the canonical affine fibres, with the two inverse laws.

    These are the smaller manuscript-shaped inputs from def:Xgamma, lem:obstructionseparation, lem:affinelifting, and prop:finalfourier. The arbitrary I, O cardinality packet is deliberately not reconstructed: the manuscript uses the canonical middle exact-image index and the affine fibres attached to the selected obstruction map.
