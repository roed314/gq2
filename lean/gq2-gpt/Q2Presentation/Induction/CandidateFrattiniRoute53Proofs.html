    Candidate Frattini Route53 proofs #

    This sidecar is candidate Section 8 only at the provider surface, and it does not edit any integrated route files.

    Route52 exposed the two current candidate leaves

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarObstructionEvaluatorData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniAffineFiberCoordinateData.

    Route53 proves those two packet shapes from the lower scalar-pushout and Frattini coordinate constructors already isolated in the candidate sidecars:

    • pointwise scalar-pushout values and additivity in lambda : X_R, with ZMod 2 homogeneity proved theorem-level;
    • base coordinate, affine fibre coordinate, curried realization, and the two inverse laws for the final Frattini affine fibre.

    The exported provider has the same call shape as centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_frattiniRoute52, but its axiom closure no longer contains the two Route52 candidate leaves.
