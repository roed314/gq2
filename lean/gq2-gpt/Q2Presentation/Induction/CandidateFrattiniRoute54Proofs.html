    Candidate Frattini Route54 proofs #

    This sidecar is candidate Section 8 only at the provider surface. It does not edit any integrated route files.

    Route53 exposes seven independent candidate residuals:

    • pointwise scalar-pushout values and additivity;
    • base/fibre Frattini coordinates, fibre realization, and the two inverse laws.

    Those are stronger than the route needs, because the manuscript's final Frattini step uses one selected middle R-obstruction and the matching exact-image partition of its unobstructed affine fibres. Route54 therefore packages precisely that coherent exact-image datum and proves the same drop-in lift-partition provider shape consumed by Route53.
