    Candidate Frattini Route56 proofs #

    This sidecar is a candidate-only §8 refinement over Route54. The earlier Route54 coherent exact-image packet is useful, but it is built through the Route53/Route36 scalar-coordinate path, so its transitive axiom closure can still expose the seven coordinate leaves

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutValueAtData,
    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutAdditivityData,
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniBaseCoordinateData,
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniFiberCoordinateData,
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniFiberRealizationData,
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniRealizeLiftCoordinateData, and
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniLiftCoordinateRealizeData.

    The manuscript construction is more coherent at this point: choose the final middle R-obstruction and the Frattini exact-image equivalence attached to that same obstruction. Route56 packages exactly that pair and projects all route-needed scalar/Frattini data from it theorem-level. Thus the candidate closure is reduced from the seven coordinate leaves to the two actual §8 inputs already isolated in CandidateMiddleFrattiniActualProofs.
