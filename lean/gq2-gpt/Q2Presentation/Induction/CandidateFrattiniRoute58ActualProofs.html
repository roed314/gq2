    Candidate Frattini Route58 actual proofs #

    This sidecar is candidate Section 8 only. It does not edit the active Route56 integration file.

    Route56 currently exposes two actual candidate leaves:

    • centralCover_candidateActualMiddleRLiftObstructionMapData;
    • centralCover_candidateActualFrattiniAffineFiberExactImageEquivData.

    Route58 proves Route56-compatible replacements for both actual leaves from the coherent scalar/Frattini semantic packets:

    • scalar pushout values on the middle exact-image source, with linearity in lambda : B.XR;
    • Frattini coordinate, realization, and inverse-law data for the selected middle obstruction map.

    This deliberately avoids the older broad pair centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData and centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.
