    Canonical candidate obstruction decompositions #

    This sidecar works only over a supplied canonical target quotient tower D : CentralCoverTargetCanonicalQuotientTowerData B.

    The current routed file CandidateObstructionConcreteProofs.lean packages the candidate side of manuscript Section 8 into one canonical residual, centralCover_candidateCanonicalConcreteProofData. The theorems below avoid that aggregate axiom in two ways:

    • centralCover_candidateCanonicalConcreteProofData_from_componentPackets reassembles the exact canonical concrete structure from its three concrete component packets: quotient-frame map, middle R-lift obstruction, and Frattini exact-image equivalence.
    • centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_canonicalSemanticClosers gives the route-ready candidate lift-partition packet directly from the manuscript-shaped semantic packets: actual top-to-middle postcomposition, middle exact-image obstruction evaluation, and top exact-image affine-fibre equivalence.

    No theorem in this file calls centralCover_candidateCanonicalConcreteProofData.
