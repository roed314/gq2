    Canonical candidate obstruction semantic proofs #

    This sidecar narrows the three live candidate-side Section 8 semantic residuals to the canonical quotient tower used by the current final route.

    The existing residuals in CandidateObstructionSemanticClosers.lean are stated for an arbitrary T : CentralCoverElementaryQuotientTowerData B. The final route, however, builds such a tower from the canonical target tower D : CentralCoverTargetCanonicalQuotientTowerData B. The theorems below therefore give route-ready replacements over D.tower, and reassemble the semantic R-lift packet without calling

    • centralCover_candidateActualTopToMiddlePostcompData_residual,
    • centralCover_candidateMiddleExactImageObstructionEvaluationData_residual,
    • centralCover_candidateTopExactImageAffineFiberEquivData_residual.

    The remaining assumptions are the strictly narrower component packets already isolated in the sidecar stack: the actual quotient-frame commuting square, the middle R-lift obstruction map, and the Frattini exact-image equivalence for the selected canonical affine fibres.
