    Candidate R-lift obstruction and Frattini partition closers #

    This sidecar continues the Section 8 candidate exact-image de-axiomatization without proving a semantically empty aggregate for an arbitrary bare elementary tower.

    It keeps the already-refined quotient-frame input centralCover_candidateQuotientFrameMapData_residual, but replaces the two remaining candidate semantic residuals used by the routed final closure:

    • centralCover_candidateRLiftObstructionData_residual is replaced by the sharper middle exact-image obstruction-map packet centralCover_candidateMiddleRLiftObstructionMapData_residual;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoverData_residual is replaced by the canonical-affine-fibre partition packet centralCover_candidateFrattiniAffineFiberPartition_residual.

    The reassembly target is only the downstream semantic packet CentralCoverCandidateRLiftSemanticData, and then the coherent CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData needed by the integrated Section 8 route. We deliberately do not reconstruct CentralCoverCandidateConcreteExactImageData, because that stronger aggregate still asks for more arbitrary data than the final route needs.
