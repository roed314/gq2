    Candidate Route100 coherent closers #

    This sidecar is candidate Section 8 only. It does not edit IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs100.

    Route100 currently calls

    centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_route98

    whose candidate closure exposes the two coherent residuals

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutCoherentData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoordinateCoherentData.

    The theorems below lower those two residuals to the actual manuscript-shaped middle obstruction and Frattini exact-image packets from CandidateMiddleFrattiniActualProofs, then provide a Route100-shaped branch and count theorem with only the candidate provider swapped.
