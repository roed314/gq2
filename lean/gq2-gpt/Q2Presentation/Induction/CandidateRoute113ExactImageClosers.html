    Candidate Route113 exact-image closers #

    This sidecar targets the two candidate exact-image residuals visible in IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs113:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleExactImageObstructionEvaluationData_residual;
    • centralCover_candidateTopExactImageAffineFiberEquivData_residual.

    The older Route112 attack sidecar routes these residuals through the Route106 scalar/Frattini coordinate packet, whose audit frontier is the seven coordinate leaves from the scalar-pushout and Frattini-coordinate split. The present sidecar is stricter: it routes the same exact-image pair through the current semantic split in CandidateCurrentPacketSplitClosers.

    Thus the candidate closure is lowered to the two manuscript-shaped current Section 8 packets:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarObstructionEvaluatorData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniAffineFiberCoordinateData.

    No theorem below calls the two Route113 residual axioms, and no theorem touches any local/CFT axiom.
