    Candidate Route115 semantic closers #

    This sidecar targets the two candidate-side semantic residuals visible after Route114:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarObstructionEvaluatorData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniAffineFiberCoordinateData.

    It does not prove the manuscript's scalar/Fourier and Frattini-coordinate content outright. Instead it gives a strictly smaller theorem-level lowering, using the already-isolated scalar/Frattini semantic packets:

    • scalar pushout values on the canonical middle exact-image source;
    • linearity of those values in lambda : X_R;
    • a Frattini lift-coordinate map;
    • a Frattini realization map;
    • the two inverse laws tying those maps together.

    The route-ready provider below has the same downstream target as the Route113 candidate exact-image provider, but its axiom closure should name the five smaller packets above rather than the two Route114 semantic residual axioms. No theorem below touches any local/CFT axiom.
