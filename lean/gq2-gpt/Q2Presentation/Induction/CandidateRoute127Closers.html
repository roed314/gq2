    Candidate Route127 closers #

    Route127 publicly exposes the two Route42 candidate Section 8 packets:

    • centralCover_candidateRoute42ScalarPushoutSelectedFamilyData;
    • centralCover_candidateRoute42FrattiniCoordinateSystemData.

    This sidecar proves replacements for both packet types from the lower exact-image obstruction and Frattini partition APIs already isolated by the Route44 path. The drop-in provider at the bottom has the same downstream shape as centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_scalarFrattiniRoute42:

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    It does not call either public Route42 residual axiom.
