    Candidate Route130 closers #

    Route130 currently exposes the two broad candidate exact-image packets

    • centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.

    This sidecar attacks those leaves without promoting any integrated route.

    The obstruction-map packet is derivable in its broad form: pull the actual middle exact-image obstruction evaluation back along the supplied finite index-to-base map.

    The Frattini cardinality packet is not manuscript-shaped in its fully broad form, because it quantifies over arbitrary I and O. The Section 8 statement is the canonical instance attached to the selected middle obstruction and its canonical affine fibres. The drop-in provider below routes through that coherent final residual and has the same downstream shape as centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_candidateRoute127Closers:

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)
