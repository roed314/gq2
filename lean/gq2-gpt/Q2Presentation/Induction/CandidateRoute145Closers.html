    Candidate Route145 closers #

    Route144 currently obtains candidate Section 8 lift-partition data from CandidateRoute130Closers, whose axiom closure still contains the two semantic exact-image leaves

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleExactImageObstructionEvaluationData_residual;
    • centralCover_candidateTopExactImageAffineFiberEquivData_residual.

    This sidecar leaves Route144 untouched and exposes a same-shape replacement provider for a future Route145 call site:

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    The implementation routes through the Route106 coordinate lowering. Its remaining candidate assumptions are the smaller manuscript-shaped scalar and Frattini coordinate packets: nonzero scalar values, nonzero-distinct scalar additivity, Frattini base/fibre coordinates, curried realization, and the two inverse laws.
