    Candidate Route146 pointwise coordinate closers #

    Route145 lowers the candidate Section 8 frontier to seven scalar/Frattini coordinate atoms. This sidecar keeps the Route145 downstream shape, but replaces those whole-function and global-law residuals by pointwise atoms:

    • nonzero scalar-pushout values are supplied one middle exact-image point at a time;
    • nonzero-distinct additivity is supplied one (x, lambda, mu) test at a time;
    • Frattini base/fibre coordinates and fibre realization are supplied pointwise;
    • both inverse laws are supplied pointwise.

    The old Route145 theorem is untouched. The final provider below has the same B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B) shape, so it can be used as a drop-in candidate replacement in an integrated Route146 file.
