    Candidate Route147 pointwise closers #

    Route147's integrated candidate side currently consumes CandidateRoute146Closers, whose remaining Section 8 assumptions are seven pointwise scalar/Frattini atoms. This sidecar does not edit that integrated route. It exposes a same-call-shape replacement provider whose proof bypasses those seven pointwise atoms:

    • scalar values and selected additivity are induced by the lower R-valued obstruction map on the middle exact-image source;
    • base/fibre coordinates, curried realization, and inverse laws are induced by the lower Frattini affine-fibre exact-image partition.

    The resulting closure is strictly lower than the Route147 pointwise frontier: the final provider depends only on the two semantic Section 8 leaves centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData and centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData, plus Lean standard axioms.
