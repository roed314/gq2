    Candidate Route152 exact-image closers #

    Route152 still exposes the two broad candidate exact-image leaves through the Route147 pointwise candidate provider:

    • centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.

    This sidecar keeps the integrated route untouched and supplies a stricter Section 8 replacement. The source-side residual is the coherent manuscript packet centralCover_candidateMiddleFrattiniResidualProofData: a middle R-valued obstruction on the exact-image index X_Gamma(C), together with the matching Frattini exact-image affine-fibre equivalence. From that packet we recover the Route147 pointwise objects and the route-ready lift partition without invoking either broad exact-image leaf.
