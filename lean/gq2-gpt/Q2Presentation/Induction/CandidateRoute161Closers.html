    Candidate Route161 closers #

    Route160's candidate side currently exposes two actual Section 8 leaves:

    • centralCover_candidateActualMiddleRLiftObstructionMapData;
    • centralCover_candidateActualFrattiniAffineFiberExactImageEquivData.

    This sidecar lowers both to the scalar/Frattini semantic packets matching lem:obstructionseparation and prop:finalfourier in the manuscript:

    • scalar pushout values for the middle R-obstruction;
    • linearity of those values in lambda : X_R;
    • Frattini lift coordinates;
    • Frattini realizations;
    • inverse laws for the coordinate/realization pair.

    It does not call the old broad packets centralCover_candidateMiddleFrattiniResidualProofData, centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData, or centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.
