    Candidate Route164 closers #

    Route163's candidate side still exposes the five scalar/Frattini semantic packets from CandidateScalarFrattiniSemanticClosers:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutValueData;
    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutLinearityData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniLiftCoordinateData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniRealizationData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoordinateInverseData.

    This sidecar is a drop-in candidate replacement for centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_candidateRoute161Closers. It routes through the stricter Route85 data:

    • pointwise scalar-pushout values, plus additivity and homogeneity for the selected scalar family;
    • base and fibre Frattini coordinates, curried realization, and the two inverse laws.

    The arbitrary scalar-linearity and arbitrary coordinate-inverse Route163 leaves are therefore lowered to coherent selected packets, while the bridge theorem keeps the same B T Q call shape as Route161.
