    Candidate Route167 closers #

    Route166's candidate side still exposes the eight Route85/Route164 scalar-Frattini coordinate leaves:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutValueAtData;
    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutAdditivityData;
    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutHomogeneityData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniBaseCoordinateData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniFiberCoordinateData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniFiberRealizationData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniRealizeLiftCoordinateData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniLiftCoordinateRealizeData.

    This sidecar keeps the Route164 downstream call shape while routing through the strictly lower Route146 pointwise scalar/Frattini packets. The scalar additivity and homogeneity statements are provided for the selected Route167 scalar family, as in the selected-packet Route164 bridge; they are not claimed for an arbitrary externally supplied family.
