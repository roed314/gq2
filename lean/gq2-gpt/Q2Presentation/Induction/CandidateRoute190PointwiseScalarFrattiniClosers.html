    Candidate Route190 pointwise scalar/Frattini lowering #

    This sidecar is candidate Section 8 only. It is intended as a drop-in replacement for the Route189 candidate provider centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_candidateRoute188ScalarFrattiniLowerClosers.

    Route189 exposes five candidate scalar/Frattini atoms:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutValueData;
    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutLinearityData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniLiftCoordinateData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniRealizationData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoordinateInverseData.

    Route190 reroutes the candidate lift partition through the existing Route146 pointwise coordinate layer. Thus those five Route189 atoms are replaced by the more local manuscript-shaped pointwise residuals used by Route146:

    • pointwise nonzero scalar-pushout values;
    • pointwise nonzero-distinct scalar additivity;
    • pointwise Frattini base coordinates;
    • pointwise Frattini fibre coordinates;
    • pointwise Frattini fibre realization;
    • pointwise realization-after-coordinate;
    • pointwise coordinate-after-realization.

    No new axioms or theorem-level assumptions are introduced in this file; the residual frontier is inherited from CandidateRoute146Closers.
