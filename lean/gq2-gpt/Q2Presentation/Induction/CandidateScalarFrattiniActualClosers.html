    Candidate scalar and Frattini actual closers #

    This sidecar refines the five current candidate Section 8 leaves exposed by CandidateScalarFrattiniSemanticClosers.lean.

    The scalar obstruction is split into the manuscript's scalar-pushout tests, one packet for each lambda : X_R, together with the two linearity laws used to assemble the R-valued obstruction. The Frattini equivalence is split into a base exact-image coordinate, an affine R-lift coordinate over that base, a curried realization map, and the two inverse laws.
