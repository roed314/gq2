    Candidate scalar/Frattini coherent proof providers #

    This sidecar proves theorem-level providers for the two coherent route10 candidate residuals introduced in CandidateScalarFrattiniCurrentProofs.lean.

    The routed file is not edited here. The route-ready theorem at the bottom has the same target shape as centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_scalarFrattiniCurrentProofs, but its proof uses the providers in this file, so its axiom closure does not name the two route10 coherent residual axioms:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutCoherentData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoordinateCoherentData.
