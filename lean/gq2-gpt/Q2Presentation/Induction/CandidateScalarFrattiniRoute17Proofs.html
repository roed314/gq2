    Candidate scalar/Frattini route17 proofs #

    Route17 is a candidate-side sidecar for the route16 call site. Route16 exposed five scalar/Frattini leaves from CandidateScalarFrattiniSemanticClosers:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutValueData;
    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutLinearityData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniLiftCoordinateData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniRealizationData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoordinateInverseData.

    The arbitrary linearity and arbitrary inverse-law packet shapes are stronger than the route needs: the manuscript construction supplies one coherent scalar obstruction evaluator and one matching Frattini affine-fibre equivalence. The route-ready theorem below therefore factors through the direct exact-image split from CandidateExactImageDirectResidualProofs, whose residuals are the middle R-lift obstruction map and the matching Frattini partition for its canonical affine fibres.

    This file does not edit routed integration files. A supervisor can replace the route16 call

    centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_scalarFrattiniRoute16 B T Qframe

    by

    centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_scalarFrattiniRoute17 B T Qframe

    to remove the five route16 candidate scalar/Frattini names from the integrated candidate-side closure.
