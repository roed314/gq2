    Candidate scalar/Frattini route18 proofs #

    Route18 is the direct exact-image sidecar for the two candidate Route17 leaves:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleRLiftObstructionMapData_residual;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniAffineFiberPartition_residual.

    The sidecar keeps Route17's supervisor-facing provider shape B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B), but routes the two leaves through the narrower manuscript-shaped exact-image packets already isolated in CandidateExactImagePacketClosers:

    • the R-valued obstruction map on the canonical middle exact-image index;
    • the Frattini exact-image cardinality/partition packet attached to that same obstruction datum.
