    Candidate scalar/Frattini route19 proofs #

    Route19 is the exact-image obstruction/cardinality sidecar for the two broad packets left visible after Route18:

    • centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.

    The obstruction-map packet is theorem-level in the supported broad form: an actual middle exact-image obstruction evaluation can be pulled back along any finite index-to-base map.

    The Frattini cardinality packet is not available in its over-general I, O-arbitrary form. The manuscript-shaped statement is canonical: choose the middle exact-image index, the corresponding R-valued obstruction, and the matching affine-fibre equivalence. Route19 exposes that canonical cardinality and routes the supervisor-facing B T Q lift-partition provider through the same coherent residual.
