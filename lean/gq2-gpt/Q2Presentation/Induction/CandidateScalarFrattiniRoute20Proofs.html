    Candidate scalar/Frattini route20 proofs #

    Route20 keeps the supervisor-facing hook shape from Route19,

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B),

    but does not route through the two arbitrary final exact-image residual axioms used by Route19:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleExactImageObstructionEvaluationData_residual;
    • centralCover_candidateTopExactImageAffineFiberEquivData_residual.

    Instead it exposes the manuscript-shaped packets actually needed by the lift partition:

    • the middle R-lift obstruction map on the canonical middle exact-image index;
    • the Frattini exact-image partition of the canonical affine fibres for that selected obstruction map.

    The second packet is weaker than the Route19 top affine-fibre equivalence residual: it supplies the partition consumed by CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData, without asserting a separately quantified top equivalence for an arbitrary middle evaluation.
