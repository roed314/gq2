    Candidate scalar/Frattini route21 proofs #

    Route21 keeps the route20 supervisor-facing hook shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B),

    but it no longer uses the two route20 wrapper residuals:

    • centralCover_candidateScalarFrattiniRoute20MiddleData_residual;
    • centralCover_candidateScalarFrattiniRoute20TopPartitionData_residual.

    Instead it exposes the two manuscript-shaped pieces directly:

    • the actual R-valued obstruction evaluation on the canonical middle exact-image index X_Gamma(C);
    • an actual Frattini exact-image equivalence for the canonical affine fibres attached to that selected obstruction evaluation.

    The second datum is stronger than the route20 partition field but narrower than the older arbitrary top-affine-fibre residual: it is only required for the chosen canonical middle obstruction.
