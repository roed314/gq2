    Candidate scalar/Frattini route22 proofs #

    Route22 keeps the route21 packet shapes but closes the two route21 residual leaves by repackaging narrower Section 8 scalar/Frattini APIs:

    • scalar-pushout values, additivity, and homogeneity for the middle obstruction evaluator;
    • Frattini base/fibre coordinates, realization, and the two inverse laws for the top exact-image affine fibre.

    No new residual axiom is introduced here. The middle route21 structure is just the canonical-index obstruction evaluation form of the proven middle R-obstruction map, and the route21 top structure is the equivalence form of the Frattini coordinate/realization bijection for the same selected middle obstruction.
