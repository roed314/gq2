    Candidate scalar/Frattini route23 proofs #

    Route23 targets the actual scalar/Frattini leaves left visible by route22.

    The arbitrary-packet statements in CandidateScalarFrattiniActualClosers are stronger than the data needed by the route: additivity/homogeneity are only needed for the scalar family generated by the chosen evaluator, and the Frattini fibre/inverse laws are only needed for the coordinates generated by a chosen affine-fibre equivalence. This file therefore proves the concrete route-needed versions and reassembles the route22 lift-partition shape without using the eight actual-leaf axioms.
