    Candidate scalar/Frattini route27 proofs #

    Route27 keeps the route26-compatible provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but avoids the five route26 candidate residual axiom names:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutValueData;
    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutLinearityData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniLiftCoordinateData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniRealizationData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoordinateInverseData.

    The replacement data are the stricter Section 8 packets already isolated in CandidateScalarFrattiniActualClosers: pointwise scalar-pushout values with additivity and homogeneity for the selected family, and base/fibre Frattini coordinates with realization and the two inverse laws for that same coordinate system. This file introduces no new axioms.
