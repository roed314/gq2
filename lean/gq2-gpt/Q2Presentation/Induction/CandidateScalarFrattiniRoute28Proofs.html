    Candidate scalar/Frattini route28 proofs #

    Route28 keeps the route27 downstream provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but replaces the eight route27 candidate scalar/Frattini residuals by two canonical exact-image packets fixed to the manuscript's Section 8 construction:

    • the R-valued obstruction map on the canonical middle exact-image index X_Gamma(B);
    • the Frattini exact-image cardinality statement for the same obstruction and the canonical regular affine R-lift fibres.

    The route28 assumptions are narrower than the route27 coordinate packets: they are not quantified over arbitrary scalar-pushout families or arbitrary coordinate/realization systems. All adapters from these two packets to the route27-compatible lift-partition provider are theorem-level.
