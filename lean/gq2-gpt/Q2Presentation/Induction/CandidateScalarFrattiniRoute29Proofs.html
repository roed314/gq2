    Candidate scalar/Frattini route29 proofs #

    Route29 keeps the route28 downstream provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but does not use the two route28 candidate residual names. It strictly refines them as follows.

    • The canonical middle R-obstruction map is assembled from scalar-pushout values on the middle exact-image source and their linearity in lambda : B.XR.
    • The route28 Frattini cardinality packet is reduced to the Fourier zero-obstruction count #X_top = z_R * #{x : X_mid // o x = 0}. The sigma-cardinality of the canonical affine lift fibres is then theorem-level from the existing torsor-count lemma and the canonical affine-fibre torsor.
