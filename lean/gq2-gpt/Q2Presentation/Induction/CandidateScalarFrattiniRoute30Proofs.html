    Candidate scalar/Frattini route30 proofs #

    Route30 keeps the route29 downstream provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    and removes the two route28 candidate residual names from the provider closure. It also narrows the route29 scalar-linearity packet: the only scalar linearity content left as a residual is additivity in lambda : B.XR; homogeneity over ZMod 2 is proved below by the two scalar cases.

    The final Frattini cardinality input remains the route29 Fourier zero-locus packet, because that is the manuscript's prop:finalfourier statement tying the top exact-image count to the unobstructed middle locus. The affine fibre sigma-cardinality factor is theorem-level from the existing torsor mechanism.
