    Candidate scalar/Frattini route31 proofs #

    Route31 keeps the route30 integration-facing provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but does not route through the route29/route30 candidate residual names:

    • centralCover_candidateRoute29CanonicalScalarPushoutValueData;
    • centralCover_candidateRoute29FrattiniFourierCountData;
    • centralCover_candidateRoute30CanonicalScalarPushoutAdditivityData.

    Instead, it uses the coherent current packets from CandidateScalarFrattiniCurrentProofs.lean: one chosen scalar-pushout evaluator with its linearity, and one chosen Frattini coordinate/realization equivalence. This is the route-ready theorem shape a coordinator can swap into an integrated route without editing the current route32 file.
