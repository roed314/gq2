    Candidate scalar/Frattini route34 proofs #

    Route34 keeps the route31-compatible provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but avoids the two route33 candidate residual names:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutCoherentData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoordinateCoherentData.

    The replacement route is lower in the exact-image stack:

    • the scalar coherent evaluator is recovered by evaluating the canonical middle R-obstruction map against lambda : X_R;
    • the Frattini coordinate/realization equivalence is recovered from the canonical affine-fibre exact-image partition.

    Thus the visible frontier moves from route33's coherent scalar/coordinate packets to the stricter manuscript-shaped exact-image packets: centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData and centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.
