    Candidate scalar/Frattini route35 proofs #

    Route35 keeps the route34 provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but routes the candidate side below route34's two exact-image packet residuals:

    • centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.

    Instead, it uses the lower scalar/Frattini semantic split:

    • scalar-pushout values and linearity in lambda : X_R;
    • Frattini lift-coordinate, realization, and inverse-law packets.

    This is the manuscript-shaped split corresponding to scalar separation of the middle R-obstruction and the affine-fibre Frattini partition in Section 8.
