    Candidate scalar/Frattini route36 proofs #

    Route36 keeps the route35 provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but lowers the five route35 candidate residuals.

    On the scalar side, the coherent scalar evaluator is assembled from pointwise scalar-pushout values and additivity in lambda; homogeneity over ZMod 2 is proved theorem-level. On the Frattini side, the coordinate/realization equivalence is assembled from the lower base coordinate, fibre coordinate, curried realization, and the two inverse-law leaves.
