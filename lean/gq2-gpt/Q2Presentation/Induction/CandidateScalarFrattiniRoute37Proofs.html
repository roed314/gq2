    Candidate scalar/Frattini route37 proofs #

    Route37 keeps the route36 provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but no longer routes through the seven route36 candidate residual names.

    • On the scalar side, the pointwise scalar-pushout values and additivity are supplied as one coherent selected family. The ZMod 2 homogeneity law is still theorem-level, inherited from route36's additivity argument.
    • On the Frattini side, the five base/fibre coordinate, realization, and inverse-law leaves are replaced by the exact affine-fibre partition from the central-cover transform. Coordinates and inverse laws can be recovered from that bijection, but the route only needs the partition itself.

    This matches the manuscript direction in Section 8: scalar pushouts separate the middle R-obstruction, while the final Frattini stage is an exact-image partition of unobstructed affine fibres.
