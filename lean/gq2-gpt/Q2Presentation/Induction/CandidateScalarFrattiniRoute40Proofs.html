    Candidate scalar/Frattini route40 proofs #

    Route40 keeps the Route39 drop-in provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but does not use either Route39 candidate residual axiom.

    • Scalar side: the Route39 selected scalar-pushout family is reassembled from the existing scalar-pushout value packet and its linearity in lambda : B.XR. This is the manuscript lem:obstructionseparation scalar-pushout evaluator in Section 8.
    • Frattini side: the final Fourier zero-locus count is recovered from the explicit affine-fibre exact-image partition supplied by coordinate, realization, and inverse-law packets. This is the partition underlying prop:finalfourier in Section 8, before taking its cardinality shadow.

    No new axiom is introduced in this file. The exported provider is a strict Route40 lowering of the Route39 candidate frontier: its axiom closure should name the smaller scalar/Frattini semantic packets, not centralCover_candidateRoute39ScalarPushoutSelectedFamilyData or centralCover_candidateRoute39FrattiniFourierZeroLocusData.
