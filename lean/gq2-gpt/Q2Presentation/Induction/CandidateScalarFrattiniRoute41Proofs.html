    Candidate scalar/Frattini route41 proofs #

    Route41 keeps the Route40 drop-in provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but routes the candidate side through the finer actual-closer decomposition of the Section 8 scalar/Frattini packets. In particular, the exported provider does not use the five Route40 candidate residual axioms

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutValueData;
    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutLinearityData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniLiftCoordinateData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniRealizationData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoordinateInverseData.

    They are replaced by the pointwise scalar-pushout value/additivity/homogeneity packets and the base/fibre Frattini coordinate, realization, and inverse-law packets from CandidateScalarFrattiniActualClosers. These are the manuscript-shaped pieces of Section 8, pages 42--49: lem:obstructionseparation, lem:affinelifting, and prop:finalfourier.

    No new axiom is introduced in this file.
