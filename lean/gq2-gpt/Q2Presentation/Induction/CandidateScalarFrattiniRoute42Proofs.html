    Candidate scalar/Frattini route42 proofs #

    Route42 keeps the Route41 drop-in provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but narrows the candidate Section 8 residuals used by the provider.

    Scalar side:

    • Route41 used pointwise scalar-pushout values, additivity, and homogeneity.
    • Route42 asks only for the selected scalar-pushout family used by the middle R-obstruction, together with additivity for that same family.
    • Homogeneity over ZMod 2 is recovered theorem-level from the Route36 additivity argument.

    Frattini side:

    • Route41 exposed base coordinate, fibre coordinate, fibre realization, and two inverse-law leaves as independent arbitrary packets.
    • Route42 asks for one coherent coordinate system: the selected base/fibre coordinates, selected curried realization, and the two inverse laws tying exactly those maps together.

    The residual axioms in this file are still semantic inputs from manuscript Section 8 (lem:obstructionseparation, lem:affinelifting, and prop:finalfourier), but they are the route-needed coherent data rather than the stronger arbitrary-family/arbitrary-coordinate Route41 packets.
