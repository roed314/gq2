    Candidate scalar/Frattini route43 proofs #

    Route43 keeps the Route42 drop-in provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but proves the two Route42 candidate packet shapes from the finer actual-closer obligations already present in the candidate Section 8 API.

    Scalar side:

    • Route42's selected scalar-pushout family is assembled from the pointwise scalar-pushout family and additivity packet of CandidateScalarFrattiniActualClosers.
    • Homogeneity remains theorem-level over ZMod 2, via the Route36 additivity argument.

    Frattini side:

    • Route42's coherent coordinate-system packet is assembled from the lower base-coordinate, fibre-coordinate, curried realization, and two inverse-law actual-closer obligations.

    No new axiom is introduced in this file. The exported Route43 provider has the same public target shape as Route42, but its axiom closure does not contain centralCover_candidateRoute42ScalarPushoutSelectedFamilyData or centralCover_candidateRoute42FrattiniCoordinateSystemData.
