    Candidate scalar/Frattini route44 proofs #

    Route44 is a candidate Section 8 cleanup sidecar. It keeps the Route43 drop-in provider shape

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    but avoids the seven Route43 candidate leaves in the route-ready proof path.

    Scalar side:

    • scalar-pushout values are not taken as independent pointwise data;
    • they are obtained by evaluating the selected R-valued obstruction map against lambda : X_R;
    • additivity is theorem-level from the linear-map structure of the obstruction value.

    Frattini side:

    • the arbitrary base/fibre coordinate leaves are not used;
    • coordinates, realization, and the two inverse laws are the two directions of the exact affine-fibre partition.

    The remaining candidate residuals are the lower exact-image packets already isolated in CandidateExactImagePacketClosers:

    • centralCover_candidateObstructionMapData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniExactImageCardinalityData.

    These are concrete Section 8 residuals: the obstruction map on the canonical middle exact-image index, and the finite Frattini exact-image cardinality statement that recovers the partition.
