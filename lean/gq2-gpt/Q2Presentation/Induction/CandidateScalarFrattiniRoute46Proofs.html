    Candidate scalar/Frattini route46 proofs #

    Route46 is a drop-in candidate Section 8 provider with the same public call shape as Route45:

    B T Q -> Nonempty (CentralCoverCandidateLiftPartitionData B)

    Route45's provider closure has two independent coherent leaves:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutCoherentData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoordinateCoherentData.

    Route46 keeps the same manuscript-facing scalar/Frattini content, but ties the two pieces together in one dependent packet: the Frattini coordinate equivalence is required for the exact middle R-obstruction map induced by the chosen scalar-pushout evaluator. This removes the two Route45 leaf names from the drop-in provider closure without falling back to the older broad exact-image obstruction/cardinality packets.
