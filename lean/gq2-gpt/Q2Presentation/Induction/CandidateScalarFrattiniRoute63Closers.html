    Candidate scalar/Frattini Route63 closers #

    Route63 is a candidate Section 8 sidecar. It keeps the drop-in call shape of centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_frattiniRoute58 and centralCover_candidateLiftPartitionData_from_frattiniRoute60, but routes the candidate contribution through the lower exact-image R-lift/Frattini semantic proofs.

    The five Route62-visible scalar/coordinate atoms are treated as follows.

    • scalar-pushout values are closed by evaluating the selected R-valued obstruction map against lambda : X_R;
    • scalar-pushout linearity is provided for that same selected value family;
    • Frattini lift coordinates and realizations are the two directions of the selected exact-image partition equivalence;
    • Frattini inverse laws are provided for that same selected coordinate and realization pair.

    Thus the arbitrary-linearity and arbitrary-inverse-law leaves are strictly lowered to coherent selected-data packets, and the route-ready provider has no dependency on the five Route62 scalar/coordinate axioms.
