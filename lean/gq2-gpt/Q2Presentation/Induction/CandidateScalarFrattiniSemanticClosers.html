    Candidate scalar and Frattini semantic closers #

    This sidecar pushes the current candidate Section 8 residuals one step lower without routing through retired broad packets.

    The current visible residuals are:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarObstructionEvaluatorData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniAffineFiberCoordinateData.

    Here they are replaced by smaller manuscript-shaped leaves:

    • scalar pushout values indexed by lambda in X_R, plus a separate linearity packet in lambda;
    • a Frattini lift-coordinate map, a realization map, and the two inverse laws identifying top exact-image maps with unobstructed affine fibres.

    The theorem wrappers at the bottom return the current route's packet shapes, so a coordinator can integrate this sidecar without changing downstream target types.
