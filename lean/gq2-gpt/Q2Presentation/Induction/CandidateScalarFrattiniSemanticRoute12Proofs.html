    Candidate scalar/Frattini semantic route12 proofs #

    This sidecar refines the five route12 candidate scalar/Frattini semantic residuals without editing routed files:

    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutValueData;
    • centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutLinearityData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniLiftCoordinateData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniRealizationData;
    • centralCover_candidateFrattiniCoordinateInverseData.

    The two arbitrary-family semantic packets are stronger than the manuscript route needs. The route only needs one coherent scalar-pushout family and one coherent Frattini coordinate/realization pair. We therefore route through the strictly finer pointwise scalar-pushout and base/fibre Frattini packets isolated in CandidateScalarFrattiniActualClosers.lean, then export the same lift-partition target shape consumed by the integrated route.

    Refined leaves still visible in the exported theorem's axiom closure:

    • scalar point values, additivity, and homogeneity: centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutValueAtData, centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutAdditivityData, centralCover_candidateMiddleScalarPushoutHomogeneityData;
    • Frattini base/fibre coordinate, fibre realization, and the two inverse laws: centralCover_candidateFrattiniBaseCoordinateData, centralCover_candidateFrattiniFiberCoordinateData, centralCover_candidateFrattiniFiberRealizationData, centralCover_candidateFrattiniRealizeLiftCoordinateData, centralCover_candidateFrattiniLiftCoordinateRealizeData.
