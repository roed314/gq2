    Candidate/target Route133 Section 8 closers #

    This sidecar leaves the promoted route files untouched. It gives Route133-context provider hooks that avoid the two candidate exact-image residual axioms and the Route130 target bridge, replacing them by narrower Section 8 sidecar surfaces:

    • candidate exact-image residuals -> Route113 current semantic split;
    • target Route28 bridge surface -> Route21 concrete lower-projection / representative-action bridge.

    Kernel and determinant/pushout providers are kept exactly as in the current Route133 all-Route130-lowered route.
