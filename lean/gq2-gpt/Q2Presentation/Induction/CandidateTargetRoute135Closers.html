    Candidate/target Route135 Section 8 closers #

    This sidecar leaves all routed/public files untouched. It keeps the Route135 Section 7 provider choices from IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs135WorkerCombined and replaces only the Section 8 candidate/target hooks:

    • candidate current semantic packets are routed through CandidateRoute126Closers, replacing the two current semantic leaves by the Route115 scalar/Frattini packets;
    • the target Route21 bridge is routed through TargetKmodRRoute22ActualProofs, replacing the two Route21 residual leaves by canonical Y/K ~= B.C and canonical representative-action packets.
