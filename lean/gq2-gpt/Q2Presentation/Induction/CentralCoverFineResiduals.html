    Finer manuscript-backed residuals for the Section 8 bridge #

    This sidecar continues from CentralCoverResidualRefinement.lean. It does not close the Section 8 bridge from the current library; instead it replaces the current broad residuals by smaller manuscript-facing pieces.

    The split is governed by reference/q2_manuscript.tex, Section 8 sec:fourier, and by reference/plan_4turns.md's Stage-H requirement that main-clean theorems expose their actual axiom closure.

    Compared with CentralCoverResidualRefinement.lean:

    • each source lift residual is split into:
      • an exact-image obstruction index (def:Xgamma, lem:obstructionseparation, prop:finalfourier);
      • a Z^1(R) cocycle group of size z_R (lem:affinelifting, eq:mumultiplicity);
      • lift fibres and the boundary-framed exact-image decomposition (lem:covertransform, prop:finalfourier);
    • the children residual is split into the three strict-decrease strata of lem:strictdecrease: elementary-stage/proper-image/phase-cover targets. The elementary stage is further split into the lower quotient target C = Y/K and the finite proper-image subtraction family of eq:Mstage;
    • the partial-count residual is split sourcewise, with a separate source-interface agreement axiom identifying the candidate and local recipes. The local-side live axioms are named at the smaller residual layer: finite exact-image indexing, Tate obstruction maps, Euler/Tate cocycle cardinality, affine lift partitions, and the Tate/Evens/local-Arf source-interface comparison.

    No theorem below uses centralCover_realization, centralCover_liftTorsorData, centralCover_partialRecipe, or the previous sidecar's sourcewise/partial residual axioms.
