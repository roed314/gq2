    Integrated central-cover refinements #

    This file wires together the two Section 8 sidecars:

    • CandidateExactImagePacketClosers.lean, which splits the concrete candidate exact-image packet into boundary-postcomposition, obstruction-index/base, obstruction-map, and Frattini-cardinality packets;
    • ElementaryTowerResiduals.lean, which splits the elementary target/proper image residuals into concrete subgroup, quotient-map, cardinality, and exact-sequence packets.

    The target theorem has the same shape as centralCover_realization_proven_fine, but its axiom closure names the refined manuscript packets instead of the older coarse residuals.
