    Central-cover realization: residual split for the Section 8 bridge #

    This module targets the remaining non-local-CFT Section 8 residual centralCover_realization from Q2Presentation/Induction/CentralCovers.lean.

    The exact theorem

    theorem centralCover_realization_proven {p : FramedPair} (B : MinimalBlock p) :
    Nonempty (CoverRealization B)

    cannot be derived from the current Lean library without additional Section 8 orbit-combinatorial input. The reason is visible in the fields of CoverRealization B: Lean must be given actual source-by-source finite lift indices, obstruction maps, Z^1(R)-torsor fibres, boundary-framed surjection bijections, strictly smaller recursive children, and the common partial-count recipe. The current proved files provide the Fourier algebra and the torsor cardinality mechanism, but not those finite exact-image orbit partitions.

    The split below follows manuscript reference/q2_manuscript.tex, Section 8 sec:fourier:

    • CentralCoverLiftTorsorData is the lift-torsor/orbit half of prop:finalfourier, using lem:affinelifting and the central-cover exact-image partition lem:covertransform.
    • CentralCoverPartialRecipe is the source-independent recursive recipe half, corresponding to lem:properimagesubtraction, prop:nonzeroedge, prop:zeroedge, prop:phaseidentity, lem:phasecover, and lem:strictdecrease.

    This is also consistent with reference/plan_4turns.md: the plan requires finite-quotient/epimorphism-count semantics and no hidden axioms in accepted main-clean theorems. Accordingly, the theorem below exposes the exact residual axioms in #print axioms centralCover_realization_proven rather than hiding them inside a manufactured inhabitant of CoverRealization.
