    Finer residuals for the Section 8 central-cover realization #

    Q2Presentation/Induction/CentralCoverRealization.lean already replaces the monolithic Section 8 axiom centralCover_realization by two residuals:

    • centralCover_liftTorsorData;
    • centralCover_partialRecipe.

    This sidecar refines those two residuals further. It does not use the vacuity diagnostic route through an underconstrained MinimalBlock; the data below is the explicit Section 8 exact-image/lift data required by the manuscript.

    Manuscript anchors are reference/q2_manuscript.tex, Section 8 sec:fourier:

    • sourcewise lift torsor packets: def:Xgamma, lem:covertransform, lem:affinelifting, and prop:finalfourier;
    • strict recursive children: lem:strictdecrease;
    • shared partial-count recipe: lem:properimagesubtraction, prop:nonzeroedge, prop:zeroedge, prop:phaseidentity, and lem:phasecover.

    This matches reference/plan_4turns.md's finite-quotient/epimorphism-count architecture and its #print axioms discipline: the final theorem in this file has the same target statement as centralCover_realization, but its closure exposes only the finer manuscript residuals below, not the earlier coarser residuals.
