    Integrated Route144 Section 8 + pushout lowering #

    Route144 is a non-public attack route built from the verified Route143 frontier. It keeps the Route143 Section 7 kernel provider, but swaps:

    • Section 8 candidate realization to the existing Route130 provider;
    • Section 8 target realization to the Route144 same-tower provider;
    • Section 7 kernel to the Route143 actual Frattini/raw providers;
    • Section 7 determinant/pushout to the Route143 actual-pushout provider.

    So this route is intended to remove the broad Route143 Section 8 target packet surface and the five Route143 pushout atoms from the integrated audit closure.
