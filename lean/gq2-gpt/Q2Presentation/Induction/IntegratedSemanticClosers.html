    Integrated semantic route for the remaining Section 7/8 packets #

    This sidecar is the current final-route aggregator. It combines:

    • the Section 7 semantic group-tower/determinant packets from MinimalBlockGroupTowerSemanticClosers;
    • the candidate canonical concrete obstruction route from CandidateObstructionConcreteProofs, tied to the same canonical target quotient tower used below;
    • the proper-image actual middle-restriction route from ProperImageRestrictionActualClosers.

    The point is to avoid routing the final theorem through the three old proper-image residuals

    • centralCover_elementaryProperImageMiddleKernelMarkedData,
    • centralCover_elementaryProperImageMapsToLowerMarkedData,
    • centralCover_elementaryProperImageRestrictedSurjData,

    while preserving the manuscript semantics: the restricted maps are the actual restrictions of the target quotient map B -> C.
