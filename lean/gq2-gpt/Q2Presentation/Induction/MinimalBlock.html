    Stage §7 — A minimal non-scalar module layer in the wild kernel (manuscript sec:block) #

    This file builds the §7 block machinery (reference/q2_manuscript.tex, sec:block, l.3597–3771) that founds the nonterminal Fourier branch Q2Presentation.Induction.boundaryFramed_nonterminal_fourier_branch of Induction/Recursion.lean. Given a boundary-framed target whose marked 2-kernel L_Y has a non-scalar chief factor, §7 isolates:

    • the minimal non-scalar layer S ◁ P ≤ L_Y, the minimal Y-normal K ≤ P with KS = P, and R = Φ(K), M = K/R, T = (K∩S)/R, with simple head V = M/T (lem:simplehead, l.3658; lem:collapse, l.3677; lem:decorationblock, l.3710);
    • the determinant character space 𝒳_R = (R^∨)^B = (R^∨)^C, canonically the dual of the obstruction space O_R (lem:obstructionseparation, l.4213: 𝒳_R = Ŵ, W = O_R = (𝒳_R)^∨), and the lift-torsor exponent/size dimExp = 2·dim R, zR = 2^{dimExp}·|𝒳_R| = 2^{dimExp}·|Ŵ| (prop:finalfourier, z_R = 2^{2 dim R + dim 𝒳_R});
    • the determinant pushout on the simple head (prop:simpleheaddet, l.3730): for 0 ≠ λ ∈ 𝒳_R, the square map q_λ of the central pushout K_λ = K/ker λ satisfies q_λ|_T = 0, b_λ(T, M) = 0, hence descends to a nonzero nonsingular C-invariant quadratic form q̄_λ : V → 𝔽₂.

    Decomposition: proven vs. isolated #

    The abstract algebra is genuinely proven, sorry-free:

    • Part 1 (Annihilates/descend): the pure 𝔽₂-linear-algebra heart of prop:simpleheaddet — a QuadF2 M that annihilates a submodule T (q.form|_T = 0, q.polar(T,·) = 0) descends to a QuadF2 (M ⧸ T), and the descended form is nonsingular exactly when the polar radical of q lies in T. This is the descent of prop:simpleheaddet over the exact sequence 0 → T → M → V → 0 of lem:simplehead, reusing Quadratic.QuadF2.
    • Part 2/3 (MinimalBlock): the §7 block packaged as data, with the determinant character space 𝒳_R = (R^∨)^C (= Ŵ) constructed from R and the C-action and the obstruction space W = O_R := Module.Dual 𝒳_R, the exponent dimExp = 2·dim R and size zR = 2^{dimExp}·|Ŵ| in exactly the shape FourierBranchData.hzR consumes (zR_eq_fintype), and the determinant pushout family; prop:simpleheaddet (the descended nonzero, nonsingular, C-invariant head form headForm) is proven on top of Part 1.

    The genuinely deep finite-group / representation-theoretic inputs of §7 — the existence of the minimal non-scalar configuration (chief series + minimality of K), the Frattini–centralizer collapse lem:collapse (R central elementary abelian, K^4 = 1, via the odd Hall-lift coprime action + three-subgroup lemma), the radical identification T = rad_{𝔽₂[C]} M and (M^∨)^C = 0 of lem:simplehead, and the cohomology vanishing H¹(H_V, V^∨) = 0 used in prop:simpleheaddet — are isolated as documented fields of MinimalBlock (the "genuine local inputs"), exactly as FourierBranchData isolates the pre-Fourier obstruction realization. The block's existence for a non-scalar target is the single bridge axiom minimalBlock_of_nonScalar. See the closing report for the precise §8/bridge decomposition handed to the follow-up agents.

    Part 1 — Abstract 𝔽₂ quadratic descent (the algebra of prop:simpleheaddet) #

    The simple-head determinant proposition says that the square map q_λ : M → 𝔽₂ of the central pushout K_λ annihilates the radical T (both q_λ|_T = 0 and b_λ(T, M) = 0), so it descends to a quadratic form on the head V = M/T, which is nonsingular because the polar radical is exactly T. This Part proves that descent abstractly for any QuadF2 over 𝔽₂ — pure linear algebra, sorry-free.

      b_λ(T, M) = 0.

            Smart-constructor toolkit: the pushout square map is the proven extraspecial form #

            The manuscript's central-pushout square map q_λ(m) = λ(m̃²) (l.3724) is the class-2 Heisenberg square diagonal Quadratic.Extraspecial.extraspecialForm φ_λ — form c = φ_λ(c)(c), polar the commutator pairing bcl (extraspecialForm_eq_heisSquare, extraspecialForm_polar_eq_bcl, both proven in Quadratic/GaussSign.lean) — for the self-duality φ_λ : M → M^∨ of the pushout. These three helpers discharge the MinimalBlock.pushout_* fields from φ_λ, so a block can take pushout λ := extraspecialForm φ_λ and the pushout field is the genuine manuscript object, not an opaque black box. (Over 𝔽₂ the polar of extraspecialForm φ is the symmetrized pairing φ m m' + φ m' m, so the hypotheses are stated on that.)

            Part 2 — The §7 minimal non-scalar block as data (manuscript sec:block) #

            The block is packaged as a structure MinimalBlock p, parallel to FourierBranchData p of Induction/Recursion.lean. It carries the module-level outputs of the §7 tower Y ↠ B ↠ C ↠ H and the exact sequence 0 → T → M → V → 0 (eq:targettower):

            • the central elementary-abelian Frattini quotient R = Φ(K) (an 𝔽₂-space by lem:collapse), with the C = Y/K action actR;
            • the elementary module layer M = K/R with its C-action actM and the radical submodule T = (K∩S)/R; the simple head is V = M/T (lem:simplehead);
            • the deep lem:simplehead/lem:collapse/cohomology inputs as documented fields (dualInv_zero for (M^∨)^C = 0; the pushout_* fields for prop:simpleheaddet).

            From this data, Part 3 constructs and proves W = O_R = (R^∨)^C, dimExp, zR and the descended nonzero nonsingular head form headForm (prop:simpleheaddet).
