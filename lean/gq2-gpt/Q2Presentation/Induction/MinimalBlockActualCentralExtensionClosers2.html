    Actual central-extension closers from extraspecial determinant packets #

    This sidecar targets the seven final-route Section 7 packets introduced in MinimalBlockActualCentralExtensionPushoutProofs:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionSquareData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    It does not call those seven axioms. Instead it rebuilds their targets from the older same-family extraspecial determinant interface:

    • a central-pushout self-duality family phi;
    • extraspecial naturality for that family;
    • diagonal-zero, polar-zero, and radical-containment packets on the equivariant extraspecial family induced by naturality.

    The square map is the proven extraspecial square Extraspecial.extraspecialForm (phi lam), so the diagonal and polar packets are definitional and annihilation/radical containment follow from the proven extraspecial_annihilates and extraspecial_radical_subset helpers.
