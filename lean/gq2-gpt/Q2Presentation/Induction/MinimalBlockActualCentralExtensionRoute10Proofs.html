    Route10 actual central-extension pushout closure #

    This sidecar leaves the integrated route files untouched. It gives route10 a single coherent Section 7 actual central-extension / quadratic pushout provider and projects the seven current P5 packet targets from that same family:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionSquareData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    The remaining floor is the narrower actual semantic packet from MinimalBlockExtraspecialClosers3: the actual square/polar central-pushout family and the same-family simple-head determinant theorem. Thus the route10 hooks below avoid the seven P5 packet axioms and keep all square, polar, naturality, annihilation, and radical fields tied to one pushout family.
