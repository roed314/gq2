    Concrete Section 7 minimal-block closers #

    This sidecar keeps the routed capstone files untouched and adds a more concrete Section 7 reassembly route, following the manuscript's tower Y -> B -> C -> H and determinant pushout (eq:targettower, lem:simplehead, lem:collapse, prop:simpleheaddet).

    The main theorem below avoids the current pre-head invariant-character and pre-head determinant-nonzero residuals. It also splits the extraspecial pushout packets into central-pushout self-duality, naturality, diagonal/polar vanishing, and radical-containment witnesses.
