    Section 7 determinant semantic packet projections #

    This sidecar closes the four central-pushout semantic determinant packets from the narrower actual simple-head determinant packet for the same semantic family S.

    The target packets are:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    The proof does not call those four axioms. It projects them from minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData S, whose fields are exactly the same-family prop:simpleheaddet assertions for S.toQuadraticFamily.square.
