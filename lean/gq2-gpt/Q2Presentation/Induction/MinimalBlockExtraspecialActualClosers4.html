    Actual Section 7 central-pushout/determinant closers, fourth pass #

    This sidecar leaves the routed capstone files untouched. It replaces the two current final-route extraspecial residuals

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquarePolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData;

    by one route through the actual scalar central-pushout family and the four same-family prop:simpleheaddet pieces:

    • naturality of phi_lambda;
    • diagonal vanishing q_lambda|_T = 0;
    • polar vanishing b_lambda(T,M) = 0;
    • polar-radical containment in T.

    The determinant pieces below are indexed by P.actualScalar.toSemanticData, so they cannot drift to a different self-duality family than the square/polar packet.
