    Section 7 extraspecial determinant packet refinement #

    This sidecar refines the four independent extraspecial determinant residuals in the final Section 7 route:

    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialDiagonalZeroData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialPolarZeroData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialRadicalContainmentData.

    The replacement residual is one same-family prop:simpleheaddet packet on the actual extraspecial family induced by a central-pushout self-duality family. It states the manuscript conclusions directly on Quadratic.Extraspecial.extraspecialForm: naturality, annihilation of T, and polar-radical containment. The four old packets are then projections from that single object, so the routed closure no longer needs them as independent inputs.
