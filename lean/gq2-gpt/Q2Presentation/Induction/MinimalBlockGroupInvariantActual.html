    Actual lower group/invariant packet for Section 7 minimal blocks #

    This sidecar sharpens the current Section 7 lower group/module/invariant boundary without editing the routed files. The existing lower route consumes three residual packets independently:

    • minimalBlock_lowerGroupModuleData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadQuotientNoTrivialData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData.

    The manuscript uses one object: the chosen subgroup tower R = Phi(K) <= K <= L_Y, the quotient M = K/R, the lower target C = Y/K, the simple head M/T, and the scalar Frattini quotient. The packet below records that one-object boundary. It still leaves one finite group/representation theorem as a residual, but it prevents the final route from mixing unrelated lower towers, head-invariant data, and scalar quotients.
