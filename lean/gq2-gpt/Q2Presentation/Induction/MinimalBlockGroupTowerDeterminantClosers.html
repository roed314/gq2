    Group-tower determinant closers for Section 7 #

    This sidecar keeps the final target-quotient route but refines the determinant packet used at the Section 7 minimal block. The existing final route already uses minimalBlock_groupTowerData for the subgroup tower R = Phi(K) <= K <= L_Y. The remaining combined central-pushout packet minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquarePolarData is split here into:

    • a shared central-pushout quadratic family (phi_lambda, q_lambda);
    • the statement that q_lambda is the diagonal of phi_lambda;
    • the statement that the polar form of q_lambda is the symmetrized phi_lambda pairing.

    The split is tied to the same family, so the square and polar witnesses cannot silently come from unrelated pushouts.
