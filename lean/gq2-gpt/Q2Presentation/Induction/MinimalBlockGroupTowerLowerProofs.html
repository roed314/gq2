    Lower group/module proof packet for Section 7 #

    This sidecar refines the lower GROUP-TOWER/MODULE part of the Section 7 minimal-block closure. It does not edit the routed proof files.

    Manuscript anchors: reference/q2_manuscript.tex, Section 7, pages 40--42: eq:targettower, the choice of minimal K, R = Phi(K) = K^2 [K,K], M = K/R, T = (K cap S)/R, the lower quotient C = Y/K, and lem:simplehead.

    The current final closure exposes six independent residuals:

    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastOverChiefData;
    • minimalBlock_frattiniCollapseData;
    • minimalBlock_rawModuleData;
    • minimalBlock_rawActionData;
    • minimalBlock_kmodRBridgeData;
    • minimalBlock_headNontrivialData.

    The packet below replaces those by one coherent finite group/module object for the canonical chain: the same least K supplies the literal Frattini subgroup, the raw module/action data, the K/R ~= M bridge, and the nonzero simple head. This is stricter than the previous split because downstream users cannot mix unrelated choices of K, R, M, actions, and head data.

    Type 1

    Coherent lower Section 7 group/module data for one non-scalar chief factor.

    The fields are exactly the lower tower/module facts from eq:targettower and lem:simplehead, but tied to the same canonical dependency chain:

    • a least Y-normal K <= P with K S = P;
    • the literal Frattini subgroup R = Phi(K) with collapse facts;
    • the finite F_2 objects R, M = K/R, T, C;
    • the induced C-actions and stability of T;
    • the quotient bridge from the literal K/R to the raw module M;
    • nontriviality of the simple head M/T.
    • leastKernel : MinimalBlockKernelLeastOverChiefData chief
    • frattiniCollapse : MinimalBlockFrattiniCollapseData (MinimalBlockKernelChoiceSemanticData.ofLeastOverChief self.leastKernel)
    • rawModuleData : MinimalBlockRawModuleData p chief
    • rawActionData : MinimalBlockRawActionData self.rawModuleData
    • kmodRBridgeData : MinimalBlockKmodRBridgeData (MinimalBlockKernelChoiceSemanticData.ofLeastOverChief self.leastKernel) (MinimalBlockFrattiniKernelSemanticData.ofFrattiniCollapse ⋯) self.rawModuleData
    • headNontrivialData : MinimalBlockHeadNontrivialData (MinimalBlockRawTowerData.ofModuleAction self.rawModuleData self.rawActionData)
