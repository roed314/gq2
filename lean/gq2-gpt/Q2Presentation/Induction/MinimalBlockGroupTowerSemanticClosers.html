    Semantic Section 7 minimal-block closers #

    This sidecar refines the Section 7 minimal-block residuals without changing any existing routed file. It replaces the broad group-tower and determinant-pushout residual names by manuscript-shaped semantic packets:

    • a selected minimal subgroup K;
    • its Frattini layer R = Phi(K);
    • the extraction of M = K/R, T, C, and the induced actions;
    • the simple-head/character consequences attached to that same extraction;
    • one central-pushout extraspecial square family, with naturality, diagonal vanishing, polar vanishing, and radical containment all tied to that same family.

    The final theorems below are intended as drop-in routing targets for the coordinator. They do not use the existing residual axioms minimalBlock_groupTowerData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData, minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialNaturalityData, minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialDiagonalZeroData, minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialPolarZeroData, or minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialRadicalContainmentData.
