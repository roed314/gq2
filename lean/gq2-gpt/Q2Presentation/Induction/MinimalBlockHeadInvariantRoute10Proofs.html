    Route10 head/invariant refinements for Section 7 #

    This route-neutral sidecar targets the three route10 P4 residuals

    • minimalBlock_rawHeadQuotientNoTrivialData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadSimplicityActualData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData.

    The manuscript input is Section 7, especially lem:simplehead: invariant functionals kill the lower layer T, the quotient head M/T is the first non-scalar simple head, and nonzero invariant characters on the Frattini layer give the scalar quotients needed downstream.

    The proofs below do not close the finite group theory itself. They replace the three broad route10 residual names by four smaller semantic packets already exposed by the nearby concrete sidecars:

    • invariant functionals on M kill T;
    • the quotient head has no invariant dual;
    • every nonzero stable head submodule is all of the head;
    • the Frattini layer has a raw nonzero invariant character.
