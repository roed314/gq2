    Actual Section 7 invariant packet, second pass #

    This sidecar keeps the edit isolated while narrowing the two current final-route invariant residuals

    • minimalBlock_rawHeadQuotientNoTrivialData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData.

    The new residual is attached to one selected raw tower. On the head side it records the manuscript simple-head mechanism: invariant codimension-one kernels contain T, the quotient head has no nontrivial stable submodules, and the head action is not trivial. On the scalar side it records only a C-invariant character of R that hits 1; surjectivity to F_2 is then proved here.

    The route theorem below constructs MinimalBlockGroupInvariantRouteData without calling either of the two residual axioms named above.
