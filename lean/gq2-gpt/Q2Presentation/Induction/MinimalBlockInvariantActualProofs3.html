    Actual Section 7 invariant packet, third pass #

    This sidecar refines the residual

    minimalBlock_invariantActualProofs2Data : Nonempty (MinimalBlockInvariantActualProofs2Data D).

    The previous packet asked, in one axiom, for both the first non-scalar head mechanism and the lower scalar invariant character. Here those are split along the manuscript proof of lem:simplehead and the scalar lower-layer construction:

    • quotient-head simplicity is the only new head-side residual;
    • the head action is proved nontrivial from no-trivial-dual plus the known nonzero simple head;
    • the lower invariant character hitting 1 is proved from the scalar quotient packet.

    The route theorems at the bottom replace uses of minimalBlock_invariantActualProofs2Data without editing routed files.
