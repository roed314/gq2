    Lower invariant proofs for Section 7 minimal blocks #

    This sidecar targets the two remaining representation-invariant Section 7 residuals:

    • minimalBlock_dualInvariantVanishesData;
    • minimalBlock_rawInvariantCharacterData.

    The manuscript input is lem:simplehead on pages 40-42: invariant functionals on M vanish because they factor through the unique nontrivial simple head, and the lower scalar/Frattini layers provide the invariant determinant characters in (R^vee)^C.

    The theorem-level replacements below keep that split explicit:

    • dual-invariant vanishing follows from a radical-killing statement plus no-trivial-dual-invariant statement on the quotient head M/T;
    • raw invariant character data follows from a fixed scalar quotient of the lower Frattini layer R.
