    Route112 invariant-packet closers #

    This sidecar leaves IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs112 untouched. It targets the compact Route112 kernel invariant residual

    • minimalBlock_invariantActualProofs2Data.

    The replacement below rebuilds that actual packet from the same raw-head semantic fields used by the Route63/Route106 head lowering:

    • invariant hyperplanes contain T;
    • every nonzero stable quotient-head submodule is top;
    • the quotient-head action is not pointwise trivial;
    • the lower Frattini layer has a nonzero invariant character.

    Thus the theorem has the old packet target, but its axiom closure is the smaller manuscript-shaped raw-head frontier rather than the monolithic minimalBlock_invariantActualProofs2Data axiom.
