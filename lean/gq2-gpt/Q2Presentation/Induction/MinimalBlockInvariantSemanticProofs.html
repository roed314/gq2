    Semantic invariant proofs for Section 7 minimal blocks #

    This sidecar targets the two invariant residuals that feed the lower Section 7 minimal-block route:

    • minimalBlock_dualInvariantVanishesData;
    • minimalBlock_rawInvariantCharacterData.

    It does not edit any routed file. The manuscript-shaped narrowing is the same tower used on pages 40--42: after extracting the raw tower R, M, T, C, the simple-head argument supplies the no-trivial-dual quotient statement, and the scalar lower layers supply an invariant scalar quotient of R.

    The two theorem-level replacements below therefore depend only on the already split lower invariant packets minimalBlock_rawHeadQuotientNoTrivialData and minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData, not on the two broader residuals named above.

    Same-tower semantic data for the Section 7 invariant step.

    The first field is the Lean form of the manuscript argument for (M^vee)^C = 0: invariant functionals kill the scalar radical/prehead part T, and the quotient head has no trivial dual invariant.

    The second field is the scalar-lower-layer input: the Frattini layer R has a C-invariant scalar quotient. Over F_2, this is exactly the nonzero invariant determinant character used later in X_R = (R^vee)^C.
