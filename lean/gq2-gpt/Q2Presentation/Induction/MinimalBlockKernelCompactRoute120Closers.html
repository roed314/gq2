    Compact Route120 kernel closer #

    This sidecar keeps the Route118/Route120 public kernel closure compact while removing one avoidable leaf from the Route110 raw-head kernel provider.

    The least-kernel candidate is theorem-level: for the selected upper chief-factor subgroup P, the candidate K = P is Y-normal, lies in P, and satisfies K S = P. Downstream still needs universal leastness for the selected candidate, and still uses the compact Frattini collapse, raw quotient tower, and same-raw-tower invariant packets.

    No integrated route imports this module yet.
