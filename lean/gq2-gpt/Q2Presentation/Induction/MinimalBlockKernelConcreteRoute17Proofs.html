    Route17 kernel/concrete provenance sidecar for Section 7 #

    This sidecar leaves the routed proof files untouched. Route16 exposed one coherent residual packet,

    minimalBlock_kernelConcreteRoute16Data,

    for the Section 7 minimal-block kernel construction. Route17 proves that Route16 packet from narrower manuscript-shaped leaves already present in the tree:

    • the least normal subgroup K over the selected chief factor;
    • the full Frattini--centralizer collapse for literal R = Phi(K);
    • raw R, M, T, C objects, induced actions, and the literal K/R ~= M bridge;
    • the same-raw-tower simple-head/invariant semantic packet.

    The exported theorem minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelConcreteRoute17 has the same supervisor integration shape as Route16's group-tower provider, but its axiom closure no longer contains minimalBlock_kernelConcreteRoute16Data.
