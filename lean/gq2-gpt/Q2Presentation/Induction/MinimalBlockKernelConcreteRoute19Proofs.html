    Route19 minimal-block kernel concrete packet sidecar #

    This route-neutral sidecar leaves integrated routes and shared closers untouched. Route18 exposed sixteen narrow Section 7 kernel/head atoms. Route19 repackages that surface into four same-tower manuscript packets:

    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastOverChiefData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData;
    • minimalBlock_headInvariantSemanticRoute12Data.

    The aggregate packet below keeps the selected least K, the literal Frattini-collapse package for R = Phi(K), the raw quotient/action tower, and the simple-head/invariant package on the same raw tower. The exported minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelConcreteRoute19 theorem has the supervisor-facing shape needed for future integration.
