    Route20 minimal-block kernel concrete packet sidecar #

    This route-neutral sidecar leaves integrated routes and existing route files untouched. Route19 exposes four Section 7 kernel/head packets:

    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastOverChiefData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData;
    • minimalBlock_headInvariantSemanticRoute12Data.

    Route20 weakens that surface to two same-tower packets:

    • minimalBlock_lowerGroupModuleData, containing the coherent lower K, R = Phi(K), raw M,T,C, action, bridge, and nonzero-head data;
    • minimalBlock_headInvariantSemanticRoute12Data on the raw tower supplied by that same lower packet.

    The exported minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelConcreteRoute20 theorem has the same supervisor-facing shape as the route19 hook.
