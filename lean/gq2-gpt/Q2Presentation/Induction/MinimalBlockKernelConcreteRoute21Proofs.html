    Route21 minimal-block kernel concrete semantic split #

    This sidecar is route-neutral: it does not edit the integrated routes or any existing route file. Route20 exposes the Section 7 kernel contribution through two aggregate residuals:

    • minimalBlock_lowerGroupModuleData;
    • minimalBlock_headInvariantSemanticRoute12Data.

    Route21 replaces those aggregate uses by a same-tower fine packet. The lower tower is assembled from the six manuscript-shaped lower inputs already present in the tree, and the head/invariant semantic packet is split into its four field-level Section 7 statements on the selected raw tower.
