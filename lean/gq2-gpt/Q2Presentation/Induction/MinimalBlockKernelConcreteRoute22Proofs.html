    Route22 minimal-block kernel concrete close-out #

    Route21 exposed the Section 7 kernel/head contribution as ten field-level residuals. This sidecar keeps the route21 head leaves, but replaces the lower kernel/module part by the sharper concrete tower already present in the tree:

    • least K is split into a candidate plus universal leastness;
    • the Frattini collapse is the literal Phi(K) collapse packet;
    • raw R, M, T, C objects/actions are quotient-transported pieces;
    • the K/R ~= M bridge is the concrete quotient equivalence.

    The standalone route21 minimalBlock_headNontrivialData residual is removed: nontriviality of M/T follows from the route21 quotient-head action not being pointwise trivial.
