    Route23 minimal-block kernel/module close-out #

    Route22 exposed twelve independent Section 7 kernel/module residual calls: least-candidate and least-universal data, split Frattini-collapse conclusions, split raw object/action packets, the concrete K/R ~= M bridge, and four route21 raw head/invariant leaves.

    This sidecar keeps the integration files untouched and replaces that exposed surface by four coherent manuscript-shaped packets on one selected tower:

    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastOverChiefData, the least K <= P with K S = P;
    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData, the full Frattini--centralizer collapse for R = Phi(K);
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData, the raw R,M,T,C quotient tower, transported actions, concrete K/R ~= M bridge, and nonzero head;
    • minimalBlock_headInvariantSemanticRoute12Data, the same-raw-tower simple-head/invariant packet behind the four route21 leaves.

    These are not new axioms; they are existing Section 7 packet boundaries already present in the tree. The remaining frontier maps directly to manuscript Section 7: choice of the minimal normal kernel, lem:collapse, eq:targettower, and lem:simplehead.
