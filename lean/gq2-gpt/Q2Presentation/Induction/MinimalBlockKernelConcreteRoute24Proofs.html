    Route24 minimal-block kernel/head close-out #

    Route23 reduced the current Section 7 minimal-block frontier to four coherent manuscript packets:

    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastOverChiefData: choose the least normal K <= P with K S = P;
    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData: lem:collapse, the Frattini--centralizer collapse for R = Phi(K);
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData: eq:targettower, the raw quotient tower R, M = K/R, T, C, transported actions, bridge, and nonzero head;
    • minimalBlock_headInvariantSemanticRoute12Data: lem:simplehead, the simple-head and invariant-character consequences on that same raw tower.

    This Route24 sidecar keeps the integration/root files untouched and does not add new axioms. It proves the Route23-shaped packet from finer current APIs already present in the tree, so the exported group-tower theorem no longer has the four Route23 aggregate residual names in its axiom closure. The remaining residuals are the narrower Section 7 leaves for least K, lem:collapse, eq:targettower, and the simple-head/lower-scalar layer.
