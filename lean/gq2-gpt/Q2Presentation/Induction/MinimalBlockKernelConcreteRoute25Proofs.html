    Route25 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    Route24 keeps the lower kernel/module tower concrete, but its group-tower provider still closes the simple-head/lower-character layer through three route24 residuals:

    • minimalBlock_rawHeadQuotientNoTrivialData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadSimplicityActualData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData.

    This sidecar keeps the same concrete lower tower as Route24 and replaces that head/invariant layer by the finer route10 semantic split already present in the tree:

    • invariant functionals on M kill T;
    • the quotient head has no invariant dual;
    • every nonzero stable quotient-head submodule is top;
    • the Frattini layer has a raw nonzero invariant character.

    It also avoids Route24's direct concrete head-nonzero residual by deriving the head nontriviality from the same raw tower's not-pointwise-trivial head action, using the existing Route22 theorem-level conversion.
