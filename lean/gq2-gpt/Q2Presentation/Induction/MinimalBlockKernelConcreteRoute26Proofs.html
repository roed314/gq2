    Route26 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    Route25 keeps the concrete K, Phi(K), R, M, T, C tower split into the current fine Section 7 leaves, and closes the head nontriviality field from the quotient-head action leaf. This sidecar makes two further theorem-level reductions while leaving routed files untouched:

    • the least-kernel candidate is the chief-factor upper subgroup itself, so only the universal leastness packet remains for that split;
    • the head/invariant layer is assembled directly from the route21 semantic leaves, avoiding the route10 raw dual/functionals/character residual names.
