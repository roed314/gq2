    Route27 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    Route26 keeps the lower K, Phi(K), R, M, T, C construction split into the current concrete Section 7 leaves, but it still obtains the route12 head/invariant packet from the four route21 raw-head leaves.

    This sidecar keeps the same route26 packet shape and lower tower. It replaces those four route21 head leaves by the existing same-raw-tower actual invariant packet minimalBlock_invariantActualProofs2Data, then projects that packet to the route12 semantic head data. The selected raw tower is unchanged.
