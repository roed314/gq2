    Route28 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    Route27 keeps the concrete K, Phi(K), R, M, T, C tower split into the current Section 7 kernel/module leaves, and uses the monolithic same-raw-tower packet minimalBlock_invariantActualProofs2Data to obtain the final head/invariant interface.

    This sidecar keeps the downstream shape of route27 but replaces that monolithic invariant call by theorem-level assembly from the manuscript-shaped head split:

    • minimalBlock_rawHeadQuotientNoTrivialData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadSimplicityActualData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData;
    • the nonzero head already carried by the selected lower tower.

    Manuscript anchors: Section 7, pages 40--42, especially eq:targettower, lem:simplehead, and the paragraph after the choice of the least normal K <= P with K S = P.

    No new axioms are introduced here.
