    Route29 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    Route28 keeps the concrete K, Phi(K), R, M, T, C tower split into the current Section 7 kernel/module leaves, and replaces the old monolithic actual invariant packet by the four route28 head/lower leaves.

    This sidecar keeps the same downstream route28 shape, but rebuilds those route28 head/lower leaves on the selected raw quotient tower from the smaller route21-style semantic packets:

    • invariant hyperplanes contain T;
    • nonzero stable quotient-head submodules are top;
    • the quotient-head action is not pointwise trivial;
    • the lower Frattini layer has a nonzero invariant character.

    The same not-pointwise-trivial head action also supplies the concrete nonzero-head field of the raw quotient tower, so the provider no longer depends on minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteHeadNonzeroData.
