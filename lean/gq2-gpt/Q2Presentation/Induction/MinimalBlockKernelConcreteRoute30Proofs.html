    Route30 minimal-block kernel/head concrete-coherent provider #

    Route29 closes the head/invariant layer from the four route21-style same-raw- tower leaves, but still constructs the selected K, Phi(K), R, M, T, C tower through the split concrete Frattini/raw-object/action leaves.

    This sidecar keeps the route29 head/lower assembly and replaces the concrete split calls by the existing manuscript-shaped coherent packets:

    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData, the complete Frattini--centralizer collapse for the literal R = Phi(K);
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData, the same-tower raw quotient, transported actions, literal K/R ~= M bridge, and nonzero head.

    The least kernel remains the route26 selected upper-candidate construction, so the only least-kernel residual exposed here is the universal leastness of that selected candidate.
