    Route31 minimal-block kernel/head concrete split #

    Route30 exposes the Section 7 kernel/head frontier through three coherent kernel packets plus four route21 head/lower leaves. This sidecar keeps the route30 downstream shape, but rebuilds the provider through the route27-style actual same-tower head packet and the finer concrete Frattini/raw tower split.

    The exported group-tower theorem is intended as a kernel/head-only replacement for minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelConcreteRoute30. It does not edit the integrated route files.
