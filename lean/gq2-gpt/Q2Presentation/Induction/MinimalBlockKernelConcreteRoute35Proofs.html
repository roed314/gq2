    Route35 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    Route34 keeps the route21-style simple-head/lower-character split, but its concrete kernel/raw tower is still assembled from independent leaves. This sidecar keeps the same downstream packet shape and replaces that concrete tower cluster by the existing same-tower provenance packet MinimalBlockKernelModuleConcreteProvenanceData.

    The exported group-tower theorem is route34-compatible: it still produces the same MinimalBlockGroupTowerData, and it preserves the four route21 head/lower leaves rather than replacing them by older broader invariant packets.
