    Route37 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    Route36 exported the minimal-block group tower through the broad minimalBlock_provenanceSemanticData packet. This sidecar keeps routed files untouched and exports a drop-in provider whose axiom closure replaces that packet by the finer concrete provenance and same-tower simple-head/scalar closers already available in MinimalBlockProvenanceConcreteClosers2.

    The Route36 transparent two-packet bridge is retained as an audit comparison: it honestly lowers the broad semantic packet to concrete provenance plus the actual invariant packet. The public Route37 provider below uses the finer concrete-closer route instead.
