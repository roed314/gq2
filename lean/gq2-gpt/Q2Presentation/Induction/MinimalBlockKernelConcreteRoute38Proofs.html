    Route38 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    Route37 exposes the Section 7 kernel/head frontier through five concrete K/Phi(K) tower packets and four raw head/scalar packets. This sidecar keeps the routed files untouched and exports a drop-in group-tower provider whose closure replaces those nine names by narrower manuscript-shaped obligations:

    • least K over the selected chief factor;
    • the five Frattini-centralizer collapse conclusions for the literal Phi(K);
    • raw R, M, T, C objects and induced actions on the same tower;
    • the semantic K/Phi(K) ~= M bridge and nonzero head;
    • the simple-head hyperplane, irreducibility, nontrivial-action, and lower invariant-character statements used in the proof of lem:simplehead.

    The manuscript proves these facts from the finite target tower. At the current formalization frontier they remain residual packets, but they are strictly closer to the text than the Route37 concrete/raw-head aggregate leaves.
