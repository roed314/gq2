    Route40 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    This sidecar is intentionally standalone: no routed or root files import it yet.

    Route40 is a drop-in kernel/head provider over the Route39 sidecar. It keeps the Route39 selected K and raw quotient transport route, but strictly lowers two Route39 residual names:

    • MinimalBlockKernelConcreteRoute39.frattiniCentralizerCollapseData is replaced by the field-level Section 7 collapse packets for the same literal R = Phi(K): a nonidentity witness, centrality in K, fourth-power vanishing on K, and exponent two on R. Normality remains theorem-level from characteristicity of Phi(K) in the selected normal K.
    • minimalBlock_invariantActualProofs2Data is replaced by the third-pass same-tower split from MinimalBlockInvariantActualProofs3, matching manuscript Lemma 7.1 (lem:simplehead).

    Manuscript anchors: reference/q2_manuscript.tex, Section 7, pp. 40--42: Lemma 7.1 "Simple head" and Lemma 7.2 "Frattini--centralizer collapse".

    This is the Route39 collapse assembly with the monolithic frattiniCentralizerCollapseData residual replaced by field-level manuscript-shaped obligations from Section 7, Lemma 7.2:

    • Phi(K) has a nonidentity element;
    • Phi(K) is centralized by K;
    • K^4 = 1;
    • Phi(K) has exponent two.

    The normality field is still proved theorem-level from K.Normal.
