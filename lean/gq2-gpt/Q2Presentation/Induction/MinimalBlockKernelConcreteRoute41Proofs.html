    Route41 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    This sidecar is standalone: no routed or root file imports it yet.

    Route41 keeps Route40's concrete Section 7 kernel tower exactly as selected: least K, literal R = Phi(K), raw R,M,T,C, transported actions, quotient bridge, and nonzero head. It only refines the final head/invariant projection layer after the concrete tower is fixed.

    Compared with minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelConcreteRoute40, the public Route41 provider removes these Route40 residual names from its axiom closure:

    • minimalBlock_rawHeadQuotientNoTrivialData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadSimplicityActualData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData.

    They are replaced by existing finer, same-raw-tower packets:

    • direct dual-invariant vanishing, split as the two head-dual closers;
    • the nonzero-stable-submodule criterion for the quotient head;
    • the raw nonzero invariant determinant character on (R^vee)^C.

    Manuscript anchors: reference/q2_manuscript.tex, Section 7, pp. 40--42: Lemma 7.1 "Simple head" for (M^vee)^C = 0 and quotient-head simplicity, and the beginning of the determinant-pushout paragraph for X_R = (R^vee)^B = (R^vee)^C.

    No new axioms are declared in this file.
