    Route42 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    This sidecar is standalone: no routed or root file imports it yet.

    Route42 keeps the Route40 selected least kernel and the Route40 field-level Frattini collapse frontier. It refines the remaining Route41 raw quotient/head surface in two ways:

    • the raw quotient transport is split into object-level R, M, T, C packets plus induced action and T-stability packets, and the concrete K/Phi(K) ~= M bridge is exposed as its own literal equivalence packet;
    • head nontriviality is derived from the same raw tower's nontrivial quotient head action, and the final head/invariant layer is rebuilt from the manuscript lem:simplehead facts: invariant hyperplanes contain T, the quotient head is irreducible, its action is not pointwise trivial, and (R^vee)^C has a nonzero invariant character.

    Thus the public provider below has the same target shape as minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelConcreteRoute41, while its axiom closure no longer contains the Route41 raw/head names minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteRawModuleObjectsData, minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteRawSubquotientObjectsData, minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteRawFrattiniActionData, minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteRawModuleActionData, minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteRawTStableData, minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteKmodRQuotientEquivData, minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteHeadNonzeroData, minimalBlock_rawInvariantFunctionalsKillTData, minimalBlock_rawHeadDualInvariantZeroData, minimalBlock_rawHeadNonzeroStableEqTopData, and minimalBlock_rawInvariantCharacterData.

    The remaining old Route41 kernel leaves are the genuinely finite-group leastness and Frattini-collapse facts from Section 7.
