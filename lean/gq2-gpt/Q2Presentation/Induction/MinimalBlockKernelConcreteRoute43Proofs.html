    Route43 minimal-block kernel/head refinement #

    This sidecar is standalone: no routed or root file imports it yet.

    Route43 keeps the Route42 target shape but avoids the Route42 residual constants in the public provider. The replacement surface is closer to the manuscript Section 7 tower:

    • raw R, M, and C objects are carried with literal identifications to Phi(K), K/Phi(K), and Y/K;
    • raw actions and T-stability are carried as route43 transport packets and converted through Route42's definitional adapters;
    • the quotient bridge K/Phi(K) ~= M is supplied by the literal M packet, so MinimalBlockKernelConcreteRoute42.kmodRConcreteEquivData is not used;
    • the hyperplane conclusion is proved from the manuscript maximal-submodule form in the proof of lem:simplehead;
    • quotient-head irreducibility is taken in orbit-span form, the nontrivial action in witness form, and the lower invariant character in chosen-character form.

    The finite-group collapse leaves from lem:collapse and the leastness leaf are unchanged; those remain the hard Section 7 group-theoretic inputs.
