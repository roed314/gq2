    Route44 minimal-block kernel/raw tower cleanup #

    This sidecar is route-neutral: no routed/root file imports it yet.

    Route43 exposes the Section 7 kernel/head frontier through many independent literal/raw packets. Route44 keeps the Route43 target shape, but routes the kernel/module tower through the coherent manuscript packets already present in the tree:

    • the route26 upper-candidate least-kernel construction, so the only remaining least-kernel content is the universal leastness packet;
    • the full lem:collapse Frattini packet for the selected K;
    • the same-tower raw quotient/action/bridge/head packet for R, M, T, and C;
    • one same-raw-tower semantic lem:simplehead packet, rather than the Route43 maximal/orbit/witness/character leaves.

    Thus the public Route44 provider avoids the Route43 namespace-local raw/literal leaves and the split Frattini pointwise leaves in its axiom closure. The remaining residuals are the coherent manuscript-shaped Section 7 packets.
