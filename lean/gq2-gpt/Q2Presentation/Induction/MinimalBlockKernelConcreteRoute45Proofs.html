    Route45 minimal-block kernel/raw tower cleanup #

    This sidecar is route-neutral: no routed/root file imports it yet.

    Route45 is an integration-ready replacement for minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelConcreteRoute44. It removes the four Route44 kernel/raw aggregate leaves from the provider closure:

    • the route26 fixed-upper universal leastness shortcut is not used; the route selects the genuine least-over-chief Section 7 packet;
    • the coherent minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData leaf is replaced by the field-level Frattini-collapse packets already isolated lower in the tree;
    • the coherent minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData leaf is replaced by the raw object/subquotient/action/bridge/head transport packets;
    • the Route44 rawSimpleHeadSemanticData aggregate is replaced by the same-tower InvariantActualProofs3 route.
