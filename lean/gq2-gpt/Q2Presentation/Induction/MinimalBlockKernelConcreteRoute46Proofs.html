    Route46 minimal-block kernel/raw tower compaction #

    This sidecar is route-neutral: no routed/root file imports it yet.

    Route45 proves that the Route44 kernel/head aggregates can be expanded into field-level packets, but its drop-in provider has a much larger closure. Route46 takes the opposite conservative step for integration: it keeps the Section 7 lower tower coherent and only swaps the Route44 lower-kernel assembly.

    The exported provider replaces the three Route44 lower-kernel leaves

    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastUniversalData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData;

    by the older, same-tower minimalBlock_lowerGroupModuleData packet. It retains Route44's manuscript-shaped simple-head semantic packet for the selected raw tower. Thus the closure is strictly smaller than Route44's four active kernel/head leaves, without importing Route45's broad split.
