    Route63 raw/head closers for the Route42 frontier #

    This sidecar is route-neutral: it does not edit the active integrated route.

    Route60 currently assembles its raw/head transport through the twelve Route42 raw/head atoms. Route63 keeps the same consumer shapes, but obtains them from the lower Route43 manuscript packets:

    • literal R = Phi(K), M = K/Phi(K), T, and C = Y/K object packets;
    • transported actions on R and M, plus T-stability;
    • the literal K/Phi(K) ~= M bridge carried by the Route43 M packet;
    • the simple-head maximal/orbit/nontrivial-action/lower-character packets.

    The public raw-head provider below has the same target as MinimalBlockKernelRoute60RawHead.rawHeadTransportPieces_from_route60RawHeadClosers, but its axiom closure avoids the twelve Route42 atom constants.
