    Coherent route13 kernel/Frattini refinements for Section 7 #

    This sidecar is route-neutral: it does not edit the integrated route files. It targets the three coarse route13 kernel/Frattini residuals

    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastOverChiefData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData.

    The bridge theorems below keep the same public target shapes, but prove them from finer manuscript-shaped obligations: the concrete least-kernel packet, the split Frattini-collapse conclusions, raw quotient objects/actions, and the same-tower quotient-bridge/head packets. The exported group-tower and finite surjection providers are compatible with IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs13, but their axiom closure no longer contains the three coarse route13 names above.
