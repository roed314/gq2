    Concrete refinements for the route8 kernel/Frattini/raw residuals #

    This sidecar does not edit routed files. It refines the three route8 P3 residuals

    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastOverChiefData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData;

    into smaller manuscript-shaped packets on the same Section 7 tower.

    The file proves theorem-level replacements for the old three packet shapes, and then reassembles the route-ready lower/module and group-tower theorems through those replacements. The remaining axioms in the replacement route are the finer least-candidate/minimality, Frattini-collapse, raw-object/action, bridge, and head-nonzero packets below.
