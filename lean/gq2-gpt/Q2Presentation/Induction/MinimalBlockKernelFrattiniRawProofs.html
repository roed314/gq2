    Section 7 kernel/Frattini/raw tower refinement #

    This sidecar is deliberately route-neutral. It does not use the retired one-point/PUnit shortcut, and it does not replace the P3 frontier by the broad minimalBlock_kernelModuleActualProofs axiom.

    Instead it records the literal manuscript tower in two coherent residual packets:

    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData: the complete Frattini--centralizer collapse for the selected least K;
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData: the quotient objects/actions, the K/R ≃ M bridge, and the nonzero head for the same K and R.

    Together with the still-explicit least-kernel choice packet minimalBlock_kernelLeastOverChiefData, this removes the ten independent P3 residual calls from downstream route theorems and replaces them by manuscript- shaped same-tower obligations. The remaining proof work is exactly the finite §7 group theory: least K, the Hall/coprime-action Frattini collapse, and the quotient/action transport construction.
