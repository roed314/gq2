    Route63 least/Frattini closers for the Section 7 minimal-block kernel #

    This sidecar leaves the routed files untouched. It gives a drop-in group-tower provider with the same target shape as minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute61CombinedClosers, but replaces the five Route62 least/Frattini atoms by narrower manuscript-shaped packets:

    • least-over-chief is assembled from a candidate K plus its universal leastness proof;
    • cardinal nontriviality of Phi(K) is assembled from an explicit nonidentity Frattini witness;
    • the Frattini-centralizer step is assembled from a centralizer candidate to which leastness applies;
    • fourth-power vanishing is assembled from a kernel candidate for the fourth-power map to which leastness applies;
    • the elementary Frattini layer is assembled from the preceding two collapse steps through the Phi(K) = K^2 [K,K] exponent-two implication.
