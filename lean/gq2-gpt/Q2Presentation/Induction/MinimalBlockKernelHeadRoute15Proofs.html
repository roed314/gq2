    Route15 kernel/head sidecar for Section 7 #

    This file is route-neutral: it does not edit IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs14 or PresentationCorrect. It targets the minimal-block kernel/head residual cluster exposed by route14.

    The improvement over route14 is small but genuine. Route14 gets head_nontrivial from the standalone residual minimalBlock_headNontrivialData. Here it is derived from the route12 same-raw-tower semantic packet: if the quotient head M/T were subsingleton, then every endomorphism of it would be pointwise trivial, contradicting the not_pointwise_trivial field.

    The remaining kernel/module facts are still the concrete manuscript-shaped obligations for the fixed Section 7 tower: least K, the five Frattini-collapse conclusions for R = Phi(K), raw objects/actions, and the literal K/R ~= M bridge.
