    Unlike MinimalBlockLowerGroupModuleData, this records the subgroup-level Frattini-collapse content directly on the literal subgroup Subgroup.map Ksub.subtype (frattini Ksub). The centrality and exponent fields are not needed to assemble the current route packet, but they are part of the manuscript's lem:collapse content and prevent this residual from being merely a renaming of the older lower packet.
