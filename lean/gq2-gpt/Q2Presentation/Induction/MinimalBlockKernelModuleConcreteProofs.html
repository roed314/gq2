    Concrete kernel/module residual split for Section 7 #

    This sidecar does not edit routed files. It strictly narrows the current aggregate packet

    minimalBlock_kernelModuleActualProofs : Nonempty (MinimalBlockKernelModuleActualProofs chief)

    into the manuscript-shaped obligations on pages 40--42:

    • choose the least Y-normal K <= P with K S = P;
    • prove the actual Frattini collapse for the literal R = Phi(K), including normality, nontriviality, centrality in K, exponent two, and K^4 = 1;
    • extract the raw F_2 objects R, M = K/R, T, C = Y/K and the induced C-actions;
    • identify the literal quotient K/Phi(K) with the raw module M;
    • prove the simple head M/T is nontrivial.

    The route theorem at the bottom is the intended replacement for minimalBlock_lowerGroupModuleData_from_kernelModuleActualProofs when the main route is ready to drop the aggregate axiom.
