    Route103 kernel closers #

    This sidecar leaves IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs103 untouched. It provides a drop-in kernel provider replacing minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute98Closers.

    The compact Route98 provider exposes two broad Section 7 residuals:

    • minimalBlock_lowerGroupModuleData;
    • MinimalBlockKernelConcreteRoute44.rawSimpleHeadSemanticData.

    Route103 lowers that compact pair through the existing Route45 concrete route. The lower tower is rebuilt from the manuscript-shaped least-over-chief, Frattini-collapse, raw quotient/action, bridge, and head packets. The same-tower simple-head aggregate is rebuilt from quotient-head no-trivial-dual, quotient-head simplicity, and lower scalar quotient packets.
