    Route106 kernel Frattini/raw-module closers #

    This sidecar is isolated from the integrated route files. It targets the Route106 concrete Section 7 Frattini/raw-module subcluster exposed through MinimalBlockKernelConcreteRoute45.kernelModuleConcreteProvenanceData_from_route45.

    The replacement keeps the manuscript tower coherent:

    • choose the least normal K <= P with K S = P;
    • use one Frattini--centralizer collapse packet for R = Phi(K);
    • use one raw quotient tower packet for R, M = K/R, T, C, the induced actions, the K/R ~= M bridge, and the nonzero head.

    Thus the route-ready provider below has the same consumer shape as the Route103 kernel provider, but its closure no longer contains the eleven independent concrete Frattini/raw-module leaves singled out in Route106.
