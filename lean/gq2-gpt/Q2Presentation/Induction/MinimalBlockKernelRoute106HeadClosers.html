    Route106 kernel least/head closers #

    This sidecar is isolated from the integrated route files. It targets the Route106 Section 7 least-over-chief and raw-head subcluster left by the Route103 kernel provider:

    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastOverChiefData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadQuotientNoTrivialData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadSimplicityActualData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData.

    The lower tower keeps the Route106 coherent Frattini/raw-module route, but the least kernel is supplied by the candidate-plus-universal-leastness split from Route63. The same-tower simple-head packet is supplied by the Route63 raw-head lowering through the manuscript hyperplane/irreducibility/action/character packets, avoiding the three broader raw-head residual names.
