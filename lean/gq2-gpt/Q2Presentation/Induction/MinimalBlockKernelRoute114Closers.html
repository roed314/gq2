    Route114 kernel closers #

    This sidecar does not edit Route114. It targets the five kernel Section 7 leaves still visible in the public Route114 audit:

    • minimalBlock_invariantActualProofs2Data;
    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastCandidateData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastUniversalData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData.

    The candidate, Frattini-collapse, raw-quotient, and invariant packet surfaces are re-provided by theorem-level route closers. The universal leastness leaf is lowered to the selected least-kernel packet used downstream: one candidate together with its universal property, from which the route-ready MinimalBlockKernelLeastOverChiefData is recovered.
