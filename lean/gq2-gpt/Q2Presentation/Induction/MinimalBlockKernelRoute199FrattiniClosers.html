    Route199 Frattini closers for the Route198 kernel frontier #

    Route198 exposes ten Route72 least-kernel/Frattini atoms. This sidecar keeps the same downstream consumer surface, but replaces those Route72 atoms by smaller Route199 packets:

    • the least-kernel candidate is split into carrier, normality, containment, and product-image fields;
    • universal leastness is checked pointwise against an arbitrary candidate;
    • the internal Frattini witness is split into carrier, membership, and nontriviality;
    • centralizer and fourth-power packets are split into carrier/property/image fields;
    • the square/commutator layer is split into carrier, containment, and pointwise exponent-after-collapse data.
