    Route52 minimal-block kernel/module provenance #

    This sidecar keeps Route51 intact and swaps only the Section 7 kernel provider.

    Route51 exposes four kernel/raw leaves:

    • minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteProvenanceData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadDualInvariantZeroData;
    • minimalBlock_rawInvariantFunctionalsKillTData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData.

    Route52 narrows them to two same-tower manuscript packets:

    • minimalBlock_kernelModuleActualProofs, carrying the actual K, R = Phi(K), raw module/action data, bridge, and nontrivial head;
    • minimalBlock_invariantActualProofs2Data, carrying the simple-head/no-trivial and lower invariant-character facts on the selected raw tower.
