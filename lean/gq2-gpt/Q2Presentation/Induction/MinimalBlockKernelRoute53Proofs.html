    Route53 minimal-block kernel/module refinement #

    This sidecar keeps Route52 and the presentation files untouched. It replaces the two Route52 Section 7 kernel leaves

    • minimalBlock_kernelModuleActualProofs;
    • minimalBlock_invariantActualProofs2Data;

    by the smaller manuscript-shaped packets already exposed in the theorem-level closers:

    • the least K, Frattini-collapse, raw R,M,T,C objects/actions, K/R ≃ M bridge, and nonzero head packets from MinimalBlockKernelModuleConcreteClosers2;
    • the third-pass invariant split into quotient-head no-triviality, quotient-head simplicity, and lower scalar character packets from MinimalBlockInvariantActualProofs3.

    The exported minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute53 has the same target as the Route52 provider, so it can be used as a drop-in kernel sidecar in a later integrated route.
