    Route60 least/Frattini closers for the Section 7 minimal-block kernel #

    This sidecar is route-neutral: it does not edit the active Route59 files.

    Route55 still exposes the least/Frattini part of the kernel/raw frontier through the Route26 upper-candidate leastness shortcut and the field-level Frattini leaves. This file keeps the same manuscript tower shape, but narrows that surface as follows:

    • the least kernel is selected by the genuine least-over-chief packet, avoiding the Route26 minimalBlock_kernelLeastUniversalData leaf for the fixed upper candidate;
    • nonidentity of Phi(K) is recovered theorem-level from cardinal nontriviality;
    • the three remaining Lemma 7.2 conclusions are exposed as lower proof-step packets: centralizer collapse, fourth-power map vanishing, and elementary Frattini layer;
    • the raw head nonzero witness is recovered from same-tower head nontriviality, avoiding minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteHeadNonzeroData.

    The public provider at the bottom has the Route55 target shape, so it can feed a future Route60 replacement without changing downstream consumers.
