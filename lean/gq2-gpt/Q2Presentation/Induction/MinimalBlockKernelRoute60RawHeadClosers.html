    Route60 raw transport, K/R bridge, and simple-head closers #

    This sidecar is route-neutral. It does not edit the active integrated route.

    Route58 compressed the Section 7 raw/head frontier into broad same-tower packets. Route60 keeps the same manuscript tower, but exports theorem-level providers whose axiom closures are the lower-level leaves:

    • raw R, M, T, and C object packets;
    • induced actions on R and M, plus T-stability;
    • the literal K/Phi(K) ~= M bridge;
    • invariant-hyperplane, irreducible-head, nontrivial-head-action, and lower-character packets.

    The public provider has the Route55 target shape, so a future integrated route can swap it in where minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute55 is used.
