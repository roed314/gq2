    Route61 combined kernel closers #

    The two Route60 kernel sidecars lower complementary parts of the Section 7 minimal-block tower:

    • MinimalBlockKernelRoute60FrattiniClosers supplies the least kernel and Frattini-collapse data from lower proof-step packets.
    • MinimalBlockKernelRoute60RawHeadClosers supplies the raw module, K/R bridge, head, and lower-scalar data from Route42-style raw/head packets.

    This file combines them into the same drop-in provider shape as minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute55, without adding any axioms.
