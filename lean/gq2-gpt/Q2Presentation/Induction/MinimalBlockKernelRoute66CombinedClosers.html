    Route66 combined kernel provider #

    Route65 used the Route63 least/Frattini lowering, but its kernel provider still obtained the raw/head transport through the Route60 raw/head closer, whose closure exposed the twelve Route42 atoms.

    This sidecar keeps the same final MinimalBlockGroupTowerData surface, but combines:

    • Route63 least/Frattini lowerings from MinimalBlockKernelFrattiniRoute63Closers; and
    • Route63 raw/head lowerings from MinimalBlockKernelConcreteRoute63RawHeadClosers.

    The public provider is minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute66CombinedClosers.
