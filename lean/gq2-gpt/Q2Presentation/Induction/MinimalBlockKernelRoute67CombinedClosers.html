    Route67 combined kernel provider #

    This sidecar leaves Route66 and the promoted theorem untouched. It gives a drop-in replacement for minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute66CombinedClosers, but avoids the six Route66 least/Frattini residual atoms by routing through sharper manuscript-shaped packets:

    • a coherent least-kernel selection packet for the single selected K;
    • an internal nonidentity witness in Phi(K);
    • the Hall-commutator centralizer packet from Lemma 7.2;
    • the fourth-power kernel packet with the commutator-normal-closure image;
    • the square/commutator layer packet giving the exponent-two Frattini conclusion after the centralizer and fourth-power collapses.
