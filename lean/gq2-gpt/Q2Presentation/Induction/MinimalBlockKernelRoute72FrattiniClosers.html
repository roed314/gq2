    Route72 Frattini closers for the minimal-block kernel #

    This sidecar is route-neutral and only targets the Route71 kernel/Frattini frontier. It provides a drop-in group-tower provider with the same surface as minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute67CombinedClosers, but replaces the five Route67 Frattini leaves by smaller manuscript-shaped packets:

    • least K is split into a candidate and universal leastness for that candidate;
    • the nonidentity Frattini witness is split into an internal element and its nontriviality;
    • the centralizer and fourth-power steps are split into the subgroup/kernel core and the Hall-commutator image onto the selected chief factor;
    • the elementary Frattini layer is split into the Phi(K) <= K^2 [K,K] layer containment and the exponent-two conclusion on that layer after collapse.
