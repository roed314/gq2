    Route74 selected Frattini closers for the minimal-block kernel #

    Route72 split the Route67 least/Frattini packets into ten smaller fields. Some of those fields are intentionally dependent on the selected object, but their axiom statements quantify over arbitrary candidates/cores. This sidecar keeps the Route72 decomposition available while avoiding those ten Route72 atoms in the exported provider:

    • least-kernel and Frattini-nonidentity data are carried as selected coherent packets, then projected to the Route72 field shapes;
    • the centralizer and fourth-power packets are projected from the Route67 Hall-commutator packets as selected core + image data;
    • the final square/commutator containment is closed theorem-level by choosing the literal Phi(K) layer, with the exponent-two implication supplied by the Route63 elementary-collapse packet.

    The public theorem has the same target as minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute72FrattiniClosers.
