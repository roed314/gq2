    Route76 raw closers for the minimal-block kernel #

    This sidecar is a drop-in group-tower provider refining the Route75 minimal-block kernel frontier. It keeps the Route55 consumer shape, but makes two strict frontier changes:

    • the least/Frattini side is routed through the finer Route72 split packets instead of the selected Route67/Route63 atoms visible in Route75;
    • the raw-head side avoids two selected Route43 atoms by using the weaker same-tower head-action and lower-character formulations consumed downstream: not-pointwise-trivial action, and existential invariant character.

    The literal R, M, T, C object and transport-action leaves remain the Route43 manuscript packets.
