    Route81 kernel closers for the minimal-block route #

    Route80/79 exposes the Section 7 kernel frontier through seven Route78 aggregate packets. This sidecar keeps the downstream Route55 consumer shape, but routes the provider through smaller owned packets:

    • selected least-kernel data is split into a candidate and the universal leastness proof for that selected candidate;
    • selected Frattini/internal data is split into element and nontriviality packets;
    • centralizer and fourth-power collapse data are split into core and Hall-image packets;
    • the literal exponent-two implication is split into a commutator-generator step plus a generated-layer transfer, with square-generator exponent two closed directly from fourth-power vanishing;
    • raw object/action/bridge data is split into its three Route55-consumed components;
    • the simple-head semantic packet is split into the four fields of lem:simplehead.

    The public provider is a drop-in replacement for minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute79CombinedClosers.
