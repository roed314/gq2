    Route82 kernel closers for the minimal-block route #

    This sidecar advances the Route82 kernel frontier without editing the integrated Route82 proof. It keeps the Route81 least/Frattini lowering for the selected K, but routes the raw tower and simple-head layer through smaller Section 7 manuscript packets:

    • the raw R, M, T, C objects are selected as the literal Route43 objects R = Phi(K), M = K/Phi(K), T = (K cap S)/Phi(K), and C = Y/K;
    • the raw actions are selected as the Route43 transported actions on R and M, plus T-stability;
    • the bridge K/Phi(K) ~= M is theorem-level from the selected literal quotient object, so no separate Route81 bridge atom is used;
    • the four Route81 simple-head fields are supplied by Route43 proof-step packets: maximal-submodule containment, orbit spanning, a nontrivial-action witness, and a chosen invariant lower character.

    The public provider is a drop-in replacement for minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute81Closers, but it is intentionally not imported by the integrated route.
