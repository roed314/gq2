    Route85 kernel closers for the minimal-block route #

    This sidecar advances the Route88 kernel frontier without editing the integrated Route88 file. Route84 already lowers the raw tower to one coherent literal R, M, T, C packet and Route83 projects the simple-head fields from the actual invariant packet. The remaining Route84 provider closure still passes through ten Route81 least/Frattini atoms.

    Route85 replaces those atoms by two Section 7 packets:

    • a selected least-kernel packet, matching the manuscript choice of K normal in Y, K <= P, minimal subject to K S = P;
    • a Frattini--centralizer collapse packet for that selected K, matching the manuscript proof that R = Phi(K) is nontrivial, central elementary abelian, and that K^4 = 1.

    The downstream Route55 consumer still sees the same least, concrete Frattini, raw object/action/bridge, raw-head, and simple-head fields, so the public provider is a drop-in replacement for minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute84Closers.
