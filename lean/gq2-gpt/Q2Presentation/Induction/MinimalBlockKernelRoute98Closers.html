    Route98 kernel closers for the minimal-block route #

    This sidecar is isolated from IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs98. It provides a drop-in replacement for minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute95Closers and keeps two audit routes available:

    • minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute98Closers is the compact route. It replaces the four Route98 Section 7 kernel packets by the two coherent lower/head packets used by Route46.
    • minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_kernelRoute98Closers_fieldSplit is the fully lowered audit route. It avoids the four Route98 packet names by using Route40's field-level least/Frattini/raw/head split.

    The supervisor can choose which provider to integrate.
