    Lower group/invariant proof reductions for Section 7 #

    This sidecar closes or narrows the three current lower group/invariant packets used by the final Section 7 route:

    • minimalBlock_lowerGroupModuleData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadQuotientNoTrivialData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData.

    It does not edit routed files. The replacements follow the manuscript tower in Section 7, pages 40--42: choose the least K, set R = Phi(K), extract M = K/R, T, and C, use the simple-head vanishing (M^vee)^C = 0, and replace a surjective lower scalar quotient by the weaker raw nonzero invariant character over F_2.
