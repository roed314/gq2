    Theorem-level closers for Section 7 minimal-block packets #

    This sidecar keeps the existing MinimalBlockResidualRefinement API untouched and adds small theorem-level replacements/splits for the remaining manuscript Section 7 packets. The main low-hanging closer is the determinant nonzero field: once the simple head M / T is known nontrivial, the polar-radical containment forces the extraspecial diagonal to be nonzero. This proves the nonzero determinant packet from lem:simplehead's nontrivial-head field plus the radical field of prop:simpleheaddet.

    The file also records concrete reassembly lemmas for two other packet boundaries: nonzero invariant characters give scalar quotients over F_2, and the determinant annihilation/invariance packets can be split into diagonal/polar/naturality data.
