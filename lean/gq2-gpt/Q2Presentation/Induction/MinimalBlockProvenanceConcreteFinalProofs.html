    Concrete final provenance closers for the Section 7 minimal block #

    This sidecar pushes one step beyond MinimalBlockProvenanceFinalClosers. The earlier final closer assembled minimalBlock_provenanceSemanticData from Curie's four packets:

    • concrete subgroup/module provenance;
    • quotient-head no-trivial-dual data;
    • quotient-head simplicity;
    • lower scalar quotient data.

    Here the same selected concrete K/R tower is used, but the quotient-head no-trivial and lower scalar quotient packets are rebuilt from the still smaller raw ingredients on that tower:

    • direct (M^vee)^C = 0 dual-invariant vanishing;
    • raw nonzero invariant determinant character in (R^vee)^C.
