    Lower Section 7 pushout packet reductions #

    This sidecar is intentionally not routed into PresentationCorrect. It records the next lower proof boundary for the final-closure Section 7 PREHEAD PUSHOUT / EXTRASPECIAL residuals.

    The central-pushout square and polar packets are theorem-level consequences of a single self-duality family: the square is the extraspecial form Quadratic.Extraspecial.extraspecialForm.

    The extraspecial vanishing/radical packets are reduced to one coherent simple-head determinant packet on the same bare family. That lower packet states prop:simpleheaddet on the actual extraspecial quadratic form, using the proven Annihilates abstraction from MinimalBlock.lean, rather than postulating the diagonal and polar identities independently.

          Prop

          Lower same-family determinant packet for the PREHEAD extraspecial residuals.

          The annihilates_on_T and radical_subset_T fields are stated on the actual extraspecial quadratic form attached to F.phi lam. They are the formal counterpart of q_lambda|_T = 0, b_lambda(T,M)=0, and polar-radical descent in prop:simpleheaddet.

          • phi_natural (lam : Module.Dual (ZMod 2) D.R) : lam ≠ 0 → (∀ (c : D.C), lam ∘ₗ ↑(D.actR c) = lam) → ∀ (c : D.C) (m m' : D.M), ((F.phi lam) ((D.actM c) m)) ((D.actM c) m') = ((F.phi lam) m) m'
          • annihilates_on_T (lam : Module.Dual (ZMod 2) D.R) : lam ≠ 0 → (∀ (c : D.C), lam ∘ₗ ↑(D.actR c) = lam) → Annihilates (Quadratic.Extraspecial.extraspecialForm (F.phi lam)) D.T
          • radical_subset_T (lam : Module.Dual (ZMod 2) D.R) : lam ≠ 0 → (∀ (c : D.C), lam ∘ₗ ↑(D.actR c) = lam) → ∀ (m : D.M), (∀ (m' : D.M), ((Quadratic.Extraspecial.extraspecialForm (F.phi lam)).polar m) m' = 0) → m ∈ D.T
