    Route15 semantic central-pushout reduction #

    This sidecar is deliberately local: it creates a route15 replacement hook for the §7 determinant/central-pushout cluster without editing the integrated route files.

    The route14 pushout theorem currently bottoms out in seven route12 residuals:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    Manuscript §7 constructs the scalar pushout K_lambda = K / ker lambda. Once the central-pushout pairing phi_lambda is available, the square used by the route is the extraspecial square form attached to that same pairing, so the quadratic family, square-diagonal identity, and polar-symmetry identity are already theorem-level projections from MinimalBlockPreheadCentralPushoutSemanticData.

    The resulting route15 floor is therefore strictly smaller than the route12 cluster: it still needs the missing semantic phi_lambda family and the same-family prop:simpleheaddet semantic facts, but it does not assume the separate square/diagonal/polar packets.
