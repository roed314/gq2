    Route16 semantic pushout/determinant reduction #

    Route15 bottoms out in one semantic central-pushout family plus four independent same-family semantic determinant facts:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    This sidecar keeps route15's central-pushout family, but replaces the four determinant facts by the narrower manuscript-shaped actual simple-head determinant packet for that same family. In manuscript terms this is the prop:simpleheaddet packet for the scalar pushout K_lambda = K / ker lambda: naturality, vanishing on T, polar vanishing on T x M, and polar-radical containment are projected from the actual determinant square.
