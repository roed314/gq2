    Route197 Section 7/6 determinant lowerer #

    Route196 exposes the two pushout/determinant atoms

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualScalarCentralPushoutData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    This sidecar gives a Route196-compatible provider whose closure avoids both atoms. The replacement surface is the lower determinant-semantic manuscript surface:

    • the standard scalar pushout square/polar packets minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData, and minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData;
    • the four same-family simple-head determinant pieces minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData, and minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    Manuscript anchors: Section 6 for the scalar determinant square/polar family and Section 7.1, prop:simpleheaddet, for the simple-head determinant pieces.
