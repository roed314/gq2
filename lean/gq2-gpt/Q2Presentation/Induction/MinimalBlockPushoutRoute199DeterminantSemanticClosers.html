    Route199 determinant-semantic closer for the Route198 pushout atoms #

    Route198 exposes seven determinant-semantic atoms:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    This sidecar keeps the same downstream determinant-provider shape, but ties the seven fields to one semantic central-pushout family S and one actual simple-head determinant packet for that same S. The square/diagonal/polar fields are definitional projections of S, and the four prop:simpleheaddet semantic fields are theorem-level projections of the actual determinant packet.

    The resulting integrated route removes the seven Route198 atom names from the finite theorem closure and replaces them by the smaller same-family surface:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.
