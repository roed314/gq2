    Route19 minimal-block pushout square-polar/determinant sidecar #

    Route18 left two coherent Section 7 pushout residuals:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquarePolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    This sidecar replaces that two-residual surface by the stricter finite linear-algebra packets already exposed by the Section 7 API:

    • one standard same-family square/polar packet;
    • extraspecial naturality, diagonal-zero, polar-zero, and radical-containment packets for that same family.

    The supervisor-facing hook is minimalBlock_preheadPushoutRouteLowerData_from_route19ActualDeterminant.
