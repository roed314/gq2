    Route20 minimal-block pushout sidecar #

    Route19 exposes seven independent Section 7 pushout/determinant residuals: three central-pushout square/polar packets and four extraspecial determinant packets.

    Route20 routes the same lower pushout target through the actual central-extension pushout packet from MinimalBlockCentralPushoutFinalClosers. That packet is the manuscript-shaped object for the scalar pushouts K_lambda = K / ker lambda: it carries the same-family pairing, square map, diagonal/polar identifications, naturality, annihilation on T, and radical containment. The theorem-level projections below recover the route19-facing standard, actual determinant, and lower pushout packets from that one packet.
