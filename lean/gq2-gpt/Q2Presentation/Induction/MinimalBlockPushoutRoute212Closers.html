    Route212 determinant/pushout closers #

    Route212 currently imports the Route199 determinant provider, whose closure contains the two aggregate Section 7 determinant/pushout residuals

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSemanticData;

It gives a Route212-owned drop-in determinant provider whose closure reconstructs those two aggregate packets from the stricter manuscript-shaped surface already available in the project: one coherent scalar square/polar central-pushout packet and the four semantic simple-head determinant pieces.
