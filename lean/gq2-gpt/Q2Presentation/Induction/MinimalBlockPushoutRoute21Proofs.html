    Route21 minimal-block actual central-extension pushout sidecar #

    Route20 exposes the Section 7 pushout/determinant step through the single broad residual

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPushoutData.

    The older split-packet API in MinimalBlockActualCentralExtensionPushoutProofs already separates that manuscript construction into the same-family pieces used in the paper:

    • the scalar central-pushout pairing phi_lambda;
    • the actual square map q_lambda;
    • the diagonal identity q_lambda(m) = phi_lambda(m,m);
    • the polar identity;
    • equivariance/naturality;
    • annihilation on T;
    • polar-radical containment in T.

    This sidecar gives route21-named hooks for later integration. It reassembles the route20-facing lower pushout packet from those strictly finer residuals and does not use minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPushoutData.
