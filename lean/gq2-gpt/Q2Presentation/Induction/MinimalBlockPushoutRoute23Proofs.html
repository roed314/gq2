    Route23 minimal-block pushout/determinant close-out #

    Route22 narrowed the Section 7 determinant/pushout branch to the two broad leaves

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquarePolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    This sidecar rebuilds the same route22-facing packet from finer current APIs:

    • the standard same-family square/polar packets minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData, and minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData;
    • the four semantic prop:simpleheaddet packets for the same projected actual scalar family: minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData, and minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    Manuscript anchors: reference/q2_manuscript.tex, Section 7 prop:simpleheaddet and Section 8's Fourier bridge. The route23 packet keeps the square/polar family and determinant assertions tied to the same actual scalar central pushout, matching the paper's scalar quotients K_lambda = K / ker lambda.
