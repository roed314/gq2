    Route26 minimal-block pushout/determinant refinement #

    Route25 exposes the Section 7 pushout frontier as seven same-family leaves:

    • the scalar pushout square/polar side: minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData, and minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData;
    • the determinant side of prop:simpleheaddet: minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData, and minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    This sidecar keeps route25's lower-route output shape, but replaces the four determinant semantic leaves by the stricter same-family actual determinant packet

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    The actual determinant packet is indexed by the semantic family projected from the same actual scalar central pushout used for the square/polar side, so the route26 data still has the same K_lambda = K / ker lambda family on both sides.
