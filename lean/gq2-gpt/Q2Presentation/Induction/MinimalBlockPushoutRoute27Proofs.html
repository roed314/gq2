    Route27 minimal-block pushout/determinant refinement #

    Route26 constructs the lower pushout provider from an actual scalar central pushout family plus the same-family actual determinant packet. Its scalar side still bottoms out in the three split square/polar leaves

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData.

    This sidecar keeps route26's output shape but chooses the semantic central-pushout family as the scalar square source. The square is the extraspecial form of the same phi_lambda by definition, so the three split square/polar leaves are projected theorem-level from the single semantic family.

    The determinant side remains the route26 same-family actual determinant packet minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData for that same semantic family.
