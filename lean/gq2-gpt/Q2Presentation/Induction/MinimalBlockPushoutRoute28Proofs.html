    Route28 minimal-block pushout/determinant refinement #

    Route27 exposes the Section 7 pushout/determinant frontier as two coherent residuals:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    This sidecar keeps the same downstream lower-route shape, but splits those two route27 residuals into the finer manuscript-shaped packets already available in the Section 7 API.

    The scalar central-pushout family is built from the standard same-family quadratic packet, square diagonal, and polar symmetry leaves. The determinant side is the split prop:simpleheaddet packet for that same actual scalar family: naturality, diagonal vanishing on T, polar vanishing on T x M, and radical containment. Manuscript anchor: Section 7, pages 41-42, especially prop:simpleheaddet for the scalar pushouts K_lambda = K / ker lambda.
