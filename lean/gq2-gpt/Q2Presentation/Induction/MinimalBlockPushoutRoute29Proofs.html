    Route29 minimal-block pushout/determinant refinement #

    Route28 exposes the Section 7 pushout/determinant frontier as seven same-family leaves:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    This sidecar keeps route28's downstream lower-route shape, but replaces those semantic leaves by the actual central-extension split packets from MinimalBlockActualCentralExtensionPushoutProofs. The route28 packets become theorem-level projections from one same-family actual scalar central-extension pushout datum: the phi_lambda family, its square maps, diagonal and polar identities, naturality, annihilation on T, and radical containment.

    Manuscript anchors: Section 7, especially prop:simpleheaddet, and the Section 8 radical-edge/Fourier use of the descended scalar pushouts.
