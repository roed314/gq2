    Route30 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route29 replaced the two live Section 7 pushout residuals

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData

    by one actual central-extension pushout packet assembled from seven split actual K_lambda = K / ker lambda packets.

    This route30 sidecar keeps the route29 downstream shape but supplies its actual central-extension packet through MinimalBlockActualCentralExtensionClosers2. Thus a future route swap can use the same semantic family and same-family determinant packet while avoiding the seven route29 split actual central-extension packet axioms. The remaining pushout floor is the narrower self-duality/extraspecial determinant interface used by Closers2.
