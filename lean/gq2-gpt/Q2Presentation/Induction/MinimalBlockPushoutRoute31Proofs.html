    Route31 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route30 supplies the lower Section 7 pushout route through the extraspecial self-duality interface. Its provider closure is the five route32 residuals

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSelfDualityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialDiagonalZeroData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialPolarZeroData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialRadicalContainmentData.

    This sidecar keeps the route30 downstream shape, but rebuilds the actual central-extension packet from a same-family semantic determinant route:

    • the concrete central-pushout quadratic family;
    • its square-diagonal and polar-symmetry identities;
    • one actual simple-head determinant packet on the semantic square induced by that same family.

    Thus the route-compatible hook below should make the five route30 residual names disappear from the provider closure, replacing them by the four more concrete same-family packets.
