    Route35 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route34's pushout branch still depends on the broad residual minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPushoutData.

    This sidecar keeps the route34-facing conclusion, but obtains the actual central-extension packet from the existing extraspecial/self-duality closers in MinimalBlockActualCentralExtensionClosers2. Consequently the route35 lower provider has no dependency on the broad route34 residual and no dependency on the seven split actual-central-extension residuals; its remaining closure is the more explicit extraspecial/self-duality determinant floor.
