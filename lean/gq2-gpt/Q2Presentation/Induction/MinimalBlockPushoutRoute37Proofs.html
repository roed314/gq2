    Route37 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route36 leaves two Section 7 prehead central-pushout/determinant residuals:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquarePolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    The manuscript separates these into smaller assertions. The scalar pushout first supplies one shared quadratic family together with the square-diagonal and polar-symmetry identities. The simple-head determinant theorem then asserts, for that same family, naturality, diagonal vanishing on T, polar vanishing on T x M, and polar-radical containment.

    This Route37 sidecar does not claim a full proof of prop:simpleheaddet. Instead, it routes through the existing semantic split:

    • the combined square/polar packet is recovered from the standard shared-family, square-diagonal, and polar-symmetry packets;
    • the bundled actual simple-head determinant packet is recovered from the coherent semantic determinant packet, which itself consists of the four manuscript-shaped same-family determinant assertions above.

    Thus the exported lower-route provider has the same conclusion as the Route36 provider while avoiding both Route36 residual names.
