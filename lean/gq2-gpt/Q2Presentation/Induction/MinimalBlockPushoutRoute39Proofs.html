    Route39 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route38 leaves two Section 7 pushout residuals:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualScalarCentralPushoutData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    The manuscript's Section 7 construction is more specific than either bundled packet: for each nonzero invariant scalar character it forms the actual central pushout K_lambda = K / ker lambda, records the square map and its diagonal and polar identities, and then proves prop:simpleheaddet for the same square map.

    This Route39 sidecar keeps the Route38-facing conclusion but obtains both Route38 packets from the existing split actual-central-extension packets in MinimalBlockActualCentralExtensionPushoutProofs. Thus the exported provider does not depend on the two Route38 residual names. Its remaining nonlocal frontier is the strictly smaller same-family split:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionSquareData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    No integration or root file imports this sidecar yet.
