    Route40 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route39's Section 7 pushout provider replaces the old bundled residuals by the seven actual central-extension packet leaves:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionSquareData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    This Route40 sidecar keeps Route39's downstream shape, but obtains the broad actual central-extension packet from MinimalBlockExtraspecialClosers3. That closer reassembles the actual K_lambda = K / ker lambda pushout data from the manuscript-shaped square/polar construction and the same-family prop:simpleheaddet determinant packet (manuscript §7.1, Proposition 7.4, with the determinant background from §6).

    Thus the provider below is drop-in compatible with Route39 while removing all seven Route39 split actual-central-extension residual names from its axiom closure. No new axiom is introduced here.
