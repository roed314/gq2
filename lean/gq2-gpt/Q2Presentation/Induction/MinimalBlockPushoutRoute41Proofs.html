    Route41 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route40 lowers the Section 7 determinant-pushout branch to two coherent leaves:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquarePolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    This sidecar routes the same lower pushout interface through the finer packet split already present in the Section 7 API. The scalar central pushout is recorded by the standard same-family square/polar packet MinimalBlockPreheadCentralPushoutStandardData: a shared phi_lambda, q_lambda, the square-diagonal identity, and polar-symmetry. The simple-head determinant side is recorded by MinimalBlockPreheadCentralPushoutCoherentDeterminantSemanticData: the four same-family conclusions of manuscript Section 7.1, Proposition 7.4 (naturality, q_lambda|_T = 0, b_lambda(T,M) = 0, and polar-radical containment). The determinant square/polar background is the Section 6 construction of central double covers and extraspecial square forms.

    No new axiom is introduced here. The checkpoints at the bottom should avoid both Route40 residual names and expose the finer standard/coherent packet floor.
