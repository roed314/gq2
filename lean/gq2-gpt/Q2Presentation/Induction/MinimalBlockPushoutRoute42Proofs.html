    Route42 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route41 exposes the Section 7 pushout/determinant frontier as seven split same-family packet leaves:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    This sidecar routes the same lower pushout interface through the actual scalar central-pushout packet and the actual simple-head determinant packet for the semantic family projected from that same scalar pushout. Thus the seven Route41 leaf names disappear from the lower-provider closure, replaced by the two manuscript-shaped actual packets already present in the local Section 7 API:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualScalarCentralPushoutData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    No new axiom is introduced here.
