    Route43 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route42 leaves the Section 7 pushout branch at two bundled actual packet boundaries:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualScalarCentralPushoutData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    The manuscript's construction is more specific: for every nonzero invariant scalar character lambda : R -> F_2, it forms the actual central extension pushout K_lambda = K / ker lambda, records the square map and its diagonal and polar identities, and proves prop:simpleheaddet for that same square map.

    The older split actual-central-extension API already exposes exactly those same-family pieces. Route43 reassembles them to the broad actual central-extension packet and then projects to the Route42 interface. Thus the Route42 actual scalar and actual determinant leaves disappear from the Route43 provider closure and are replaced by the stricter manuscript-shaped split:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionSquareData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    No new axiom is introduced here.
