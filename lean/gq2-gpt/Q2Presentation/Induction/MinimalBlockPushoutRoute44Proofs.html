    Route44 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route43 exposes the Section 7 actual central-extension pushout as seven split same-family leaves:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionSquareData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    The manuscript Section 7 determinant-pushout argument is more coherent than that frontier: it fixes one actual scalar central-pushout/extraspecial family phi_lambda, uses the extraspecial square attached to that family, and proves prop:simpleheaddet for the same family.

    This sidecar keeps the Route43 downstream shape but obtains all seven split targets from MinimalBlockExtraspecialClosers3's coherent actual extraspecial packet. Thus Route44 removes the seven Route43 split residual names from the provider closure. Its remaining pushout frontier is the lower two-piece extraspecial floor:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquarePolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    No integrated/root route file imports this sidecar yet.
