    Route46 minimal-block pushout/determinant provider #

    Route44 leaves the Section 7 pushout branch at two broad packets:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquarePolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    Route45 avoids those names by expanding them into seven split square/polar and semantic simple-head determinant packets. Route46 keeps the final-route surface small instead: it uses the manuscript-shaped actual central-extension pushout packet from MinimalBlockCentralPushoutFinalClosers, recording the construction of the scalar central pushouts K_lambda = K / ker lambda, their square/polar identities, naturality, and the same-family prop:simpleheaddet conclusions.

    The exported hook minimalBlock_preheadPushoutRouteLowerData_from_route46 is a drop-in replacement for minimalBlock_preheadPushoutRouteLowerData_from_route44. Its nonstandard closure is the single actual-pushout residual minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPushoutData, rather than Route44's two broad residuals or Route45's seven expanded semantic residuals.
