    Route50 minimal-block pushout/determinant narrowing #

    Route49 exposes one determinant leaf:

    • minimalBlock_preheadDeterminantRoute49Data.

    That packet is exactly the data consumed by the nonterminal Fourier branch: one equivariant extraspecial family, annihilation on T, and polar-radical containment. This Route50 sidecar keeps the same consumed surface but replaces the opaque Route49 leaf by a single manuscript-shaped simple-head determinant packet for one bare extraspecial family.

    Manuscript anchor: Section 7, prop:simpleheaddet. The Route50 packet records one family phi_lambda together with the simple-head determinant theorem for the extraspecial square form attached to that same family. The Route49-consumed annihilation and radical packets are theorem-level projections from it.
