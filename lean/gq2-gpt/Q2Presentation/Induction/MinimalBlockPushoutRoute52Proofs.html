    Route52 minimal-block pushout/determinant narrowing #

    Route51 still consumes the Route50 determinant provider minimalBlock_preheadDeterminantPackets_from_route50, whose closure bottoms out in the single Route50 residual minimalBlock_preheadSimpleHeadDeterminantRoute50Data.

    This sidecar proves the same provider surface from the manuscript-shaped Section 7 determinant pieces:

    • one semantic central-pushout family S;
    • naturality/equivariance of S.phi;
    • diagonal vanishing on T;
    • polar vanishing on T x M;
    • radical containment in T.

    Those are exactly the four prop:simpleheaddet conclusions for the scalar central pushouts K_lambda = K / ker lambda, tied to the same semantic family. No new residual axiom is introduced here.
