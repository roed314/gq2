    Route53 minimal-block pushout/determinant narrowing #

    Route52 closes the determinant side from one semantic central-pushout family plus four semantic prop:simpleheaddet packets:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    This sidecar keeps the Route52 provider surface but routes it through the same-family actual determinant packet already present in the Section 7 API. The remaining determinant obligations are the concrete square/polar central-pushout packets and the actual simple-head determinant theorem for that same projected semantic family.
