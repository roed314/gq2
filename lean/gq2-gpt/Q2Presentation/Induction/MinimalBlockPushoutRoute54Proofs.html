    Route54 minimal-block pushout/determinant narrowing #

    Route53 replaces the older semantic pushout family by the split square/polar packets

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData;

    and keeps the actual simple-head determinant packet for the semantic family projected from those split packets.

    This sidecar keeps Route53's provider surface, but replaces those four leaves by one same-family actual packet. The packet records the actual scalar central-pushout construction and the simple-head determinant theorem for its own semantic projection. Thus the square map, diagonal identity, polar identity, semantic family, and determinant theorem cannot come from unrelated choices.
