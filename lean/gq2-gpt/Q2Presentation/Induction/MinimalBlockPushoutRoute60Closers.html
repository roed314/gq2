    Route60 minimal-block pushout/determinant closers #

    Route58 normalized the actual central-extension square to the extraspecial square attached to phi_lambda, removing the square, diagonal, and polar residuals from the final Section 7 pushout cluster. The final route still sees four non-local packets:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    This Route60 sidecar does not integrate a new final route. It exports a drop-in Route58-shaped provider whose closure replaces those four packet names by lower-level manuscript-shaped obligations from §7:

    • construction of the scalar pushout pairing family for K_lambda = K / ker lambda;
    • conjugation naturality of that pairing;
    • vanishing of the diagonal square on T;
    • vanishing of the polar form on T x M;
    • containment of the symmetrized polar radical in T.

    The assembly from those packets to Route58 data is theorem-level: annihilation is extraspecial_annihilates, and radical containment is extraspecial_radical_subset.
