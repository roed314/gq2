    Route63 minimal-block pushout/determinant closers #

    Route62's integrated theorem still consumes minimalBlock_preheadDeterminantPackets_from_route60, whose axiom closure contains five Route60 determinant-pushout atoms. This sidecar leaves routed files untouched and reconstructs the same Route60 packet surface from the lower standard/extraspecial pushout packets already exposed by the Section 7 semantic API.

    The main exported replacement is minimalBlock_preheadDeterminantPackets_from_route63. It has the same downstream surface as the Route60 provider, but its closure avoids the five Route60 atoms:

    • minimalBlock_route60ScalarPushoutPairingData;
    • minimalBlock_route60PairingNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_route60DiagonalVanishingOnTData;
    • minimalBlock_route60PolarVanishingOnTData;
    • minimalBlock_route60PolarRadicalContainmentData.

    For integration auditing, the file also mirrors the current Route62 final theorems under currentIntegrated63 names without editing IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs62.lean or PresentationCorrect.lean.
