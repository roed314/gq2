    Route81 minimal-block determinant/pushout closer #

    Route80's determinant/pushout provider leaves six Section 7 atoms:

    • the split scalar central-pushout square/polar family;
    • three Route79 fields for the actual simple-head determinant theorem.

    This sidecar keeps the same downstream determinant-packet surface, but routes it through smaller same-family packets. The square/polar family is split into an explicit pairing family, an explicit square family, and the two identities tying them together. The actual simple-head determinant theorem is split into the four manuscript pieces of prop:simpleheaddet: naturality, diagonal vanishing on T, polar vanishing on T x M, and polar-radical containment.

    Manuscript anchors: Section 6 for the square/polar determinant context and Section 7, prop:simpleheaddet, for the simple-head pushout theorem.
