    Route82 minimal-block determinant/pushout closer #

    Route81 leaves eight determinant/pushout atoms:

    • a same-family pairing phi_lambda;
    • an independently chosen square family q_lambda;
    • the square diagonal and polar identities tying q_lambda to phi_lambda;
    • naturality of phi_lambda;
    • diagonal and polar vanishing on T;
    • containment of the symmetrized polar radical in T.

    This sidecar keeps the same downstream determinant-packet surface, but lowers that frontier in the shape of Section 7, prop:simpleheaddet.

    The square family is no longer a residual atom: it is defined theorem-level as Quadratic.Extraspecial.extraspecialForm (phi lambda). The square diagonal and polar identities are then definitional/proven projections. The two vanishing Route81 atoms are replaced by one Annihilates packet for the actual extraspecial square, and the radical-containment atom is replaced by descended nonsingularity of that quotient form.

    Manuscript anchors: Section 6 for the extraspecial square construction and Section 7, prop:simpleheaddet, for annihilation on T and nonsingularity on the simple head.
