    Route83 minimal-block determinant/pushout closer #

    Route82 lowers the determinant/pushout frontier to four packets attached to one abstract pairing Phi:

    • construction of the central-pushout pairing family;
    • naturality of that pairing;
    • annihilation of T by the extraspecial square;
    • nonsingularity after descent to the simple head.

    This sidecar removes those free-standing Route82 residual providers from the route. The new coherent packet is closer to the manuscript proof of prop:simpleheaddet: it starts with the actual scalar central-pushout family and the same-family semantic determinant packet for the induced family. The Route82 packet is then recovered by theorem-level projection. In particular, the naturality/annihilation/nonsingularity fields are not supplied for an arbitrary external Phi; they are tied to the actual scalar-pushout pairing.

    Manuscript anchors: Section 7, pages 41--42, especially the scalar pushouts K_lambda = K / ker lambda, the square map q_lambda, the two vanishing statements on T, and nonsingularity after descent to the simple head.
