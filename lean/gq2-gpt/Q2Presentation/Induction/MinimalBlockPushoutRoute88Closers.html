    Route88 minimal-block determinant/pushout closer #

    This sidecar lowers the seven Route88 determinant/pushout frontier leaves that were still exposed by the Route83 provider:

    • the three central-pushout square/polar split packets minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData, and minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData;
    • the four same-family determinant packets minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData, and minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    The replacement packet is manuscript-shaped: one actual scalar central pushout family supplies the scalar quotients and square/extraspecial comparison, and one actual simple-head determinant theorem for the semantic projection of that same family supplies naturality, vanishing on T, and radical containment.

    Manuscript anchors: Section 7, especially the scalar pushouts K_lambda = K / ker lambda, their square maps q_lambda, and prop:simpleheaddet.
