    Routed lower Section 7 pushout proof packet #

    This sidecar is the route-ready layer above MinimalBlockPushoutLowerProofs. It replaces the seven independent final-closure PREHEAD PUSHOUT / EXTRASPECIAL packets by one coherent same-family residual:

    • a central-pushout self-duality family phi;
    • the simple-head determinant theorem for the extraspecial square form built from that same phi.

    The old square, polar, naturality, diagonal-zero, polar-zero, and radical containment packets are then projections from that one packet.

    Coherent route packet for the Section 7 determinant pushout.

    The determinant field is deliberately indexed by the extraspecial family induced from selfDuality, so every downstream projection uses the same scalar pushout pairing. This is stronger than the previous final route, where the central pushout family, square/polar identities, and extraspecial determinant conditions were seven independent residual axioms.
