    Pushout-side Section 7 semantic proof reductions #

    This sidecar focuses only on the determinant/extraspecial pre-head pushout packets from manuscript Section 7, pages 40-42. It leaves the routed capstone untouched.

    The current semantic route uses five axioms over one central-pushout family:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutNaturalitySemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutDiagonalZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarZeroSemanticData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutRadicalContainmentSemanticData.

    This file gives theorem-level replacements for those five packet boundaries from the narrower standard determinant/extraspecial packets already present in the Section 7 API:

    • the concrete central-pushout quadratic family, square diagonal, and polar symmetry packets;
    • the extraspecial naturality, diagonal-zero, polar-zero, and radical-containment packets.

    The aggregate theorem at the end is the intended replacement hook for the determinant half of minimalBlock_preheadDeterminantPackets_from_semanticClosers.
