    Finer residuals for the Section 7 minimal block #

    This file continues the strengthened Section 7 work. It does not use the retired one-point diagnostic from MinimalBlockExistence.lean, and it does not weaken the MinimalBlock interface. Instead, it proves the exact statement of minimalBlock_of_nonScalar from smaller manuscript-shaped residuals.

    Manuscript anchors: reference/q2_manuscript.tex, Section 7 sec:block:

    • non-scalar chief factor: the paragraph choosing S triangleleft P <= L_Y;
    • lem:simplehead: the exact sequence 0 → T → M → V → 0, nontrivial simple head, T = rad M, and (M^vee)^C = 0;
    • lem:collapse: the Frattini-centralizer collapse giving the elementary central quotient R = Phi(K) and the character space used below;
    • prop:simpleheaddet: the determinant pushout square maps and their annihilation, radical, nonzero, and invariance properties.

    Plan anchor: reference/plan_4turns.md, Stage H, where Section 7 is separated from Section 8 central-cover/Fourier recursion and treated as finite group/module work.
