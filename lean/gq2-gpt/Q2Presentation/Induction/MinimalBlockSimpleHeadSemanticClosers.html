    Semantic simple-head and scalar closers for Section 7 #

    This sidecar owns the simple-head/scalar part of the Section 7 route. It keeps the routed files untouched and replaces the four exposed packets

    • minimalBlock_rawInvariantNonzeroFunctionalsKillTData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadOrbitSpansData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadActionNontrivialData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerNonzeroInvariantCharacterData;

    by smaller manuscript-shaped residuals:

    • invariant hyperplanes cannot miss T;
    • the quotient head has no nonzero proper stable submodule;
    • the quotient-head action is not pointwise trivial;
    • the lower scalar layer has a nonzero invariant character.

    The conversion lemmas below do the remaining linear algebra in Lean.
