    Proper-image restriction actual closers #

    This sidecar attacks the three proper-image semantic residuals from ProperImageRestrictionSemanticClosers.lean without editing the routed files.

    The key point is to keep the restricted maps tied to the actual quotient map T.middleToLower. A single actual middle restriction packet records:

    • the marked restriction L_B -> L_C;
    • surjectivity of that marked restriction;
    • agreement with the ambient quotient map B -> C;
    • the kernel comparison between the marked restriction and the ambient kernel.

    From that semantic packet the three proper-image packets are theorem-level: ker(B -> C) <= L_B, J ∩ L_B maps into L_C, and J ∩ L_B -> L_C is surjective for every proper exact-image subgroup J < B mapping onto C.
