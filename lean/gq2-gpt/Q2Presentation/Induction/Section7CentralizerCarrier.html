    The centralizer carrier for the Frattini-collapse step #

    This file packages the normal subgroup that appears after the Hall--Witt / three-subgroup step of lem:collapse.

    For the odd Hall lift Ñ, the previous file proves [K,Ñ] ≤ C_Y(R), where R = Φ(K). Since K and C_Y(R) are both normal in Y, the normal closure of [K,Ñ] is contained in K ∩ C_Y(R).
