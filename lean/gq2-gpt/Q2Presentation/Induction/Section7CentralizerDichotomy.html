    The chief-dichotomy branch for the Frattini centralizer #

    This file proves the formal branch split used in lem:collapse after the Hall--Witt step. For the normal closure D of [K,Ñ], the subgroup D ⊔ S lies between S and P, hence chief-ness gives D ⊔ S = S or D ⊔ S = P.

    The = P branch is already enough to force K ∩ C_Y(R) = K by the ⊆-minimality of K, hence [K,R]=1. The other branch, D ≤ S, is the remaining representation-theoretic contradiction branch.
