    Concrete §7 tower objects over the ⊆-minimal kernel #

    The literal manuscript objects (eq:targettower, l.3626–3642) for a first non-scalar chief factor with ⊆-minimal kernel K:

    • towerC K := Y ⧸ K — the lower target C (finite group);
    • towerMgrp K := K ⧸ Φ(K) — the Frattini quotient, elementary abelian by the Burnside-basis core (FrattiniQuotientElementary), so towerM K := Additive (towerMgrp K) is a finite ZMod 2-module;
    • towerActM K : towerC K →* (towerM K ≃ₗ[ZMod 2] towerM K) — conjugation, descended twice: through Φ(K) (characteristic) and through K (inner conjugation is trivial mod Φ(K) since commutators lie in Φ(K)).

    Everything here is proved — no axioms. The simple-head facts (T, M/T ≅ V, (M^∨)^C = 0) and the K/R ≃ M bridge are the remaining parts of sec7_rawTowerPacket_exists (subsequent files).
