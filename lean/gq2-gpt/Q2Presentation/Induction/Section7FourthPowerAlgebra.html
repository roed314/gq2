    Class-two algebra for the Section 7 fourth-power step #

    Once R = Phi(K) centralizes K, the commutators of elements of K are central inside K. Together with the fact that squares of K lie in R, this gives the pointwise exponent-two statement for commutators. These are local algebraic ingredients for the manuscript's fourth-power map k |-> k^4.
