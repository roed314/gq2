    Assembly bridge for the Section 7 fourth-power map kernel #

    The preceding files prove all of the tame-witness and lower-branch bookkeeping needed by the fourth-power part of lem:collapse. This file isolates the remaining mathematical construction: for the selected odd Hall witness Ñ, find a normal subgroup of K containing [K, Ñ] on which fourth powers vanish.

    Once that witness-specific kernel is supplied, the theorem below assembles the route-neutral KernelMinimalFourthPowerMapKernelData used by the rest of the corrected Section 7 trunk.
