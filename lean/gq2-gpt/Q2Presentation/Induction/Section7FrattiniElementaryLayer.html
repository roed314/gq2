    The literal Frattini layer is elementary #

    This is the final elementary-layer step in the corrected lem:collapse proof. We work inside the manuscript kernel K and show that the Frattini subgroup is contained in the subgroup generated by squares and commutators. The already-proven fourth-power and commutator-square identities then make every element of Phi(K) square to one.
