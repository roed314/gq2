    Route135 Section 7 kernel closers #

    This sidecar leaves the promoted route files untouched. It targets the three kernel/provenance leaves visible in the current Route135 worker-combined audit:

    • minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteProvenanceData;
    • minimalBlock_rawHeadQuotientNoTrivialData;
    • minimalBlock_rawLowerScalarQuotientData.

    The replacement uses the existing Route106 head closer. That route keeps the manuscript Section 7 tower K, R = Phi(K), M = K/R, T, and C, and rebuilds the quotient-head and lower-scalar packets from the finer raw simple-head semantic data. Candidate/target and pushout/determinant providers are inherited unchanged from Route135.
