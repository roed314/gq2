    Route138 Section 7 kernel closers #

    This sidecar leaves the public presentation route and the existing Route138 file untouched. It targets exactly the Route138 kernel/provenance frontier:

    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastCandidateData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelLeastUniversalData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelFrattiniCollapseFullData;
    • minimalBlock_kernelRawQuotientTowerData;
    • the four Route43 raw simple-head packets.

    The replacement keeps the manuscript Section 7 tower. The least kernel is the upper-chief candidate with selected universal leastness, the Frattini collapse is the Route81 split of R = Phi(K), and the raw quotient tower is assembled from literal Route43 R,M,T,C object/action packets through Route90. The head field is supplied from Route83's same-raw-tower actual invariant packet, so the new integrated theorem does not depend on the four Route43 raw-head leaves.
