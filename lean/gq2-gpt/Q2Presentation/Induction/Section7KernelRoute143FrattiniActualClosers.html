    Route143 Section 7 Frattini actual closers #

    This sidecar leaves the current Route143 file untouched. It supplies a drop-in kernel provider for the Route143 integrated proof that bypasses the fifteen selected least/Frattini Route141 atoms and uses the concrete Section 7 packets instead:

    • minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteLeastData, the selected least K <= P with K S = P;
    • minimalBlock_kernelModuleConcreteFrattiniData, the actual R = Phi(K) collapse packet containing nontriviality, centrality in K, K^4 = 1, and exponent two on R.

    The raw R,M,T,C tower remains routed through the Route141 raw sidecar, so the delta here is isolated to the least/Frattini portion of the kernel provider.
