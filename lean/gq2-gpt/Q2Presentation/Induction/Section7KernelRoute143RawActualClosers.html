    Route143 Section 7 raw actual closers #

    This route-neutral sidecar leaves the current Route143 integrated file untouched. It targets the raw Section 7 kernel atoms exposed there by replacing the Route141 raw object/action split with three same-tower manuscript packets:

    • the literal object tower R = Phi(K), M = K/Phi(K), T, and C = Y/K;
    • the transported actions on that same tower;
    • the simple-head/lower-character packet on the resulting raw tower.

    The route-ready providers below avoid the Route141 namespace-local raw atoms and avoid the three broad raw-head/lower-scalar atoms in the invariant packet.
