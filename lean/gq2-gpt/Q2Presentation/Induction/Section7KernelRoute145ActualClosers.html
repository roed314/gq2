    Route145 Section 7 kernel/raw actual closers #

    This sidecar leaves Route144 untouched. It supplies a replacement kernel provider chain for the Route144 Section 7 kernel route:

    • the broad concrete least packet is lowered to the least-over-chief choice packet;
    • the broad concrete Frattini packet is lowered to the five literal Frattini-collapse fields;
    • the Route143 raw literal object/action aggregates are lowered to the Route43 literal R, M, T, C objects and transported-action pieces;
    • the Route143 raw simple-head aggregate is lowered to the Route43 maximal-submodule, orbit-span, action-witness, and lower-character atoms.

    The exported route theorems are drop-in replacements for the current Route144 kernel provider chain, but no integrated file is edited here.
