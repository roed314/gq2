    Route146 Section 7 kernel raw Route43 closers #

    This route-neutral sidecar leaves Route145 and all integrated files untouched. It supplies Route145-shape replacements for the two raw/head providers whose Route145 axiom closure still exposed the Route43 literal/raw/head atoms.

    The raw quotient tower is assembled from the Route141-owned lower literal object/equivalence/action-family packets. The simple-head semantic packet is assembled from the lower head no-trivial, head-simplicity, head-nontrivial, and lower-scalar quotient packets rather than from the Route43 head atoms.
