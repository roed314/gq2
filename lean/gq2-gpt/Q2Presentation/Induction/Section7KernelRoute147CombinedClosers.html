    Route147 Section 7 combined kernel closers #

    This sidecar leaves the integrated Route147 file and root imports untouched. It packages the Route146 least/Frattini and Route146 raw/head providers into Route147-named kernel providers, and also gives a compact same-tower variant in which the four split raw-head/lower atoms are replaced by the coherent MinimalBlockInvariantActualProofs2Data packet on the selected raw tower.

    The manuscript shape is the Section 7 tower

    • selected least K;
    • R = Phi(K) with Frattini collapse;
    • raw R, M = K/R, T, C objects and actions;
    • a nontrivial simple head and lower invariant character on that same tower.
