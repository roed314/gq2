    Route157 Section 7 kernel/provenance closers #

    This sidecar leaves the integrated route files untouched. It lowers the Route156 kernel/provenance frontier by rebuilding the Route152 kernel bridge from two manuscript-shaped packets already present in the tree:

    • the Route141 Frattini split for the selected least K and R = Phi(K) collapse, replacing the five Route78 selected atoms;
    • the Route143 same-tower raw object/action and simple-head packets, replacing the Route141 raw object/action atoms and the three independent raw invariant facts on the selected raw tower.

    The bridge targets KernelRoute152ProvenanceModuleFacts, then uses Route152's axiom-free bridge to the Route151 package shape consumed by the current kernel route. The replayed finite-count theorem below does not call Section7KernelRoute151Closers.kernelRoute151SelectedPackageData.
