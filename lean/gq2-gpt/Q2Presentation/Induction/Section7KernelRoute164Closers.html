    Route164 Section 7 kernel/provenance closers #

    This sidecar leaves Route163 and Route161 untouched. It supplies a drop-in kernel provider for the Route163 Fourier branch, but routes the Section 7 kernel/provenance work through Route164-owned lower atoms:

    • least K is split into a selected candidate and a universal leastness field;
    • the Frattini collapse is split into normality proved from K.Normal, nonidentity, centralizer-step, fourth-power, and elementary-layer fields;
    • the Route43 literal raw object/action wrappers are split into object, equivalence, action-family, and pointwise-stability packets;
    • the Route43 simple-head atoms are replaced by the direct Route44 semantic head fields on the same raw tower.

    The replayed finite-count theorem below swaps only the kernel provider used by Route163.
