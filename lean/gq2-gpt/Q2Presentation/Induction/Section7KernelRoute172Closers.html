    Route172 Section 7 kernel/raw closers #

    This sidecar leaves all integrated route files untouched. It supplies the same group-tower target as Section7KernelRoute169Closers.minimalBlock_groupTowerData_from_section7KernelRoute169Closers, but replaces the Route169/Route146 raw-head split by the coherent Route143 same-raw-tower actual packet:

    • selected least K and R = Phi(K) still come from the current Route141 Frattini path;
    • the raw R, M = K/R, T, C object/action bridge is selected by the Route143 raw-actual tower packet;
    • the simple-head/lower-character facts are selected together on that raw tower, so the provider does not call the standalone raw-head/lower-scalar atoms or the Route141 raw object/action atoms.
