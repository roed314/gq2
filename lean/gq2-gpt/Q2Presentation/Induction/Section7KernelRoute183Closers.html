    Route183 Section 7 kernel/provenance lowering #

    This sidecar targets the compact Route182 Section 7 kernel frontier:

    • the five selected Route78 least/Frattini packets;
    • the two Route167 raw quotient/simple-head packets.

    No new assumptions are introduced here. The Route78 packets are supplied by the existing Route141/Route175 Frattini lowerings, and the Route167 raw packets are supplied by Route180's Route43 literal object/action/head lowerings.
