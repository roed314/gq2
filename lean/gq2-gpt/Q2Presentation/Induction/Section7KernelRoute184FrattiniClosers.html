    Route184 Section 7 kernel Frattini closers #

    This sidecar changes only the Frattini/provenance half of the Route183 kernel diagnostic. The Route43 raw R,M,T,C object/action/head leaves remain exactly where Route183 exposes them.

    The fourteen Route141 Frattini leaves are bypassed by using the direct Route164 Lemma 7.2 packets:

    • selected least candidate plus universal leastness;
    • a single nonidentity element in the literal embedded Phi(K);
    • the final centralizer step K centralizes Phi(K);
    • the final fourth-power step K^4 = 1;
    • the final elementary Frattini-layer exponent step.

    Those packets assemble the same Route78-selected Frattini interfaces consumed downstream, while avoiding the Route141 section-local leaf names.
