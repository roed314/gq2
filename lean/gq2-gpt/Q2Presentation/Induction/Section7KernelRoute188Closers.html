    Route188 Section 7 kernel/provenance closers #

    Route188 is the compact kernel replay for the current Route187 candidate/target semantic route. It gives named replacements for the seven Route187 kernel leaves:

    • the five Route78 selected least/Frattini packets are routed through the Route184 Lemma 7.2 split;
    • the two Route167 raw quotient/simple-head packets are routed through the Route183/Route180 Route43 raw-object and simple-head split.

    No Route187 file is modified by this sidecar.
