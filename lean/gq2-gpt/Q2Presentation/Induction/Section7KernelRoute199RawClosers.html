    Route199 Section 7 raw-module/head lowering #

    This sidecar keeps the Route198/197 Section 7 kernel shape fixed except for the raw module/head provider. The Route72 least-kernel and Frattini-collapse packets are retained, while the Route180/Route43 raw tower is replaced by the older Route146 raw/head decomposition:

    • Route141 raw object/equivalence/action-family packets assemble the literal R, M, T, and C tower and transported actions;
    • the simple-head fields are obtained from the no-trivial-dual, head simplicity/nontriviality, and lower scalar quotient packets.

    The integrated Route199 audit should therefore remove the eleven MinimalBlockKernelConcreteRoute43.* raw object/action/head atoms exposed by Route198.
