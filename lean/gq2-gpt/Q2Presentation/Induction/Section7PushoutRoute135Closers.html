    Route135 Section 7 pushout/determinant closers #

    This sidecar leaves all routed files untouched. It keeps the current Route135 candidate/target and Section 7 kernel providers, but replaces the Section 7 pushout/determinant provider by the Route82 scalar-pushout packetization.

    The Route82 frontier is closer to the manuscript's prop:simpleheaddet:

    • construction of the scalar-pushout pairing family phi_lambda;
    • naturality of that pairing;
    • annihilation of the extraspecial square on T;
    • descended nonsingularity on the simple head.

    Consequently the current Route135 pushout leaves minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData, minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData, and minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData are avoided by the route-context theorem below.
