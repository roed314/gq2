    Route138 Section 7 pushout/determinant closers #

    This sidecar leaves every routed/public file untouched. Route138 currently uses Section7PushoutRoute135Closers, whose determinant provider lowers the Section 7 pushout line to the four Route82 residual packets:

    • construction of the scalar-pushout pairing family;
    • naturality of that pairing;
    • annihilation of the extraspecial square on T;
    • descended nonsingularity on the simple head.

    Here the same Route138 context is rebuilt with the pushout provider routed through MinimalBlockPushoutRoute83Closers. That provider avoids the four Route82 residual axioms by selecting one actual scalar-pushout family and one same-family coherent semantic determinant packet, matching manuscript Section 7 prop:simpleheaddet.
