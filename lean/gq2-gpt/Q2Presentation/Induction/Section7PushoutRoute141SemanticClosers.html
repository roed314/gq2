    Route141 Section 7 pushout semantic closers #

    This sidecar keeps the public route and all existing Route141 files untouched. The Route141 implementation frontier exposes seven Section 7 pushout/determinant semantic atoms:

    • the central-pushout quadratic family, square-diagonal, and polar-symmetry packets;
    • central-pushout naturality, diagonal-zero, polar-zero, and radical-containment packets.

    The replacement below routes the same downstream determinant provider through the lower extraspecial/self-duality surface already available in MinimalBlockActualCentralExtensionClosers2. Thus the integrated route no longer depends on the seven central-pushout semantic atoms.
