    Route143 Section 7 actual pushout closers #

    This sidecar keeps the public route untouched and replaces only the Route143 Section 7 determinant/pushout provider.

    Route143 currently exposes the five extraspecial/self-duality atoms

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSelfDualityData,
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialNaturalityData,
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialDiagonalZeroData,
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialPolarZeroData, and
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialRadicalContainmentData.

    The replacement below routes the same downstream determinant packet through the Route88 actual-pushout packet already used by Route142. Thus the five Route143 atoms are replaced by the two manuscript-level actual packets:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualScalarCentralPushoutData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.
