    Route146 Section 7 pushout/determinant lowering #

    This sidecar leaves the Route145 integrated file untouched. It exports the same determinant-packet consumer shape as Section7PushoutRoute145ActualClosers.minimalBlock_preheadDeterminantPackets_from_section7PushoutRoute145ActualClosers, but routes the proof through the split actual central-extension packet from MinimalBlockActualCentralExtensionPushoutProofs.

    Route145's pushout/determinant frontier consists of one square/polar atom and four semantic determinant atoms. Route146 replaces those five atoms by the actual scalar central-extension split:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionSquareData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    The projections below show explicitly that the Route145 square/polar and semantic determinant surfaces are theorem-level consequences of that split.
