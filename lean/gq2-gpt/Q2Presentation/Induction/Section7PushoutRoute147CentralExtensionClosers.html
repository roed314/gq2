    Route147 Section 7 actual central-extension closers #

    This sidecar leaves the integrated Route147 file and root imports untouched.

    Route147 currently exposes the Section 7 pushout/determinant boundary as the seven split actual central-extension atoms from MinimalBlockActualCentralExtensionPushoutProofs:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionSquareData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    The route below reassembles those seven packet targets from a smaller manuscript-shaped boundary:

    • the central-pushout quadratic family;
    • its square-diagonal identity;
    • its polar-symmetry identity;
    • the actual simple-head determinant theorem for the selected semantic family.

    This is the Lean shape of the manuscript's Section 7 pushout construction: q_lambda is the scalar central-extension square map on the same phi_lambda family, and prop:simpleheaddet supplies naturality, annihilation on T, and polar-radical containment.
