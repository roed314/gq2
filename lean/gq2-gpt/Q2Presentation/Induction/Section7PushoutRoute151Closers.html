    Route151 Section 7 pushout/determinant closers #

    This sidecar leaves every integrated route and root import untouched.

    Route150 compacted the Section 7 pushout/determinant frontier to four leaves:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutQuadraticFamilyData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquareDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutPolarSymmData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    The providers below lower that compact surface through the Route82 manuscript-shaped scalar pushout API. The square family is the extraspecial square attached to the same phi_lambda pairing, so the diagonal and polar identities are theorem-level projections. The selected simple-head determinant packet is the Route82 prop:simpleheaddet surface: naturality, annihilation on T, and descended nonsingularity/radical containment for the same family.
