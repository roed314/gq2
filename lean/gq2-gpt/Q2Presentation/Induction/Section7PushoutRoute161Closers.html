    Route161 Section 7 pushout/determinant lowering #

    Route160's current pushout/determinant frontier is the seven split actual central-extension packets from MinimalBlockActualCentralExtensionPushoutProofs:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionSquareData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    This sidecar lowers that seven-packet frontier to two same-family manuscript atoms:

    • the scalar central-pushout self-duality family phi_lambda;
    • the simple-head determinant packet for the extraspecial square Quadratic.Extraspecial.extraspecialForm (phi_lambda).

    The bridge does not call Route82 and does not route through the old minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSemanticData leaf. The square, diagonal, and polar actual central-extension packets are definitional from Extraspecial.extraspecialForm; naturality, annihilation, and radical containment are projections of prop:simpleheaddet.

    Manuscript anchors: Section 6 constructs the determinant obstruction of scalar central pushouts, and Section 7, prop:simpleheaddet, proves q_lambda|_T = 0, b_lambda(T,M) = 0, and the descended nonsingular radical statement for K_lambda = K / ker lambda.
