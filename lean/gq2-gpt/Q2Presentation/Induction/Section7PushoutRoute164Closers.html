    Route164 Section 7 pushout/determinant actual-atom lowering #

    Route163's pushout frontier is the two coherent Route161 leaves:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSelfDualityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadExtraspecialSimpleHeadDeterminantData_residual.

    This sidecar lowers them together to actual scalar-pushout atoms. The self-duality family is projected from MinimalBlockPreheadActualScalarCentralPushoutData, and the simple-head determinant packet is projected from MinimalBlockPreheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData for the same actual scalar-pushout family. The exported determinant-packet theorem has the same consumer shape as the Route161 provider.

    Manuscript anchors: Section 7, prop:simpleheaddet, and the scalar pushouts K_lambda = K / ker lambda.
