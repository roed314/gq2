    Route175 Section 7 actual pushout/determinant lowering #

    Route173 reaches the Section 7 pushout/determinant line through Section7PushoutRoute167Closers.minimalBlock_preheadDeterminantPackets_from_section7PushoutRoute167Closers. Its pushout frontier is the seven split actual central-extension packets:

    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPhiData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionSquareData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionDiagonalData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionNaturalityData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionAnnihilationData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionRadicalContainmentData.

    This sidecar exports a drop-in determinant provider with the same downstream consumer shape as the Route167 provider, but routes through the actual semantic central-pushout split from MinimalBlockExtraspecialClosers3.

    Replacement frontier:

    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSquarePolarData;
    • minimalBlock_preheadCentralPushoutSimpleHeadDeterminantActualData.

    This is a strict manuscript-level split of the seven actual central-extension packets: the square/polar construction of the actual scalar pushouts is kept separate from the simple-head determinant theorem for the same semantic family. It is not the broad minimalBlock_preheadActualCentralExtensionPushoutData wrapper used by Route172.
