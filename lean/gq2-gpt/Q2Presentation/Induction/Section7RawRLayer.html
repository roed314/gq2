    The literal Frattini layer as an F_2[C]-module #

    This file supplies the R side of the corrected raw §7 tower packet. The M = K / Phi(K) side and the radical layer T are already built in Section7ConcreteObjects and Section7SimpleHeadRadical. Here we use the proved centrality [K, Phi(K)] = 1 and the exponent-two theorem for Phi(K) to make

    • R = Additive (Phi(K)) a finite ZMod 2-module;
    • conjugation by Y on R descend to an action of C = Y/K.
