    Assembly of the proved raw §7 tower fields #

    This file packages the concrete objects already built over the corrected first chief factor and the selected KernelMinimal:

    • R = Additive Phi(K) with the descended C = Y/K action (Section7RawRLayer);
    • M = Additive (K/Phi(K)), T = (K cap S)/Phi(K), and the descended C-action (Section7ConcreteObjects, Section7SimpleHeadRadical);
    • head nontriviality and (M^vee)^C = 0.

    The remaining field of Sec7RawTowerPacket is the ambient quotient-image bridge Subgroup.map (QuotientGroup.mk' (kernelFrattini K)) K.Ksub ~= Multiplicative M.
