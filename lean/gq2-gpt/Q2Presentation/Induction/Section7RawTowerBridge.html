    The ambient quotient-image bridge for the corrected Section 7 raw tower #

    The concrete module file defines

    • towerMgrp K = K.Ksub ⧸ frattini K.Ksub,
    • towerM K = Additive (towerMgrp K), and
    • kernelFrattini K = Subgroup.map K.Ksub.subtype (frattini K.Ksub).

    The remaining bridge required by Sec7RawTowerPacket is the first-isomorphism-theorem identification between the image of K in the ambient quotient Y / kernelFrattini K and the intrinsic quotient K / frattini K.
