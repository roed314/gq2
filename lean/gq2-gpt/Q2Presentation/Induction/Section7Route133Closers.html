    Route133 Section 7 closers #

    This sidecar leaves the promoted presentation files untouched. It re-routes the current Route133 nonterminal branch through narrower Section 7 providers:

    • kernel/provenance: MinimalBlockInvariantSemanticProofs removes the current direct dual-invariant, raw-head simplicity, and raw invariant-character leaves from the route closure;
    • pushout/determinant: MinimalBlockDeterminantPacketProofs2 removes the four semantic determinant leaves by projecting them from the same-family actual simple-head determinant packet.

    The candidate and target parts are inherited unchanged from IntegratedCurrentRouteProofs133AllRoute130Lowered.
