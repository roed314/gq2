    The radical layer T and head nontriviality (lem:simplehead, part 1) #

    Over the concrete §7 tower (Section7ConcreteObjects): the manuscript's T = (K ∩ S)/R ◁ M (l.3628) as a C-stable submodule of M = K/Φ(K), and the nontriviality of the head M/T:

    • towerT K — the image of K ∩ S in M, as a ZMod 2-submodule;
    • towerT_stable — conjugation stability (S = chief.lower is normal in Y);
    • towerT_ne_top — T ≠ M: otherwise (K∩S) ⊔ Φ(K) = K, so by the Frattini non-generating property K ≤ S, whence K ⊔ S = S < P, contradicting KS = P;
    • head_nontrivial — Nontrivial (M ⧸ T).

    Everything here is proved — no axioms.
