    The three-subgroup step for the Frattini centralizer #

    This is the Hall--Witt/three-subgroups part of lem:collapse.

    For the odd Hall lift Ñ from Section7OddHall.lean:

    • Ñ centralizes R = Φ(K);
    • [K,R] ≤ R, so [K,R] centralizes Ñ;
    • therefore the Three Subgroups Lemma gives [[K,Ñ],R]=1.

    Equivalently, [K,Ñ] ≤ C_Y(R).
