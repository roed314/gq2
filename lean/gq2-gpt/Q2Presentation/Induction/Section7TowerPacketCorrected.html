    • Sec7RawTowerPacket — the raw §7 tower over the ⊆-minimal kernel: the finite 𝔽₂ modules R, M, T, the lower group C, the actions, head nontriviality, dual-invariant vanishing (lem:simplehead), and the literal K/Φ(K) ≃ M bridge.
