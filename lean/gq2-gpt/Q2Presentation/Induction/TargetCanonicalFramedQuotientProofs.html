    Canonical framed quotient target constructor #

    This sidecar refines the remaining Section 8 target packet centralCover_targetCanonicalFramedQuotientData.

    The existing subgroup packet already gives the canonical normal subgroups R <= K <= L_Y, so the tame quotient map piY factors through both quotients Y/R and Y/K. The missing semantic input is the manuscript decoration-block fact (lem:decorationblock): the boundary decoration thetaY vanishes on K. Once that is supplied, the framed quotient targets are canonical, not arbitrary children:

    • middle child: Y/R, with marked kernel the image of L_Y;
    • lower child: Y/K, with marked kernel the image of L_Y;
    • frame: the same (alpha, psi, beta), since H and E are unchanged.
