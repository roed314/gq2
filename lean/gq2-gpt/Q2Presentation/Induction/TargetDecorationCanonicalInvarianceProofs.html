    This sidecar refines the remaining target-decoration residual centralCover_targetDecorationCanonicalInvarianceData.

    Manuscript anchor: §7 lem:decorationblock and §8 lem:covertransform. After TargetDecorationActualDescentProofs proves that the decoration kills R = Phi(K), the descended decoration on K/R is invariant for the actual quotient-conjugation action because thetaY is a homomorphism into an abelian target.
