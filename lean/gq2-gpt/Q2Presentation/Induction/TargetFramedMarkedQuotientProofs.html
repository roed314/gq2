    Target framed marked-quotient proofs #

    This sidecar closes the routed centralCover_targetFramedMarkedQuotientData packet down to the strictly smaller manuscript packet centralCover_targetCanonicalFramedQuotientData.

    The narrower packet already says that the quotient children realizing Y/R and Y/K carry the inherited marked kernels:

    • L_B is the image of L_Y in Y/R;
    • L_C is the image of L_Y in Y/K.

    From those image equalities, the ambient quotient maps restrict surjectively to the marked kernels, and the middle ambient kernel is marked because it is K/R and K <= L_Y.
