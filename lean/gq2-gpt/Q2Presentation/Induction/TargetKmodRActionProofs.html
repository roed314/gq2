    Target K/R action compatibility #

    This sidecar refines the residual centralCover_targetKmodRActionCompatibilityData.

    Manuscript anchor: §7 eq:targettower and §8 lem:decorationblock / lem:covertransform. The selected abstract lower group B.C should be the actual quotient Y/K, and the selected action B.actM on the elementary module M = K/R should be the quotient action induced by right conjugation in Y.

    The packet below is closer to that concrete tower than an arbitrary family of representatives: it supplies an equivalence Y/K ~= B.C and a pointwise equation for every ambient representative y : Y. Representatives for the older packet are then chosen from the quotient map.
