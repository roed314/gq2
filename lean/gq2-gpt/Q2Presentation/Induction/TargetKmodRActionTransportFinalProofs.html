    Final transport packets for the target K/R action #

    This sidecar further decomposes the two residuals introduced by TargetKmodRQuotientActionFinalClosers:

    • centralCover_targetLowerQuotientEquivData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRActionTransportData.

    The replacement packets are tied directly to the manuscript tower Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K and to conjugation on the literal quotient K/R:

    • a concrete projection Y -> B.C with kernel K;
    • compatibility of any selected Y/K ~= B.C equivalence with that projection;
    • the assertion that the projected B.C-action on M is conjugation on K/R.
