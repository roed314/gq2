    Semantic closers for the target Y/K projection and K/R action #

    This sidecar refines the two target-side packets left by TargetKmodRActionTransportFinalProofs:

    • centralCover_targetLowerProjectionData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRProjectedConjugationActionData.

    The lower projection is theorem-level once the canonical lower quotient map Y -> lowerChild and an equivalence from that lower child to the abstract action group B.C are supplied. The action packet is split into a representative-level conjugation law and the assertion that conjugation on K/R depends only on the lower quotient class.
