    Final target K/R quotient-action closers #

    This sidecar further decomposes the current final-closure residual centralCover_targetKmodRQuotientActionData.

    Manuscript anchors:

    • §7, eq:targettower: B = Y/R, C = Y/K, and M = K/R;
    • §8, lem:decorationblock: the abstract C-action on M must be the right-conjugation action on the concrete quotient K/R.

    The current concrete target packet already carries R <= K <= L_Y and K/R ~= M. What it does not yet expose is:

    • that the abstract lower action group B.C is the concrete quotient Y/K;
    • that B.actM, transported across K/R ~= M, is right conjugation by representatives in Y.

    Those are recorded below as two smaller residuals and then reassembled into the existing quotient-action packet consumed by TargetKmodRActionProofs.
