    Route101 target K/R projection/action semantic lowering #

    Route100's target provider uses Route99, whose axiom frontier is the two transport-final packets

    • centralCover_targetLowerProjectionData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRProjectedConjugationActionData.

    This sidecar is a drop-in Route101 provider which routes the decoration-kernel closure through TargetKmodRProjectionSemanticClosers. The target frontier is therefore lowered to the four smaller manuscript-shaped semantic packets:

    • centralCover_targetLowerQuotientMapData;
    • centralCover_targetLowerActionEquivData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRRepresentativeActionData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRConjugationClassIndependenceData.

    No Route48 or Route99 coherent projection/action atom is used by the providers below.
