    Route102 target K/R semantic lowering #

    Route101 exposed four target-side projection/action residuals:

    • centralCover_targetLowerQuotientMapData;
    • centralCover_targetLowerActionEquivData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRRepresentativeActionData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRConjugationClassIndependenceData.

    This sidecar removes the lower quotient-map residual by using Route34's literal Y/K lower child, removes the lower-action residual by using the literal same-tower equivalence Y/K ~= B.C, and closes class-independence from the already theorem-level inner-conjugation bridge on K/R.

    The only target semantic residual added here is a smaller literal representative-action packet for the Route34 lower child: representatives are the canonical quotient representatives of elements of Y/K, not arbitrary data in the packet.
