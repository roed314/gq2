    Route103 target K/R literal quotient-action lowering #

    Route102 leaves two target residuals:

    • the literal quotient identification Y/K ~= B.C, packaged as the same-tower lower quotient equivalence; and
    • the action law for the canonical representatives of literal Y/K classes.

    This sidecar keeps the Route102 downstream API but lowers those two names to packets stated directly on the manuscript tower

    Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K, M = K/R.

    The lower-side residual is now the Route28/Route34 lower-action equivalence for the already theorem-level lower child C = Y/K. The action residual is stated as well-defined quotient action: every representative of a literal class acts on K/R by the transported B.C action. Route102's chosen-representative packet is recovered theorem-level by applying this action law to its chosen quotient representative.
