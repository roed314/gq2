    Route106 target K/R literal quotient-action lowering #

    Route103 left two target-side non-local packets:

    • TargetKmodRRoute103Closers.centralCover_targetRoute103LowerActionEquivData;
    • TargetKmodRRoute103Closers.centralCover_targetRoute103LiteralQuotientActionData.

    This sidecar avoids both Route103 axiom names in the downstream decoration-kernel provider. The literal lower quotient is still Route34's theorem-level child Y/K. The lower-action equivalence is exposed as Route28's lower-action packet for that child, and the all-representatives literal quotient action is recovered theorem-level from Route28's selected-representative action packet plus the already closed inner-conjugation bridge on K/R.

    Axiom frontier effect for the target branch:

    • replaces the Route103 lower-action axiom by TargetKmodRRoute28BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute28LowerActionEquivData instantiated at Route34's literal quotient map;
    • replaces the Route103 all-representatives action axiom by TargetKmodRRoute28BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute28RepresentativeActionData for the induced lower projection and its theorem-level choice section.
