    Route127 target K/R closers #

    Route127's public target branch still uses TargetKmodRRoute45BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute45LiteralSelectedActionData. The Route126 sidecar shows that the same downstream decoration-kernel consumer can instead be routed through the two Route28 atoms for the literal quotient C = Y/K and the action on M = K/R.

    This file keeps both viewpoints available:

    • centralCover_targetRoute45LiteralSelectedActionData_from_route127_via_route28 reconstructs the public Route45 selected-action packet from the two Route28 atoms, with the quotient map supplied theorem-level by Route34.
    • centralCover_targetRoute45LiteralSelectedActionData_from_route127 lowers the same Route45 packet further to Route102's stricter same-tower residuals: the canonical quotient equivalence Y/K ~= B.C and the literal representative action law.
    • centralCover_targetDecorationKernelData_from_route127 is the requested drop-in provider for downstream consumers.

    Manuscript anchors: §7 eq:targettower, with C = Y/K and M = K/R; §8 exact-image lower maps and central-cover transforms, where selected representatives of lower classes realize the transported C-action on K/R.
