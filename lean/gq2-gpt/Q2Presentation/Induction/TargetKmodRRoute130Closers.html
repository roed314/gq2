    Route130 target K/R closers #

    Route130's public target branch routes through TargetKmodRRoute127Closers.centralCover_targetDecorationKernelData_from_route127, whose target frontier contains two Route102/same-tower packets:

    • TargetKmodRSameTowerActualClosers.centralCover_targetCanonicalLowerQuotientEquivData;
    • TargetKmodRRoute102SemanticClosers.centralCover_targetRoute102LiteralRepresentativeActionData.

    This sidecar attacks those leaves without promoting any root file. The lower quotient equivalence is rebuilt from Route28's lower-action packet for Route34's literal quotient child Y/K. The Route102 literal representative action packet is rebuilt, for any supplied quotient equivalence, from Route28's selected-representative action packet plus the theorem-level inner-conjugation bridge on K/R.

    Manuscript anchors: §7 eq:targettower, with C = Y/K and M = K/R; §8 exact-image lower maps and central-cover transforms, where representative choices inside a Y/K coset are immaterial on K/R.
