    Route138 target K/R closers #

    This sidecar leaves the routed/public files untouched. It keeps the Route138 candidate and Section 7 providers, but replaces the target Route22 leaves by the lower transport-final K/R bridge atoms:

    • centralCover_targetLowerProjectionData, the concrete projection Y -> B.C with kernel K;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRProjectedConjugationActionData, the projected conjugation action on the literal quotient K/R.

    These are the manuscript-shaped §7--§8 tower facts for Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K and M = K/R.
