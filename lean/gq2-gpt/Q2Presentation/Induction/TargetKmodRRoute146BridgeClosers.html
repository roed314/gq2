    Route146 same-tower target bridge closers #

    Route145's target side leaves two same-tower bridge residuals:

    • TargetKmodRSameTowerBridgeClosers.centralCover_targetLowerProjectionBridgeData;
    • TargetKmodRSameTowerBridgeClosers.centralCover_targetKmodRRepresentativeActionBridgeData.

    This sidecar keeps the same downstream decoration-kernel shape as TargetKmodRRoute145SameTowerClosers.centralCover_targetDecorationKernelData_from_route145SameTower, but replaces those bridge residuals by lower quotient/action atoms:

    • the manuscript lower target is the literal quotient C = Y/K, identified with the abstract action group B.C;
    • the B.C action is checked on theorem-level representatives of literal Y/K classes.

    The projection Y -> B.C, its kernel, representatives over B.C, and the extension to the Route145 bridge-data surface are theorem-level quotient bookkeeping. This is a strict lowering, not a full target closure.
