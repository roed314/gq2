    Route146 same-tower target pointwise bridge closers #

    Route145 lowers the target same-tower frontier to two bridge residuals:

    • a lower projection bridge Y -> B.C with kernel K;
    • a single-representative action bridge for K/R.

    This Route146 sidecar keeps the same downstream quotient-action and decoration-kernel shapes, but decomposes those bridge residuals into smaller manuscript-shaped atoms: a raw projection map, pointwise fibres for surjectivity, two kernel inclusions, a representative selector, pointwise section equalities, and pointwise representative-action equalities.
