    Route147 same-tower target pointwise closers #

    Route147 currently uses the Route146 pointwise target atoms:

    • a raw lower projection map, fibres, and two kernel inclusions;
    • a representative selector, pointwise section equalities, and pointwise representative-action equalities.

    This sidecar ties those pointwise packets to the literal same-tower quotient semantics already isolated in TargetKmodRRoute146BridgeClosers: the lower target is Y/K, identified with B.C, and representatives are the theorem-level representatives of literal Y/K classes. Six pointwise atoms are therefore theorem-level from the quotient equivalence; the action pointwise atom is reduced to the literal representative-action atom.
