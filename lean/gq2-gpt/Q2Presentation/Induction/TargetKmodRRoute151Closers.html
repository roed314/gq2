    Route151 target K/R quotient/action closers #

    Route150 leaves two compact target-side atoms from TargetKmodRRoute146BridgeClosers:

    • centralCover_targetRoute146LowerQuotientEquivData;
    • centralCover_targetRoute146LiteralRepresentativeActionData.

    This sidecar lowers that pair to concrete Section 8 quotient/action compatibility facts:

    • a lower projection Y -> B.C;
    • pointwise lower-projection fibres and the two kernel inclusions identifying the kernel with K;
    • compatibility between a selected Y/K ~= B.C equivalence and the projection;
    • the projected B.C-action on M = K/R as quotient conjugation.

    The exported providers have the same theorem-level target surfaces as the two Route150 leaves, but their axiom dependencies are the finer Route151 residuals.
