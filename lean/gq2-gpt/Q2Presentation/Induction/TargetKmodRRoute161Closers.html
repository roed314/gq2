    Route161 target K/R quotient/action closers #

    Route160's target branch uses Route157's drop-in decoration-kernel provider, whose target frontier is the five Route151 lower projection/action leaves:

    • a selected lower projection map Y -> B.C;
    • pointwise fibres of that projection;
    • the two kernel inclusions identifying its kernel with K;
    • the all-ambient projected action law on K/R.

    This sidecar keeps the same downstream Route157-shaped conclusion but replaces those leaves by more concrete Route161 packets:

    • a raw projection function and its monoid-hom laws;
    • pointwise fibre witnesses split from their proof equations;
    • pointwise tests for K-membership and projection-triviality;
    • a representative selector, its section law, and representative-level action.

    The all-ambient action law is then theorem-level from the existing closed fact that inner conjugation by K is trivial on K/R.
