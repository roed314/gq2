    Route167 target K/R quotient/action closers #

    Route166's target frontier contains the four coherent Route164 projection and representative-action atoms. This sidecar replaces that Route164 packet by the lower manuscript-shaped projection/action bridge already isolated in TargetKmodRProjectionSemanticClosers:

    • a literal lower quotient map Y -> Y/K;
    • an equivalence from that lower child to the abstract action group B.C;
    • representative-level action on K/R;
    • class-independence of quotient conjugation on K/R.

    The Route164 surface is rebuilt theorem-level from those lower packets only so the downstream decoration-kernel provider is a drop-in replacement for TargetKmodRRoute164Closers.centralCover_targetDecorationKernelData_from_route152Closers_route164 without depending on any Route164 residual declarations.
