    Route17 target K/R bridge proofs #

    This sidecar keeps route16's target bridge surface but supplies theorem-level providers for the two same-tower bridge leaves:

    • centralCover_targetLowerProjectionBridgeData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRRepresentativeActionBridgeData.

    The lower projection bridge is assembled from the projection-semantic lower quotient map and lower-action equivalence. The representative bridge is assembled directly from the representative-level action packet. Thus the route17 quotient-action theorem avoids the two route16 bridge residual names and reduces them to strictly narrower manuscript-shaped packets.
