    Route18 target projection-semantic sidecar #

    Route17 exposed three target K/R projection-semantic leaves:

    • centralCover_targetLowerQuotientMapData;
    • centralCover_targetLowerActionEquivData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRRepresentativeActionData.

    This sidecar gives a route18 target hook whose primary path avoids those route17 leaves. It uses the more concrete same-tower provenance:

    • the literal lower quotient Y/K is equivalent to the abstract action group B.C;
    • the B.C action on M = K/R is realized by ambient conjugation on the literal quotient K/R.

    After the route18 decoration-kernel theorem is available, the old lower-child quotient-map shape is recovered as theorem-level framed-quotient bookkeeping.
