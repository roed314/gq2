    Route211 target bridge lowering #

    This sidecar is a Route210-compatible target provider that does not use the two same-tower bridge residual axioms

    • TargetKmodRSameTowerBridgeClosers.centralCover_targetLowerProjectionBridgeData,
    • TargetKmodRSameTowerBridgeClosers.centralCover_targetKmodRRepresentativeActionBridgeData.

    Instead it routes the target K/R decoration-kernel packet through the already available Route146 literal same-tower quotient/action surface:

    • TargetKmodRRoute146BridgeClosers.centralCover_targetRoute146LowerQuotientEquivData,
    • TargetKmodRRoute146BridgeClosers.centralCover_targetRoute146LiteralRepresentativeActionData.

    The projection Y → B.C, its kernel, the selected representatives, and the upgrade to quotient-action data are theorem-level reassembly from that literal quotient/action surface.
