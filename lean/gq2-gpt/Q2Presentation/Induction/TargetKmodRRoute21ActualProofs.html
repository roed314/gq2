    Route21 actual bridge for the target K/R tower #

    Route20 exposed the two remaining target same-tower facts as broad packets:

    • identify the abstract lower action group B.C with the literal quotient Y/K;
    • prove that the abstract action B.actM is conjugation-induced on K/R.

    This sidecar keeps the same downstream route20 surface available, but replaces those two broad assumptions by a stricter manuscript-shaped bridge:

    • a concrete projection Y -> B.C with kernel exactly K, hence an explicit first-isomorphism-theorem identification Y/K ~= B.C;
    • one representative in each lower class whose action realizes conjugation on K/R.

    The upgrade from selected representatives to all ambient representatives is theorem-level: TargetKmodRInnerConjugationClosed already proves inner conjugation by K is trivial on K/R from the realized bridge K/R ~= M.
