    Route26 target K/R bridge sidecar #

    Route25's target decoration-kernel provider routes through the two bridge packets in TargetKmodRSameTowerBridgeClosers:

    • centralCover_targetLowerProjectionBridgeData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRRepresentativeActionBridgeData.

    This sidecar keeps the same downstream provider shape as route25, but avoids both route25 bridge residual names. The lower bridge is rebuilt from the literal same-tower quotient Y/K ~= B.C. The representative bridge is split into a theorem-level selected section of the canonical lower projection and a single narrower packet asserting that this selected section realizes the B.C-action on K/R.
