    Route27 target K/R bridge sidecar #

    Route26 leaves two target-side facts in the closure:

    • TargetKmodRSameTowerActualClosers.centralCover_targetCanonicalLowerQuotientEquivData;
    • TargetKmodRRoute26BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute26RepresentativeSectionActionData.

    This sidecar does not fall back to either name. It replaces them by the manuscript-shaped projection/action provenance for the tower

    Y -> B.C = Y/K, with M = K/R:

    • a concrete projection Y -> B.C whose kernel is the selected subgroup K;
    • compatibility of the abstract action B.actM with ambient conjugation through that projection.

    The first-isomorphism-theorem quotient equivalence, the route26 selected-section action packet for the induced section, and the downstream decoration-kernel provider are all rebuilt theorem-level from these two route27 packets.
