    Route28 target K/R bridge sidecar #

    Route27 leaves two broad target-side packets:

    • TargetKmodRRoute27BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute27LowerProjectionData;
    • TargetKmodRRoute27BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute27ProjectionActionData.

    This sidecar replaces those names by finer manuscript-shaped packets. The lower projection is split into the concrete quotient map Y -> C = Y/K and the identification of that lower child with the abstract action group B.C. The action law is required only for one selected representative of each lower class; the all-representatives route27 action is then recovered theorem-level from the already closed inner-conjugation triviality on K/R.
