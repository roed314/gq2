    Route30 target K/R bridge sidecar #

    Route29's target decoration-kernel provider closes through the route26 selected canonical-section action packet and the literal same-tower lower quotient equivalence. This sidecar keeps the same downstream decoration-kernel surface but bypasses both route29 residual names.

    The lower side is routed through the concrete projection-semantic split:

    • a framed lower quotient map Y -> C = Y/K;
    • an equivalence from that concrete lower child to the abstract action group B.C.

    The action side is routed through the all-ambient-representatives law on K/R; restricting that law to the chosen lower projection gives the projected conjugation action directly, so no selected-section residual is needed in the exported provider.
