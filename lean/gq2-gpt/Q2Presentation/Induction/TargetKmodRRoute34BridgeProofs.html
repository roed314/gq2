    Route34 target K/R bridge sidecar #

    Route33's target branch goes through route31, whose remaining non-local target frontier is the three route28 packets:

    • centralCover_targetRoute28LowerQuotientMapData;
    • centralCover_targetRoute28LowerActionEquivData;
    • centralCover_targetRoute28RepresentativeActionData.

    This sidecar closes the first of those at the current interface level. The lower quotient group is the literal quotient Y/K; for the FramedPair carrier we keep the original tame quotient and attach a trivial elementary decoration. The route28 packet only uses the carrier group and the quotient map, so no decoration-kernel circularity is introduced.

    The exported provider has the same downstream shape as route31, but its axiom closure no longer contains TargetKmodRRoute28BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute28LowerQuotientMapData.
