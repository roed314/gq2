    Route35 target K/R bridge sidecar #

    Route34 closes the concrete lower quotient map Y -> Y/K, but its target decoration-kernel provider still depends on the two remaining route28 target packets:

    • centralCover_targetRoute28LowerActionEquivData;
    • centralCover_targetRoute28RepresentativeActionData.

    This sidecar keeps route34's literal lower child and replaces those two route28 residual names by the already separated same-tower semantics:

    • the lower action equivalence is the canonical same-tower equivalence Y/K ~= B.C;
    • selected representatives realize the action because the all-ambient same-tower action law holds for every representative.

    The exported provider has the same usable downstream shape as route34 while its target axiom closure contains no route28 lower-action or representative-action residuals.
