    Route38 target K/R bridge sidecar #

    Route37 lowered the target bridge to the literal quotient Y/K and the action of theorem-level representatives of classes in that quotient. This sidecar keeps the same downstream provider, but removes the two Route37 residual names from its closure.

    The remaining Route38 packets are the manuscript-shaped quotient-action facts:

    • a concrete projection Y -> B.C whose kernel is K, i.e. the abstract lower action group is reached from the literal lower target C = Y/K;
    • the projected action law saying that the B.C-action on M = K/R is actual right conjugation on the literal quotient K/R.

    This does not honestly close the lower quotient identification, because MinimalBlock.C is still an abstract field. Route38 records the needed provenance as a concrete projection, then derives the Route37 literal quotient equivalence by the first isomorphism theorem.
