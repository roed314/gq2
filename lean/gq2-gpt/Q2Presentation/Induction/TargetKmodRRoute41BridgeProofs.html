    Route41 target K/R bridge sidecar #

    Route40 leaves two target-side Section 8 K/R bridge residuals in the active provider:

    • TargetKmodRRoute39BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute39LowerQuotientIdentificationData;
    • TargetKmodRRoute40BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute40SelectedRepresentativeActionData.

    This sidecar removes those two residual names from the target decoration-kernel provider without changing downstream interfaces. The lower quotient identification is supplied from the canonical same-tower quotient packet Y/K ~= B.C. The selected representatives are then chosen theorem-level from the induced canonical lower projection, and the only action-side residual used is the narrower Route26 section-action packet.

    Manuscript anchors:

    • §7, eq:targettower (pp. 40--42): Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K and M = K/R;
    • §8 (pp. 42--49): exact-image lower maps use selected lower-target representatives, with same-coset independence supplied theorem-level by the realized K/R ~= M bridge.
