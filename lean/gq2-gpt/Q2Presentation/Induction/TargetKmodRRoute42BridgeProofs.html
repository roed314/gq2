    Route42 target K/R bridge sidecar #

    Route41's target provider removes the Route39/Route40 bridge names, but its axiom closure still contains two target-side leaves:

    • TargetKmodRSameTowerActualClosers.centralCover_targetCanonicalLowerQuotientEquivData;
    • TargetKmodRRoute26BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute26RepresentativeSectionActionData.

    This sidecar replaces those two leaves by the direct manuscript quotient-action surface:

    • a concrete projection Y -> B.C whose kernel is the selected subgroup K;
    • the projected action law saying that the B.C-action on M = K/R is literal right conjugation on K/R.

    The first-isomorphism-theorem quotient equivalence and the Route26 selected section action packet are both recovered theorem-level from these Route42 packets. The exported provider has the same downstream target shape as TargetKmodRRoute41BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetDecorationKernelData_from_route41.

    Manuscript anchors:

    • §7, eq:targettower (pp. 40--42): Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K and M = K/R;
    • §8 (pp. 42--49): exact-image lower maps and central-cover transforms use the transported C-action on K/R.
