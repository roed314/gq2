    Route43 target K/R bridge sidecar #

    Route42 leaves two target-side packets:

    • a projection Y -> B.C with kernel K;
    • the all-ambient projected conjugation action law for that projection.

    This sidecar refines those packets to the literal same-tower data from the manuscript:

    • an identification of the lower action group with the quotient Y/K;
    • a selected section of Y/K whose representatives realize the action on K/R.

    The projection, its kernel, and the extension from selected representatives to all ambient representatives are then theorem-level. The last step uses the already closed fact that inner conjugation by elements of K is trivial on K/R.

    Manuscript anchors:

    • §7, eq:targettower (pp. 40--42): Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K and M = K/R;
    • §8 (pp. 42--49): exact-image lower maps and central-cover transforms use selected lower-target representatives, with same-coset independence on K/R.
