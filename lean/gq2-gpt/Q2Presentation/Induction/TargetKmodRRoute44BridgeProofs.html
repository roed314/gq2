    Route44 target K/R bridge cleanup #

    Route43 leaves two target-side non-local packets:

    • a literal lower quotient identification Y/K ~= B.C;
    • a selected representative section of Y/K whose representatives realize the B.C-action on K/R.

    This sidecar reduces both packets against the literal quotient/tower APIs. Route34 already constructs the lower quotient map Y -> Y/K theorem-level, so Route44 leaves only the identification of that literal lower child with the abstract action group B.C. Likewise, the representative section is chosen theorem-level from the induced projection; Route44 leaves only the action law for that fixed section.

    Manuscript anchors:

    • §7, eq:targettower: Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K and M = K/R;
    • §8 target quotient semantics: exact-image lower maps use the literal lower quotient and selected representatives, with same-coset independence supplied by the closed inner-conjugation theorem for K/R.
