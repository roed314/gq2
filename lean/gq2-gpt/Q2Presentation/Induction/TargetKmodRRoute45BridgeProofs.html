    Route45 target K/R literal lower-action bridge #

    Route44 leaves two target-side packets:

    • a literal lower action equivalence from Route34's child Y/K to B.C;
    • the action law for Route44's theorem-level selected representatives.

    This sidecar replaces those two names by one coherent literal-tower packet. It supplies the Y/K ~= B.C identification together with a selected section of the literal lower child whose representatives realize the B.C action on K/R. Route44's fixed theorem-level representative section is recovered by same-coset independence, using the already closed inner-conjugation theorem for K/R.

    Manuscript anchors:

    • §7, eq:targettower: Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K and M = K/R;
    • §8 exact-image lower maps and central-cover transforms: representative choices in the lower quotient are immaterial because changing representatives inside a Y/K coset acts trivially on K/R.
