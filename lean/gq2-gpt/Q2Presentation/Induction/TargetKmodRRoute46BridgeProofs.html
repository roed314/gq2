    Route46 target K/R canonical ambient-action bridge #

    Route45 leaves one coherent target-side packet:

    • a literal lower quotient equivalence from Route34's Y/K child to B.C;
    • a selected section of that literal quotient;
    • the selected-representative action law on K/R.

    This Route46 sidecar removes that selected-section packet from the target provider closure. It constructs the section theorem-level from the quotient map Y -> Y/K, and obtains the action law by restricting the older all-ambient action packet to those representatives.

    The remaining residuals are the sharper manuscript-shaped facts already exposed by TargetKmodRSameTowerActualClosers:

    • the canonical same-tower quotient equivalence Y/K ~= B.C;
    • the all-ambient assertion that the B.C-action on M = K/R is conjugation on the literal quotient K/R.
