    Route48 target K/R coherent projection/action bridge #

    Route46's active target hook still depends on two broad actual same-tower leaves:

    • TargetKmodRSameTowerActualClosers.centralCover_targetCanonicalLowerQuotientEquivData;
    • TargetKmodRSameTowerActualClosers.centralCover_targetKmodRAmbientActionData.

    This Route48 sidecar does not route the older Route47 literal quotient sidecar. Instead it exposes one coherent same-tower target packet:

    • a concrete lower projection Y -> B.C with kernel exactly K;
    • the B.C-action on M = K/R, checked on the theorem-level representatives chosen from that same projection.

    The quotient equivalence Y/K ~= B.C is then first-isomorphism-theorem bookkeeping, and the upgrade from selected representatives to all ambient representatives uses the already closed inner-conjugation theorem for K/R.
