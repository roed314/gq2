    Route50 target K/R projection-action bridge #

    Route48 leaves one coherent target packet:

    • TargetKmodRRoute48BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute48ProjectionActionData.

    This sidecar removes that broad packet from the exported Route50 providers. The Route48 structure is rebuilt from the more literal Route37 packets:

    • an equivalence between the manuscript lower quotient Y/K and the abstract action group B.C;
    • the representative action law for theorem-level representatives of literal Y/K classes.

    The lower projection Y -> B.C, its kernel, representatives, and same-coset transport on K/R are theorem-level from quotient algebra plus the closed inner-conjugation theorem.
