    Route52 target K/R coherent literal bridge #

    Route51's target path uses Route50, whose closure exposes the two Route37 literal target leaves:

    • an equivalence between the literal quotient Y/K and the abstract action group B.C;
    • the representative action law for that selected equivalence.

    This sidecar narrows that pair to one coherent packet. Instead of asking for an arbitrary lower equivalence and then an action law for every such equivalence, Route52 asks for one literal lower equivalence together with the action law for the theorem-level representatives attached to that same equivalence. The quotient projection, kernel statement, representative bridge, Route38-style projected action packet, and Route50/Route48 provider surfaces are then reassembled theorem-level.
