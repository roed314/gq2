    Route54 target K/R canonical selected-action bridge #

    Route53 still exposes the Route45 target residual

    • TargetKmodRRoute45BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute45LiteralSelectedActionData.

    This sidecar narrows that residual to one canonical selected-tower packet. The Route54 packet is tied directly to the manuscript tower Y -> C = Y/K and M = K/R: it supplies a concrete lower projection Y -> B.C with kernel K, selected representatives for that projection, and the action law for those same representatives. The literal Route45 Y/K-indexed selected-section packet is then recovered theorem-level from the projection-induced quotient equivalence.

    Route54 canonical selected-action data.

    This is narrower than Route45's literal selected-section packet in the sense used by the current route: the quotient identification is not an independent field over an auxiliary lower child. It is induced by the concrete lower projection whose kernel is the selected subgroup K, and the selected action law is stated for representatives of that same projection.
