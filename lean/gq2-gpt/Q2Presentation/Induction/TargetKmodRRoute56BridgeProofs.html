    Route56 target K/R literal-tower bridge for the Route54 hook #

    The current Route54 target provider exposes two same-tower bridge residuals:

    • TargetKmodRSameTowerBridgeClosers.centralCover_targetLowerProjectionBridgeData;
    • TargetKmodRSameTowerBridgeClosers.centralCover_targetKmodRRepresentativeActionBridgeData.

    This sidecar does not edit the integrated route. It gives a theorem-level Route54-compatible provider whose remaining target facts are Route39's literal manuscript tower packets:

    • identify the lower action group with the quotient Y/K;
    • prove the K/R action law for the theorem-level representative of each literal Y/K class.

    Thus the projection to B.C, the kernel statement, and the chosen representatives consumed by Route54 are rebuilt theorem-level from the literal quotient map Y -> Y/K.
