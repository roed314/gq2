    Route57 target K/R coherent projection/action bridge #

    Route56 exposes two target Section 8 Route39 leaves:

    • identify the abstract lower action group with the literal quotient Y/K;
    • prove the K/R action law for the theorem-level representatives of that literal quotient.

    This sidecar leaves the routed files untouched and replaces that pair, for a new provider chain, by one coherent manuscript-shaped packet: a concrete lower projection Y -> B.C with kernel K, together with the projected conjugation action law for that same projection. The quotient equivalence Y/K ~= B.C, the Route39 literal representative law, and the Route56-compatible decoration provider are then reassembled theorem-level.
