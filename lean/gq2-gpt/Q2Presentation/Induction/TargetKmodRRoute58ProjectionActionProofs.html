    Route58 target K/R projection/action refinement #

    Route57 leaves one coherent target-side Section 8 residual:

    • TargetKmodRRoute57BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute57ProjectionActionData.

    This sidecar keeps the active integration untouched and refines that residual into smaller packets matching the manuscript tower

    Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K, with M = K/R.

    The split is deliberately not routed back through the older Route39/Route41/ Route26 selected-representative leaves, nor through the same-tower lower-level residual names. It records:

    • the lower target map Y -> C and its surjectivity;
    • the kernel identification ker(Y -> C) = K;
    • the projected C-action law on the concrete quotient K/R.

    These three packets reassemble theorem-level into Route57's coherent projection/action data and into the Route57-compatible decoration providers.
