    Route60 target projection/action closers #

    Route58 split the target Section 8 surface into three packets: the lower target map Y -> C, its kernel, and the projected K/R action law. This sidecar lowers that split to the manuscript same-tower packets:

    • B.C is identified with the literal quotient Y/K;
    • the lower projection is induced by Y -> Y/K;
    • the B.C action on M = K/R is ambient quotient conjugation.

    The exported provider reassembles the Route58/Route57 downstream surfaces without using the three Route58 residual axioms.
