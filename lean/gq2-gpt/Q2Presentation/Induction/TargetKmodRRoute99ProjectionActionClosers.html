    Route99 target K/R projection/action lowering #

    Route98's target provider routes through Route63 and leaves one coherent Route48 projection/action atom:

    • TargetKmodRRoute48BridgeProofs.centralCover_targetRoute48ProjectionActionData.

    This sidecar lowers that single coherent atom to the two transport-final manuscript packets already isolated in TargetKmodRActionTransportFinalProofs:

    • centralCover_targetLowerProjectionData, a concrete projection Y -> B.C whose kernel is the selected subgroup K;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRProjectedConjugationActionData, the assertion that the projected B.C-action on M = K/R is quotient conjugation on the literal quotient K/R.

    The Route48, Route63, quotient-action, decoration-kernel, and framed-quotient surfaces are then reassembled theorem-level from the same selected projection.
