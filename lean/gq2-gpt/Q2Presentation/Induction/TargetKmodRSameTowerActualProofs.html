    Route-ready replacements for actual same-tower target K/R packets #

    This sidecar targets the two current route8 target same-tower residuals:

    • TargetKmodRSameTowerActualClosers.centralCover_targetCanonicalLowerQuotientEquivData;
    • TargetKmodRSameTowerActualClosers.centralCover_targetKmodRAmbientActionData.

    The current API does not expose enough data in CentralCoverTargetSubgroupConcreteData to prove these packets outright for an arbitrary B,D: the packet gives R <= K and K/R ~= M, but it does not identify the abstract lower action group B.C with the literal quotient Y/K, nor does it state that B.actM is the conjugation action.

    The replacements below therefore refine the two actual residuals through the already available projection-semantic split:

    • lower quotient map Y -> Y/K plus lower-action equivalence to B.C;
    • representative-level action plus class-independence of conjugation on K/R.

    These are strictly smaller manuscript-shaped obligations than the two actual packets: the quotient construction and the action law are separated, and the all-ambient action law is rebuilt from representative compatibility plus same-class independence.
