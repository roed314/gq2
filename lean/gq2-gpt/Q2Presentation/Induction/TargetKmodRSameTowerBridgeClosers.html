    Bridge closers for the same-tower target K/R packets #

    This sidecar replaces the two current actual same-tower target residuals by strictly lower manuscript-shaped data:

    • a concrete lower projection Y -> B.C with kernel K;
    • one representative of each lower class whose action realizes conjugation on the literal quotient K/R.

    The all-ambient-representatives action law is then theorem-level: changing a representative inside the same Y/K coset is inner conjugation by an element of K, and that has already been closed from the realized bridge K/R ~= M.
