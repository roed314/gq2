    Same-tower lower K/R closers #

    This sidecar targets the four current Section 8 lower target K/R packets:

    • centralCover_targetLowerQuotientMapData;
    • centralCover_targetLowerActionEquivData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRRepresentativeActionData;
    • centralCover_targetKmodRConjugationClassIndependenceData.

    The direct quotient-map construction is theorem-level once the decoration kills K, because the canonical lower child is literally Y/K. The action side is reduced to same-tower provenance: the abstract lower group B.C is the canonical quotient Y/K, B.actM is the transported conjugation action through that quotient, and conjugation on K/R only depends on the lower quotient coset.
