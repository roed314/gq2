    Same-tower semantic packets for the target K/R action #

    This sidecar narrows the route2 same-tower target packets to the concrete manuscript semantics:

    • the lower action group is reached by one canonical projection Y -> B.C with kernel K;
    • representatives for B.C realize the abstract action on M by conjugation on the literal quotient K/R;
    • inner conjugation by elements of K is trivial on K/R.

    The last item is the exact group-theoretic core behind "same Y/K coset representatives act identically on K/R".
