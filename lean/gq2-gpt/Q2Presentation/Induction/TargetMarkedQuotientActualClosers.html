    Actual target marked-quotient closers #

    This sidecar keeps the §8 target quotient route tied to the manuscript tower

    Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K.

    The older split packets in TargetMarkedQuotientClosers separated map existence, kernel identification, and quotient-map agreement. Here the marked restriction residuals are replaced by actual provenance packets: the selected marked maps are required to be the restrictions of the already constructed ambient quotient maps Q.topToMiddle and Q.middleToLower.

    The quotient-child split packets are theorem-level projections of the existing canonical quotient data. The middle-kernel image packet is constructed from the quotient-map tower itself.
