    Lower target marked-quotient proof packets #

    This sidecar is not imported by the routed theorem. It targets the six current Section 8 target-side residuals in TargetMarkedQuotientClosers.lean:

    • centralCover_targetCanonicalMiddleQuotientData
    • centralCover_targetCanonicalLowerQuotientData
    • centralCover_targetTopMarkedRestrictionData
    • centralCover_targetTopMarkedKernelOfRestrictionData
    • centralCover_targetMiddleMarkedRestrictionData
    • centralCover_targetMiddleMarkedKernelOfRestrictionData

    Manuscript anchors:

    • Section 7, eq:targettower: Y -> B = Y/R -> C = Y/K with R <= K <= L_Y;
    • Section 8, eq:Mstage/eq:Mdescent: the marked-kernel restrictions L_Y -> L_B -> L_C.

    The existing framed-target API does not yet construct quotient framed targets from a normal subgroup killed by the decoration. The lower packets below are therefore exactly the missing construction facts: the quotient children are framed quotient targets, and the ambient quotient maps restrict to the marked kernels. Once those are supplied, the top kernel is theorem-level from ker(Y -> B) = R, and the middle kernel is theorem-level from the single fact that ker(B -> C) lies in the middle marked kernel.

    Framed quotient targets for the manuscript quotients Y/R and Y/K.

    The final two fields record the genuinely framed part of the construction: the marked kernels of the quotient targets are the images of the original marked kernel under the quotient maps.
