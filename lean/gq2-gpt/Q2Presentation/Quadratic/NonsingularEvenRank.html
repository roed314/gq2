    A nonsingular 𝔽₂ quadratic space has even dimension (F-E1). Dickson.gaussSum_sq gives D² = |V| = 2^d; then |D| is a power of two (Nat.dvd_prime_pow) whose square has exponent d, so d = k·2 (Nat.pow_right_injective). Mirrors the banked dickson_arith arithmetic verbatim.
