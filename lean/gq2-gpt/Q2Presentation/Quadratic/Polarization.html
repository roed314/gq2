    Polarizations of F₂ quadratic forms (checkpoint-F10 support) #

    Every quadratic form q : QuadF2 V on a finite-dimensional F₂-space admits a (non-equivariant) polarization: a bilinear B with diagonal q and symmetrization the polar form. Construction: fix finBasis, set B(e_i,e_j) := polar(e_i,e_j) for i < j, q(e_i) for i = j, 0 for i > j. This eliminates the equivariant-refinement component of the leaf-#1 crux (PROGRESS.md, F10 finding): downstream consumes only the diagonal and the symmetrization of phi.
