    Proposition 3.10 — the maximal pro-2 quotient of Γ_A is Π #

    Paper (20)/(21): Π = ⟨σ, x₀, x₁ ∣ x₀^{σ²} x₀ [x₁,σ] = 1⟩_{pro-2} with ν₂(σ, x₀, x₁) = (1, 0, 0).
