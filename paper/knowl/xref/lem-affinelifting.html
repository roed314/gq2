Lemma 8.7. Affine \(\notn{Tsub}{T}\)-lifting equation.

Choose the splitting \(\notnfar{Bmid}{B}/\notn{Tsub}{T}\cong \notnfar{Vhead}{V}\rtimes \notnfar{Cfin}{C}\) above and let
\begin{equation*} e\in H^2(\notnfar{Cfin}{C},\notn{Tsub}{T}) \end{equation*}
be the class obtained by pulling \(\notnfar{Bmid}{B}\to \notnfar{Vhead}{V}\rtimes \notnfar{Cfin}{C}\) back along the zero section \(\notnfar{Cfin}{C}\to \notnfar{Vhead}{V}\rtimes \notnfar{Cfin}{C}\text{.}\) For a lower exact-image map \(\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}:\notnfar{GammaSrc}{\Gamma}\twoheadrightarrow \notnfar{Cfin}{C}\text{,}\) a class
\begin{equation*} c\in \notn{HGr}{H^1_{\Gamma,\rho}}(\notnfar{Vhead}{V}) \end{equation*}
is the \(\notnfar{Vhead}{V}\)-coordinate of an actual \(\notnfar{Mmid}{M}\)-lift if and only if
\begin{equation} \partial_{\Gamma,\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}}(c)=\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*e \quad\text{in }\notn{HGr}{H^2_{\Gamma,\rho}}(\notn{Tsub}{T}).\tag{8.8} \end{equation}
For every such class, the number of raw lifts above it is
\begin{equation} \mu=|B^1_{\Gamma,\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}}(\notnfar{Vhead}{V})|\,|\notn{Z1Gr}{Z^1_{\Gamma,\rho}}(\notn{Tsub}{T})|,\tag{8.9} \end{equation}
which is independent of \(\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}\) and has the same value on the two sources.

Proof.
Represent the \(\notn{Tsub}{T}\)-extension of \(\notnfar{Vhead}{V}\rtimes \notnfar{Cfin}{C}\) by a normalized cocycle. Its pure \(\notnfar{Vhead}{V}\times \notnfar{Vhead}{V}\) component is zero because \(\notnfar{Mmid}{M}\) is elementary abelian; its mixed component is the module extension \(0\to \notn{Tsub}{T}\to \notnfar{Mmid}{M}\to \notnfar{Vhead}{V}\to0\text{;}\) and its restriction to the zero section is \(e\text{.}\) Pulling this cocycle back along the graph of a \(\notnfar{Vhead}{V}\)-cocycle gives exactly \(\partial c+\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*e\text{,}\) proving (8.8) in characteristic two.
🔗
Each cohomology class has \(|B^1(\notnfar{Vhead}{V})|\) cocycle representatives, and each solvable \(\notn{Tsub}{T}\)-defect equation has \(|Z^1(\notn{Tsub}{T})|\) solutions. For the nontrivial simple \(\notnfar{Vhead}{V}\text{,}\) \(|B^1(\notnfar{Vhead}{V})|=2^{\dim \notnfar{Vhead}{V}}\text{;}\) Proposition 5.15, Proposition 5.16 gives the common value of \(|Z^1(\notn{Tsub}{T})|\text{.}\)
