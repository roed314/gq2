Lemma 8.9. Elementary lifting and exact-image subtraction.

For every \(\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}\in \notnfar{XGamma}{X_\Gamma}(\notnfar{Cfin}{C})\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} \notn{HGr}{H^2_{\Gamma,\rho}}(\notnfar{Mmid}{M})=0, \qquad |\notn{Z1Gr}{Z^1_{\Gamma,\rho}}(\notnfar{Mmid}{M})|=2^{2\dim \notnfar{Mmid}{M}}. \end{equation*}
Consequently
\begin{equation} \notnfar{eGamma}{e_\Gamma}(\notnfar{Bmid}{B})=2^{2\dim \notnfar{Mmid}{M}}\notnfar{eGamma}{e_\Gamma}(\notnfar{Cfin}{C}) -\sum_{\substack{J<\notnfar{Bmid}{B}\\J\twoheadrightarrow \notnfar{Cfin}{C}}}\notnfar{eGamma}{e_\Gamma}(J),\tag{8.13} \end{equation}
where each \(J\) has the boundary-framed structure induced from \(\notnfar{Bmid}{B}\text{.}\) For every proper \(J<\notnfar{Bmid}{B}\) occurring in this sum,
\begin{equation} |J\cap L_B|=|J\cap \notnfar{Mmid}{M}|\,|L_C| \le \frac{|\notnfar{Mmid}{M}|}{2}|L_C| =\frac{|L_B|}{2}<|\notnfar{LY}{L_Y}|.\tag{8.14} \end{equation}

Proof.
The unique simple head of \(\notnfar{Mmid}{M}\) is nontrivial, so \((\notnfar{Mmid}{M}^\vee)^\notnfar{Cfin}{C}=0\) by Lemma 7.1. Candidate deformation duality and local Tate duality therefore give the displayed vanishing and lift multiplicity. Every relation-compatible \(\notnfar{Mmid}{M}\)-lift has an exact image \(J\le \notnfar{Bmid}{B}\) mapping onto \(\notnfar{Cfin}{C}\text{;}\) partitioning by that exact image gives (8.13). If \(J<\notnfar{Bmid}{B}\text{,}\) then \(J\cap \notnfar{Mmid}{M}\) is a proper subgroup of the elementary abelian group \(\notnfar{Mmid}{M}\text{,}\) and the exact sequence
\begin{equation*} 1\longrightarrow J\cap \notnfar{Mmid}{M}\longrightarrow J\cap L_B \longrightarrow L_C\longrightarrow1 \end{equation*}
gives (8.14).
