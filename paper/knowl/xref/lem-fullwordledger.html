Lemma 5.4. Full finite-word ledger.

At a tame lower map, write first-order variations of \((\notn{sigma}{\sigma},\notn{tau}{\tau},\notn{x0}{x_0},\notnfar{x1}{x_1})\) as \((a,b,c,d)\text{.}\) Let \(\notn{opS}{\mathsf S},\notn{opT}{\mathsf T}\) be the lower actions of \(\notn{sigma}{\sigma},\notn{tau}{\tau}\text{,}\) put \(\notn{opU}{\mathsf U}=\notn{opS}{\mathsf S}^\notn{w}{\w}\text{,}\) and let \(\notn{Pnorm}{P}=1+\notn{opT}{\mathsf T}+\cdots+\notn{opT}{\mathsf T}^{e-1}\) for the odd order \(e\) of \(\notn{opT}{\mathsf T}\text{.}\) The raw finite Fox rules give
\begin{equation*} \notn{Dfox}{D}(u_i)=\notn{Pnorm}{P}(Dx_i+\notn{Dfox}{D}\notn{tau}{\tau}), \qquad \notn{Dfox}{D}(d_0)=\notn{Pnorm}{P} b+(\notn{Pnorm}{P}+1)c, \qquad \notn{Dfox}{D}(z_0)=\notn{opU}{\mathsf U}^{-1}c. \end{equation*}
The first-order contributions of the wild factors are
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c|cccc} \text{factor}&a&b&c&d\\ \hline h_0&0&0&0&0\\ u_1^{-1}&0&\notn{Pnorm}{P}&0&\notn{Pnorm}{P}\\ \notnfar{x1}{x_1}^\notn{sigma}{\sigma}&0&0&0&\notn{opS}{\mathsf S}^{-1}\\ {[d_0,z_0]}&0&0&0&0. \end{array} \end{equation*}
Thus the wild row is \(L_{\mathrm w}=Pb+(\notn{Pnorm}{P}+\notn{opS}{\mathsf S}^{-1})d\text{.}\)

Write \(b_{\mathrm{cl}}\) for the bilinear commutator pairing of the chosen class-two coefficient group. For the mixed Heisenberg or extraspecial calculation on the normalized cohomology representative supported on \(\notn{x0}{x_0}\text{,}\) the complete central ledger is
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c|c|c} \text{factor}&\text{$\notn{Vhead}{V}$-coordinate}&\text{central coordinate}\\ \hline h_0&0&\lambda(c)\text{ in }\mathcal H(\notn{Vhead}{V}),\; q(c)\text{ for }\notn{kappaq0}{\kappa_q^0}\\ u_1^{-1}&0&0\\ \notnfar{x1}{x_1}^\notn{sigma}{\sigma}&0&0\\ d_0=(\notn{x0}{x_0}\notn{tau}{\tau})^\notn{w}{\w} \notn{x0}{x_0}^{-1}&(\notn{Pnorm}{P}+1)c&\ast\\ z_0=\notn{x0}{x_0}^{\sigma_2}&\notn{opU}{\mathsf U}^{-1}c&\ast\\ {[d_0,z_0]}&0&b_{\mathrm{cl}}((\notn{Pnorm}{P}+1)c,\notn{opU}{\mathsf U}^{-1}c). \end{array} \end{equation*}
The symbols \(\ast\) are irrelevant because \(d_0\) and \(z_0\) enter the relator only through their commutator. The safety of replacing \(h_0\) by \(\notn{x0}{x_0}^2\) in these ledgers is exactly Lemma 5.3: the variation of \(g_0=\sigma_2^2\) starts in order at least two; when \(\notn{Pnorm}{P}=0\) one has \(Dd_0=\notn{Dfox}{D}\notn{x0}{x_0}\text{,}\) so Lemma 5.2(i) applies; when \(\notn{Pnorm}{P}=1\text{,}\) the lift of \(\notn{opU}{\mathsf U}\) to the class-two coefficient group is a central automorphism of order at most two, so \(g_0\) acts trivially and Lemma 5.2(ii) applies.
