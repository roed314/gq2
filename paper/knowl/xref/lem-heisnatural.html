Lemma 5.6. Strict coefficient naturality.

For every \(\notn{Cfin}{C}\)-module map \(f:\notn{Amod}{A}\to A'\text{,}\) the mixed derivatives satisfy
\begin{equation*} \beta^{A'}_{\rho,r}(f_*a,\lambda') =\beta^A_{\rho,r}(a,f^\vee\lambda'). \end{equation*}

🔗

Proof.
Evaluate the same finite relator in the mixed Heisenberg group
\begin{equation*} \notn{Amod}{A}\times A'^\vee\times\F_2, \qquad (a,\lambda,z)(a',\lambda',z') =(a+a',\lambda+\lambda',z+z'+\lambda(f(a'))). \end{equation*}
Its two natural images in \(\mathcal H(A')\) and \(\mathcal H(\notn{Amod}{A})\) have the same central coordinate. This gives the identity before passage to cohomology.
