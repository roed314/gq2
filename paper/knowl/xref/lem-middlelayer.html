Lemma 6.10. The middle unit layer is unramified.

Assume \(K/\notn{Qtwo}{\Qtwo}\) is tame and put \(e=v_K(2)\text{.}\) Then \(e\) is odd, and tame inertia acts trivially on the middle graded piece
\begin{equation*} U_e/U_{e+1}. \end{equation*}
Consequently a ramified simple tame module does not occur in that graded piece.
🔗
🔗

Proof.
The ramification index of a tame extension of \(\notn{Qtwo}{\Qtwo}\) is odd and equals the order of tame inertia. On the odd-depth piece \(\gr_jM_K\text{,}\) an inertia element represented by \(\zeta\) acts through the character \(\zeta\mapsto\zeta^j\text{.}\) At \(j=e\) this character is trivial. A ramified simple module has nontrivial inertia, so its isotypic multiplicity at the middle depth is zero.
