Lemma 8.15. Phase-cover transform.

For \(\zeta\in H^2(C,\F_2)\text{,}\) let \(p_\zeta:C_\zeta\twoheadrightarrow C\) be the corresponding central double cover and let \(n_{\Gamma,0}(\zeta)\) count the lower exact-image maps for which it is unobstructed. Then
\begin{equation} s_\Gamma(\zeta)=2n_{\Gamma,0}(\zeta)-\notnfar{eGamma}{e_\Gamma}(C)\tag{8.21} \end{equation}
and
\begin{equation} \boxed{ 8n_{\Gamma,0}(\zeta)= \sum_{\substack{\widetilde J\le C_\zeta\\ p_\zeta(\widetilde J)=C}} \notnfar{eGamma}{e_\Gamma}(\widetilde J).}\tag{8.22} \end{equation}
🔗
🔗

Proof.
The first identity is the decomposition of the signed sum according to the two values of the scalar obstruction. The second is the exact eight-lift partition of Lemma 8.3, applied to \(p_\zeta\text{.}\)
