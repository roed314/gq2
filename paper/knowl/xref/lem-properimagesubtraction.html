Lemma 8.12. Proper-image subtraction for a scalar pushout.

Fix \(0\ne\lambda\in\notnfar{calX}{\mathcal X_R}\text{.}\) Let \(\notn{ZGl}{Z_{\Gamma,\lambda}}(\notnfar{Bmid}{B}/C)\) count all compatible lifts of exact-image maps to \(C\text{,}\) without imposing generation in \(\notnfar{Bmid}{B}\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation} \boxed{ m_{\Gamma,\lambda}(\notnfar{Bmid}{B})=\notn{ZGl}{Z_{\Gamma,\lambda}}(\notnfar{Bmid}{B}/C) -\sum_{\substack{J<\notnfar{Bmid}{B}\\J\twoheadrightarrow C}} m_{\Gamma,\lambda}(J).}\tag{8.16} \end{equation}
For every proper \(J\) in the sum,
\begin{equation} \boxed{ 8m_{\Gamma,\lambda}(J)= \sum_{\substack{\widetilde J\le p_\lambda^{-1}(J)\\ p_\lambda(\widetilde J)=J}} \notnfar{eGamma}{e_\Gamma}(\widetilde J).}\tag{8.17} \end{equation}

Proof.
Partition the relation-compatible maps to \(\notnfar{Bmid}{B}\) by their exact image. This gives (8.16). For a fixed proper image \(J\text{,}\) an unobstructed map has exactly eight scalar lifts; partitioning those lifts by their exact images in \(p_\lambda^{-1}(J)\) is Lemma 8.3 and gives (8.17).
