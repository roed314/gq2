Lemma 5.12. Simple characteristic-two modules are tame.

Let \(V\) be a simple \(\F_2[C]\)-module occurring in the induction. Then the marked normal \(2\)-subgroup of \(C\) acts trivially on \(V\text{.}\) Hence \(V\) factors through the finite tame quotient of \(C\text{.}\)

Proof.
A finite \(2\)-group acting on a nonzero vector space over \(\F_2\) has a nonzero fixed vector. Since the \(2\)-subgroup is normal in \(C\text{,}\) the fixed subspace is a nonzero \(C\)-submodule. Simplicity forces this fixed subspace to be all of \(V\text{.}\)
