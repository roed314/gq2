Lemma 5.12. Simple characteristic-two modules are tame.

Let \(\notnfar{Vhead}{V}\) be a simple \(\F_2[\notn{Cfin}{C}]\)-module occurring in the induction. Then the marked normal \(2\)-subgroup of \(\notn{Cfin}{C}\) acts trivially on \(\notnfar{Vhead}{V}\text{.}\) Hence \(\notnfar{Vhead}{V}\) factors through the finite tame quotient of \(\notn{Cfin}{C}\text{.}\)

Proof.
A finite \(2\)-group acting on a nonzero vector space over \(\F_2\) has a nonzero fixed vector. Since the \(2\)-subgroup is normal in \(\notn{Cfin}{C}\text{,}\) the fixed subspace is a nonzero \(\notn{Cfin}{C}\)-submodule. Simplicity forces this fixed subspace to be all of \(\notnfar{Vhead}{V}\text{.}\)
