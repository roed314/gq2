Proposition 1.1. Marked dyadic Demushkin normalization.

Let \(\notn{Dloc}{D_{\mathrm{loc}}}=G_{\notn{Qtwo}{\Qtwo}}(2)\) and let \(\notn{nuur}{\nu_{\mathrm{ur}}}:\notn{Dloc}{D_{\mathrm{loc}}}\twoheadrightarrow\mathbb{Z}_2\) be normalized by sending geometric Frobenius to \(1\text{.}\) There exist topological generators \(a,s,y\) such that
\begin{equation} \notn{Dloc}{D_{\mathrm{loc}}}\cong \left\langle a,s,y\;\middle|\;a^2s^4[s,y]=1\right\rangle_{\mathrm{pro}-2}, \qquad \notn{nuur}{\nu_{\mathrm{ur}}}(a)=-2,\quad \notn{nuur}{\nu_{\mathrm{ur}}}(s)=1,\quad \notn{nuur}{\nu_{\mathrm{ur}}}(y)=0.\tag{1.4} \end{equation}
The proof is given in Sections 3.1–3.3.
