Proposition 8.13. The nonzero radical-edge case.

If the radical edge of \(p_\lambda\) is nonzero, then
\begin{equation} \boxed{ \notn{ZGl}{Z_{\Gamma,\lambda}}(\notnfar{Bmid}{B}/C) =2^{2\dim \notnfar{Mmid}{M}-1}\notnfar{eGamma}{e_\Gamma}(C).}\tag{8.18} \end{equation}
🔗
🔗

Proof.
By Lemma 8.6, the scalar obstruction varies by a nonzero affine functional on each free \(\notn{Z1Gr}{Z^1_{\Gamma,\rho}}(\notn{Tsub}{T})\)-orbit of unrestricted \(\notnfar{Mmid}{M}\)-lifts. Exactly half of the \(2^{2\dim \notnfar{Mmid}{M}}\) lifts over each \(\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}\in \notnfar{XGamma}{X_\Gamma}(\notnfar{Cfin}{C})\) satisfy the central relation, which is (8.18).
