Proposition 8.8. Completed-square phase identity.

For either source, every lower exact-image map \(\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}\text{,}\) every \(b\in \notn{HGr}{H^1_{\Gamma,\rho}}(\notnfar{Vhead}{V})\text{,}\) and every \(\chi\in \notnfar{calX}{\mathcal X_T}\text{,}\)
\begin{equation} Q_{\kappa,\notnfar{GammaSrc}{\Gamma},\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}}(b) +\langle\partial_{\Gamma,\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}} b+\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*e,\chi\rangle =Q^0_{\Gamma,\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}}(b+\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*a_{\chi,\kappa}) +\notn{iotaGamma}{\iota_\Gamma}(\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*\notn{DeltaChiKappa}{\Delta_{\chi,\kappa}}).\tag{8.12} \end{equation}
Here \(Q^0\) is the base determinant form and \(Q_\kappa\) is the obstruction for the actual scalar pushout class (8.7).

Proof.
By Corollary 5.17,
\begin{equation*} \langle\partial_{\Gamma,\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}}b,\chi\rangle =\langle b,\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*\gamma_\chi\rangle_\Gamma. \end{equation*}
Using (6.7), the left side of (8.12) is
\begin{equation*} Q^0_{\Gamma,\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}}(b) +\langle b,\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*(\gamma_\kappa+\gamma_\chi)\rangle_\Gamma +\notn{iotaGamma}{\iota_\Gamma}(\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*\delta_\kappa) +\notn{iotaGamma}{\iota_\Gamma}(\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*(\chi_*e)). \end{equation*}
By the definition of \(a_{\chi,\kappa}\text{,}\) the middle linear term is the polar pairing \(\notnfar{bq}{b_q}(b,\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*a_{\chi,\kappa})\text{.}\) Completing the square for the base quadratic form gives
\begin{equation*} Q^0(b)+\notnfar{bq}{b_q}(b,\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*a_{\chi,\kappa}) =Q^0(b+\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*a_{\chi,\kappa}) +\notn{iotaGamma}{\iota_\Gamma}\bigl(\notnfar{rhoEpi}{\rho}^*\Theta_q^0(a_{\chi,\kappa})\bigr). \end{equation*}
Adding the two inflated scalar terms proves (8.12). The class \(\notn{DeltaChiKappa}{\Delta_{\chi,\kappa}}\) is defined on the finite target \(\notnfar{Cfin}{C}\text{;}\) no comparison map between the two source \(H^1\)-spaces is involved.
